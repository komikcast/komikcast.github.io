PERPANGKATAN ADALAH SUATU BENTUK OPERASI MATEMATIKA YG PENGERJAAN NYA BERULANG-ULANG ATAU DISEBUT JUGA PERKALIAN GANDA

SOAL :

1) 9³ + 11³

= [ 9.9.9 ] + [ 11.11.11 ]

= [ 81 . 9 ] + [ 121 . 11 ]

= 729 + 1.331

= 2.060

dengan menggunakan:

a³+b³ = (a+b)(a²-ab+b²)

___

9³ + 11³

=(9+11)(9²+(9)(11)+11²)

=20(81-99+121)

=20(-18+121)

=20(121-18)

=20(103)

=2.060

7. an object is released 5m above the ground.what is its velocity at the moment it strikes the ground? (take g=10 m/s)



h = 5 m
g = 10 m/s²
v = __?

velocity at the moment it strikes the ground
v = √(2 g h)
v = √(2 • 10 • 5)
v = 10 m/s ✔️h = 5 m
g = 10 m/s²
v?

Arrangement:
v = √2 . g . h

v = √2 . 10 . 5

v = √100

v = 10 m/s

14. Mommy cleans the room every day. Change into Passive Sentence. A The room was clean by mommy every day B The room is clean by mommy every day C The room is cleaned by mommy every day D Mommy is cleaned the room every day E The room was being cleaned by mommy every day


Jawaban:

C The room is cleaned by mommy every day

Penjelasan:

Dalam membentuk kalimat passive dengan tense simple present, rumusnya adalah"

subjek  + is/am/are + verb3  + by objek + keterangan dst

Jadi kalimat passive teks soal adalah:

subjek (The room)

+ is/am/are (kita pilih is) karena subjeknya the room (singular noun/kata benda tunggal)

+ verb 3 (cleaned => verb 3 dari clean)

+ by object (di sini objek nya adalah mommy, jadi by mommy)

+ keterangan ( di sini keterangannya: every day)
