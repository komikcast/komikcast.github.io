32. Todays I .... (26) so busy that I don't have much time for relaxing. I and my sisters .....(27) yet since two weeks ago. We .... (28) away for almost one year but recently she ....(29) me so We ...(30) informed well * has met has meet have not meet have not met has not met


Today I was (26) so busy that I don't have much time for relaxing. I and my sisters have not met (27) yet since two weeks ago. We were (28) away for almost one year but recently she visited (29) me so We were not (30) informed well.

Pembahasan

Teks di atas dilengkapi dengan simple past tense dan present perfect tense. Cara mengetahuinya adalah dari konteks kalimat:

Today I was (26) so busy that I don't have much time for relaxing. (Hari ini___saya sangat sibuk dan tidak punya waktu bersantai).

Kalimat ini membicarakan kejadian yang sudah terjadi maka kita pakai past tense dengan I was.

I and my sisters ______ (27) yet since two weeks ago. (Saya dan adik saya ___ sejak dua pekan yang lalu)

Kata yet menyiratkan bahwa kalimat ini menggunakan present perfect tense have not met yang artinya belum bertemu setelah sekian lama.  

Recently she visited (29) me so We were not (30) informed well. (Dia baru-baru ini mengunjungi saya jadi kami tidak diberi tahu)

Kalimat ini menggunakan past tense dengan melihat tandanya yaitu recently (baru-baru ini).
