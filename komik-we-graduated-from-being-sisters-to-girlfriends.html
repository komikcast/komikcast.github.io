13. He will graduated from the university when you come back to indonesia .pke interrogrative


Future perfect tense dipakai untuk menyatakan peristiwa yang sudah terjadi dan akan selesai di waktu tertentu di masa depan.

Formulasi future perfect tense:

S + will + have + past particple (verb 3)

Contoh:

(+) I will have finished my homework at 10 p.m.

Arti: Saya akan sudah menyelesaikan PR saya jam 10 malam.

(-) I won't (will not) have finished my homework at 10 p.m.

Arti: Saya tidak akan sudah menyelesaikan PR saya jam 10 malam.

(?) Will I have finished my homework at 10 p.m?

Arti: Akankah saya sudah menyelesaikan PR saya jam 10 malam?


Keterangan waktu yang biasa dipakai untuk tense ini : by tomorrow, at 7 o'clock, by next month, by next year, by the the end of this month, by the end of this year, dll.


Pembahasan

(+) He will be graduated from the university when you come back to Indonesia.

(-) He won't be graduated from the university when you come back to Indonesia.

(?) Will he be graduated from the university when you come back to Indonesia.

40. My brother was graduated from college in 2015


Jawaban:

Passive: My brother was graduated from college in 2015.

Active: College graduated my brother in 2015

Penjelasan:

Karena soal di atas tidak ada perintahnya, saya berasumsi "change into active voice" karena kalimat pertama berpola passive voice.

Kalimat di atas menggunakan simple past tense.

My brother (object) was graduated (verb 3) from college (subject) in 2015 (adverb of time).

Active: College (subject) graduated (verb 2) my brother (object) in 2015. (adverb of time).
