3. if she....(to be) my girl friend, i would ask her to have a romantic dinner


Answer :

If she were my girlfriend, I would ask her to have a romantic dinner.

Kalimat di atas menggunakan conditional clause type 2.

----------

Explanation :

Conditional sentence adalah kalimat pengandaian. Ada tiga tipe kalimat pengandaian :

Type 1 → untuk kejadian yang masih mungkin terjadi di masa yang akan datang (future possible).

Construction : IF + S + infinitive/infinitive+s, S + will + infinitive


Type 2 → pengandaian untuk peristiwa yang terjadi di masa sekarang (present unreal), tidak sesuai kenyataan sekarang.

Construction : IF + S + preterite (v₂), S + would + infinitive (v₁)

Kata kerja "be" dalam if clausenya selalu were jika berupa pengandaian.


Type 3 → pengandaian untuk peristiwa yang terjadi di masa lampau (past unreal).

Construction : IF + S + had + past participle (v₃), S + would + have + past participle (v₃)

----------

Answer details

Subject : English

Category : Structure and written expressions

Sub-category : Conditional sentence

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 8.5

Keywords : if, conditional, type 2

9. A han1. The girl said to me, I have lost my dollThe girl told me that her doll​


Jawaban:

...has been lost.

Penjelasan:

The girl told me that her doll has been lost.

It's the passive form of a sentence with present perfect grammar.

Present perfect, passive =

I/we/you/they/(two objects or people) + HAVE + (v3) / been + (adjective)_______She/he/it/(an object or someone) + HAS + (v3) / been + (adjective)

example:

She + HAS + done her homework

Her doll + HAS + been lost.
