13. the characteristic of plant that lives in water is​


Jawaban:

1. Submerged plants are usually without a cuticle layer in order to avoid excessive dryness.

2. Submerged plants lack xylem since their leaves can do all of the work.

3. The leaves of submerged plants rarely have stomata.

4. Emersed plants have leaves that stick out of the water with access to the air and sun, though their roots are always located at the bottom of a body of water.

5. Free-floating plants have leaves that float on the surface of the water as opposed to sticking out of it.

16. bahD8A/formResponse 5. He recited the Holy Qur'an (move to Present Continuous Tense) * a. He will recite the Holy Qur'an b. He had recited the Holy Qur'an c. He is reciting the Holy Qur'an Od. He would has recited the Holy Qur'an


Jawaban:

c. He is reciting the Holy Qur'an

Penjelasan:

Karna rumus Present Continous tense adalah Subjek + be (am/is/are) + verb (-ing) + Object.

Maka dari itu, Opsi yang tepat dari soal tersebut adalah C .

Semoga Membantu!

≡ Present Continuous ≡

(c) He is reciting the Holy Qur'an

Pembahasan

Present continuous tense adalah jenis kalimat yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di masa sekarang yang sedang berlangsung.

formula:

(+) subject + to be + verb-ing + O/C

(-) subject + to be + not + verb-ing + O/C

(?) to be + Subject + verb-ing + O/C + ?

to be: is → untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggalam → untuk subjek Iare → untuk subjek You/We/They

based on the question:

Convert this sentence into present continuous!

Ubah kalimat ini ke bentuk present continuous!

He recited the Holy Qur'an.

Untuk mengubah kalimat dari bentuk past tense ke bentuk present continuous, ubah verba yang ada (dari bentuk verb 2) menjadi bentuk gerund (verb yang berakhiran -ing). Kemudian, beri to be yang sesuai sebelum kata kerjanya.

He recited the Holy Qur'an.

Subjek 'he' menggunakan to be is. Bentuk gerund dari 'recite' adalah reciting.

➥ He is reciting the Holy Qur'an

Jawaban yang tepat adalah opsi C.

38. The process of plants that give of the water/let out the extra water is called​


Jawaban:

The process of plants that give of the water/let out the extra water is calles transpiration

Transpiration is the evaporation of water from the surface of leaf cells in actively growing plants. The process of transpiration provides the plant with evaporative cooling, nutrients, carbon dioxide entry and water to provide plant structure.
