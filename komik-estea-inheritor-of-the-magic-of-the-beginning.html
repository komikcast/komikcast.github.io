26. Andrew deposited some money in the bank at the beginning of the year. At the end of one year, he earned an interest and had $3045 in the bank. This was 101.5% of the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year. a) How much did Andrew deposit in the bank at the beginning of the year? b) What was the amount of interest Andrew earned?​


Jawab:

a) Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

b) The amount of interest he earned was $45. The interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The amount of Andrew's money in the bank at the end of one year is 101.5% of the amount of money he deposited at the beginning of the year:

$3045 = 101.5% × m

(m = the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year)

$3045 = 101.5% × m

m = $3045 ÷ 101.5%

   = $3045 ÷ (101.5/100)

   = $3045 × 100/101.5

   = $3000

∴ Therefore, Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

The amount of interest he earned:

$3045 – $3000 = $45

Because this is the amount of interest in one year, we can say that the interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.


27. Who was the owner of the magic candle?


Who was the owner of the magic candle?

The owner of the magic candle in the story entitled “The Magic Candle” is an old woman.

Terjemahan: Pemilik dari lilin ajaib dalam cerita yang berjudul “Lilin Ajaib” adalah seorang wanita tua.

Penjelasan:

Jawaban untuk pertanyaan ini dapat kamu temukan dalam cerita “The Magic Candle” berikut ceritanya.

One day, a young wanderer got lost in the wood. Suddenly, he saw a light from an old hut. He knocked on the door, and old woman opened it. She was crying.

She said that a devil had stolen her magic candle. The candle could grant anything she asked. The wanderer asked her where the devil lived. “in a castle not far from here,” said the old woman.

The wanderer went to the castle. There he found the devil, but he was old and weak. Therefore, when the wanderer grabbed the magic candle from the devil’s table and ran away, he couldn’t chase him.

However, the wanderer was not a kind man. He didn’t return the candle to the old woman, but kept it for himself. He lit the candle and made a wish, “I want to go far away from here.”

Suddenly, a genie appeared and took him to a beautiful palace. The wanderer wanted to make some money. So, he lit the candle again and wished for some jewelry.

He sold them to the guests and was soon making a lot of money. Then, a princess came to buy some jewelry, but there was nothing left.

The wanderer fell in love with her and asked her to marry him. The kind princess said yes, and they got married the next day.

In his happiness, the wanderer told the princess about his adventure and the magic candle. Hearing that, the princess got very angry. At night, she lit the candle and wished the wanderer disappeared.

In the morning the wanderer awoke and found himself back in his ugly house in the village.

Dalam paragraf kedua cerita tersebut yang berisi “She said that a devil had stolen her magic candle” dikatakan bahwa si wanita tua kehilangan lilin ajaibnya karena dicuri oleh seorang iblis. Dari sini, dapat disimpulkan bahwa si wanita tua merupakan pemiliki dari lilin ajaib tersebut.
