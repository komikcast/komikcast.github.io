4. Mr william is 4 times older than his daughter, Mow. Six years ago, mr william was seven times older than his daughter. How old is mr william now?


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

﻿Mr. William = W

﻿Mow = M

﻿Mr. William is 4 times older than his daughter, Mow

﻿W = 4M

﻿Six years ago, mr william was seven times older than his daughter

﻿W - 6 = 7 × (M - 6)

﻿W = (7 × (M - 6)) + 6

﻿4M = (7 × (M - 6)) + 6

﻿4M - 6 = 7M - 42

﻿42 - 6 = 7M - 4M

﻿36 = 3M

﻿M = 12 years old

﻿W = 4M = 4 × 12 = 48 years old
