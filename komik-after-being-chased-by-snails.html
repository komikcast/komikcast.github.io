His room are tidies up by Hans everydayD. His room is being tidied up. by Gans everydayE. His room tidies ul by Hans everyday26.kelly does not speak English after school A. English is not spoken by kelly after schoolB. English is not being spoken by kelly after schoolC. English is not spoke by kelly after schoolD. English is not speak by kelly after schoolE. All answers are wrong(help me(​


ACTIVE-PASSIVE

25) A.His room is tidied up by Hans everyday

26) A. English is not spoken by kelly after school

EXPLANATION

It's called an active voice when the doer of action performs the action. Meanwhile, the passive voice is used when we want to emphasize the action and recipient of the verb rather than the doer of action.

The steps how to change the active voice to the passive voice are :

~ pay attention to the verb form whether it's transitive or not.

~ the object of active voice is moved to the subject of passive voice. Meanwhile, the subject of active voice is changed into the object of passive voice accompanied with the "by" preposition or sometimes it's omitted.

~ all of the verbs are changed into the past participle verb (V³).

BASED ON YOUR QUESTION

Two questions above refers to the simple present tense usage. It can be seen on the adverb of frequency usage "everyday" and the verb form.

Here's the pattern of simple present tense to guide you how to change the active voice to the passive voice.

A : S+Verb¹(s/es)+O

P : S(O)+to be(is/am/are)+Verb³+by Agent

1) Hans tidies up his room everyday.

Subject(he)+phrasal verb(tidies up)+Object(his room)+adverb(everyday)

changes to :

New subject(his room)+verb(is tidied up)+by+Agent(Hans)+adverb(everyday)

Thus, the answer is the "A" option.

2) Kelly does not speak English after school

Subject (Kelly)+verb(doesn't speak)+Object(English)+PP(after school)

changes to :

New subject(English)+verb(isn't spoken)+by+Agent(Kelly)

Thus, the answer is the "A" option"

33. Sherly and Budi will eat that rujak after school The passive voice for the sentence above is ____ a. That rujak will be being eaten by Sharely and Budi after school b. That rujak will be eat by Sharely and Budi after school c. That rujak will be eating by Sharely and Budi after school d. That rujak will be eaten by Sharely and Budi after school e. That rujak will being eaten by Sharely and Budi after school​


The correct answer is

d. That rujak will be eaten by Sherly and Budi after school.

Explanation

The original sentence is in the active voice, where Sherly and Budi are the subjects performing the action of eating the rujak. In the passive voice, the object (that rujak) becomes the subject of the sentence, and the focus is on the action being done to it, rather than who is doing it.

To form the passive voice, we use the auxiliary verb "to be" + past participle of the main verb (in this case, "eaten"). The subject performing the action (Sherly and Budi) is introduced using the preposition "by."

Therefore, the passive voice of the sentence is "That rujak will be eaten by Sherly and Budi after school."

The formula to form the passive voice is:

Passive voice = auxiliary verb "to be" (in the appropriate tense) + past participle of the main verb + preposition "by" + subject performing the action (optional)

Pembahasan

cara membentuk kalimat pasif (passive voice) dalam bahasa Inggris, yaitu dengan menggunakan auxiliary verb "to be" + past participle of the main verb + preposition "by" + subject performing the action (optional).
