7. correct some usages of verb tense mistakes in the following story. Why the sun and the moon live in the sky A long time ago, the Sun and the Moon are a married couple who lived on the Earth and were great friends of the Sea. One day, they invite the Sea to visit them. So the Sea go along, with the À sh and all the members of his family. Surprisingly, the water begin to rise, so that the Sun and the Moon have to climb up to the roof because they do not want to be drowned, then they climbed up into the sky, where they have remain ever since.


Subject : English

Category : Structure

Sub-category : Past Simple Tense

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 8.5

Keywords : ago, past simple

----------------

Tense yang digunakan dalam bacaan ini adalah past simple tense karena ada time signal a long time ago (pada jaman dahulu kala).

Past simple tense digunakan untuk mnyatakan kegiatan yang dimulai, terjadi dan selesai di masa lalu.

Konstruksi past simple tense adalah subject + preterite (v₂)

-----------

Answer :

A long time ago, the Sun and the Moon were a married couple who lived on the Earth and were great friends of the Sea. One day, they invited the sea to visit them.

So the sea went along, with the fish and all the members of his family. Surprisingly, the water began to rise, so that the Sun and the Moon had to climb up to the roof because they did not want to be drowned, then they climbed up into the sky, where they have remained ever since.

------------------

"where they have remained ever since" menggunakan present perfect tense karena menggunakan time signal since.

Present perfect tense digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang dimulai di masa lalu dan "sempurna" atau masih terjadi sekarang.

12. Coba jelaskan apa yang dimaksud dengan doujinshi?​


Kita ambil positifnya saja ya, Doujinshi (同人誌) artinya sama saja seperti Personal Publishing atau karya yang kita terbitkan secara pribadi (tanpa perantara perusahaan percetakan elite) dimana penerbitan ini bisa kita lakukan secara individu atau secara asosiasi (berkelompok)

Pembahasan

Doujinshi sendiri merupakan frasa ubahan Jepang artinya bukan kata dasar. Doujinshi ini tersusun atas dua kata dasar, yaitu Doujin [ 同人 ] yang bisa didefinisikan dengan "subjek yang sama" dan Shi [ 誌 ] yang bisa didefinisikan sebagai partikel penanda penerbitan musiman. Karya ini secara umum pertama kali di publikasikan oleh seorang animator bernama Meiroku Zasshi di abad 19 (1885).

Banyak animator yang mulai merintis karier seperti Meiroku Zasshi misalnya saja Masaki Kajishima (pengarang Tenchi Muyo! GXP), Maki Murakami (pengarang Gravitation), 07th Expansion (pengarang Umineko no Naku Koro ni), dan masih banyak lagi.

39. "Tiara and Zidan (wear) the couple shirt for their commitee" buatlah kalimat simple present passive +,-,?​


Jawaban:

Passive Voice:

(+) The couple shirt is worn by Tiara and Zidan for their committee.

(-) The couple shirt is not worn by Tiara and Zidan for their committee.

(?) Is the couple shirt worn by Tiara and Zidan for their committee?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Passive Voice dari Simple Present Tense dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Kalimat aktif subjeknya aktif melakukan aksi dalam kalimat

Kalimat pasif subjeknya pasif dikenai aksi dalam kalimat

Simple Present Tense:

Passive Voice: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-3 + Object

(-) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Verb-3 + Object

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + Subject + Verb-3 + Object + ?
