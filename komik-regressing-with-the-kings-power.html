1. If Rapunzel was born with the power of the sun drop the power was in her hair, why did the power go to a stone? (Rapunzel season 3)


Jawaban:

The power of the sun drop did not go to a stone. Instead, Mother Gothel, the main antagonist of the story, discovered that when Rapunzel's hair was cut, it lost its magical properties. The power of the sun drop resided in Rapunzel's hair and remained with her until it was cut. The stone you mentioned might be referring to the magical flower that contained the sun drop's power, which was used by Rapunzel's mother, Queen Arianna, during her pregnancy, leading to Rapunzel's birth with the sun drop's power.

22. QUIZZ !!!(B.Inggris)The Constitution gave the legislative branch of government ________ to pass laws.a. the powerb. the power isc. has the powerd. of the power#englishtestbyalfan2/2​


Jawaban:

A. the power

Penjelasan:

Dalam Konstitusi Amerika Serikat, legislatif memiliki wewenang untuk menetapkan undang-undang. Hal ini dinyatakan dengan kalimat "the legislative branch of government has the power to pass laws". A. the power

artinya

Konstitusi memberikan cabang legislatif kekuasaan pemerintah untuk mengesahkan undang-undang.

for non-accommodating eyes
M = Sn/f
f = Sn / M
f = 25/10
f = 2.5 cm

P = 100/f = 100/2,5 = 40 diopri

so, the power of the magnifying glass is 40 dioptri.
