1.

(-) My sister didn't visit me in the boarding school last month.

(?) Did my sister visit me in the boarding school last month?

2.

(-) The sun doesn't rise in the east.

(?) Does the sun rise in the east?

3.

(-) She is not a beautiful girl.

(?) Is she a beautiful girl?

4.

(-) Dewi was not dancing with me last night.

(?) Was Dewi dancing with me last night?

5.

(-) They don't write a good novel.

(?) Do they write a good novel?

---------------------------------------------

1. Rumus yg digunakan adalah kalimat verbal simple past tense.

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + didn't + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

2. Rumus yg digunakan adalah kalimat verbal simple present tense.

(+) S + V1/Vs + O

(-) S + don't/doesn't + V1 + O

(?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O?

3. Rumus yg digunakan adalah kalimat nominal simple present tense.

(+) S + is/am/are + C

(-) S + is/am/are + not + C

(?) Is/Am/Are + S + C

4. Rumus yg digunakan adalah kalimat verbal past continuous tense.

(+) S + is/am/are + V-ing + O

(-) S + is/am/are + not + V-ing + O

(?) Is/Am/Are + S + V-ing + O?

5 Rumus yg digunakan adalah kalimat verbal simple present tense.

29. Fill in the blank with suitable relative pronoun (who, whom, whose, which)1. The girl ...... you like has married yesterday.2. You has lost the book ....... I want to borrow3. The man ...... drives my car everyday is my uncle.4. The man ...... the car has been stolen is my teacher.5. I don't like the girl ...... sitting beside me.​


RELATIVE CLAUSE

1. The girl whom you like has married yesterday.

2. You has lost the book which I want to borrow

3. The man who drives my car everyday is my uncle.

4. The man whose car has been stolen is my teacher.

5. I don't like the girl who is sitting beside me.

EXPLANATION

1) WHO

Digunakan untuk menjabarkan orang dan berfungsi sebagai pengganti posisi subyek. 

Exp.

~ The man is my uncle. He drives my car everyday.

Jika digabung akan menjadi :

~ The man who drives my car everyday is my uncle.

~ Who sebagai relative pronoun menggantikan subyek "He."

2) WHICH

Digunakan untuk menjabarkan kata benda dan hewan (thing, animal). Berfungsi pengganti posisi subyek, obyek.

Exp.

~ You has lost the book. I want to borrow it.

Jika digabung akan menjadi :

~ You has lost the book which I want to borrow.

~ Which sebagai relative pronoun, menggantikan posisi obyek "it."

3) WHOM

Digunakan untuk memodifikasi orang, penjelas obyek kata kerjanya.

Exp.

The girl has married yesterday. You like her.

Jika digabung akn menjadi :

~ The girl whom you like has married yesterday.

~ Whom sebagai relative pronoun, menggantikan obyek "her."

4) WHOSE

Digunakan untuk memodifikasi kepemilikan obyek. Whose menggantikan possessive adjective determiner [her, his, their and etc]

Exp.

~ The man is my teacher. His car has been stolen.

Jika digabung akan menjadi :

~ The man whose car has been stolen is my teacher.

~ Whose sebagai relative pronoun, menggantikan possessive determiner "his."

example:

She + HAS + done her homework
