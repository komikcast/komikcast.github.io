10. jf i finish the assignment before saturday I,.....(submit) it to my teacher


Conditional sentences are sometimes confusing for learners of English as a second language.

Watch out:Which type of conditional sentences is it?Where is the if-clause (e.g. at the beginning or at the end of the conditional sentence)?

There are three types of conditional sentences.

type    condition

I          condition possible to fulfill

II         condition in theory possible to fulfill

III        condition not possible to fulfill (too late)


There are three types of form as well

type     if-clause main clause

I           Simple Present will-future or (Modal + infinitive)

II          Simple Past would + infinitive *

III         Past Perfect would + have + past participle *


Based on the question and explanation above, the question is using Simple Present Tense, hence the answer would be


If I finish the assignment before saturday I will submit it to my teacher


Kelas : SMP

Mapel : Bahasa Inggris

Kategori : Conditional Sentence

Kata kunci : If

Pembahasan

Adverbial Clause adalah klausa yang berfungsi sebagai adverb atau kata keterangan buat ngejelasin kata yang lain bisa berupa verb, adjective, bisa juga adverb. Adverbial clause termasuk ke dalam dependent clause (anak kalimat, atau klausa yang tidak bisa berdiri sendiri). Dengan adanya klausa ini kemudian membuat sebuah kalimat menjadi complex sentence. Dan fungsinya sebagai adverb di independent clause (klausa yang bisa berdiri sendiri atau induk kalimat). bisa disimpulkan adverb berguna untuk menjawab berbagai pertanyaan “mengapa”, “bagaimana”, “dimana”, “kapan”
