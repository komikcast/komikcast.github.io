9. A group of 60 students went to the beach. Of the 33 students who brought a hat, 25 were boys. There were 36 boys altogether. Find the percentage of the girls who did not bring a hat to the total number of students went to the beach.


Jawab:

26.67%

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

number of girls = 60 - 36 = 24

number of girls with hat = 33 -25 = 8

number of girls without hat = 24 - 8 = 16

percentage of girls without hat = [tex]\frac{16}{60}[/tex] x 100% = 26.67%

A grup = 60 students

There were = 36 boys and 24 girls

33 students who brought a hat = 25 boys and 8 girls

percentage of the girls who did not bring a hat

= (24 - 8)/60 × 100%

= 16/60 × 100%

= 26,67%

15. Ben tries to find a job,but he hasn't had any luck yet


Jawaban:

Finding a job can be a difficult and time-consuming process, but there are a few things that Ben can do to increase his chances of finding employment:

Networking: Ben can reach out to his friends, family members, and professional contacts to see if they know of any job opportunities that may be a good fit for him.

Online job search: Ben can use online job search engines like Indeed, LinkedIn, and Glassdoor to find job openings in his field. He can also set up job alerts on these websites to be notified when new job postings become available.

Tailor his resume and cover letter: Ben should make sure that his resume and cover letter are tailored to the specific job he is applying for. This shows that he has taken the time to research the company and understand the requirements of the job.

Prepare for the interview: Ben should research the company and be prepared to answer common interview questions. He should also bring a list of questions to ask the interviewer to demonstrate his interest in the position.

Keep trying: Even if Ben doesn't get a job right away, he should keep trying and not get discouraged. The job search process can take time, but persistence and a positive attitude can go a long way in finding the right job.

Remember, finding a job takes time and effort, but with determination and the right approach, Ben will be able to find employment that is a good fit for him.
