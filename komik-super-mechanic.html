11. 5. The mechanic had been repairingchad been repairing my car.A. The mechanic had repaired my carB. The mechanic has repaired my car.C. The mechanic had repair my car.D. The mechanic has been repairing my car.pakai rumus PAST perfect​


Jawaban:

A. The mechanic had repaired my car

PembahasanPas Perfect

Past Perfect adalah salah satu Tenses dalam Bahasa inggris yg digunakan utk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lalu dan selesai di Kegiatan di masa lalu juga.

Rumus Past Perfect

(+) S + had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) had + S + V3 + O?

Kesimpulan

karena berdasarkan rumus Past Perfect

(+) S + had + V3 + O

=> The mechanic + had + repaired + my car

Keterangan:Subject => The mechanicsHad Verb 3 => repairedObject => My car

---- Semoga Membantu ----

29. 5. The mechanic had been repairing my car.A. The mechanic had repaired my car.B. The mechanic has repaired my car.C. The mechanic had repair my car.D. The mechanic has been repairing my car.Pakai rumus past perfect​


Jawaban:

A. The mechanic had repaired my car.

Pembahasan

Permintaan soal mengubahnya ke Past Perfect.

Past Perfect adalah tenses yg digunakan utk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lalu dan sudah selesai dilakukan pada Kegiatan lain di masa lalu juga.

Rumus Past Perfect

(+) S + Had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O

Maka Berdasarkan Rumus

A. The mechanic had repaired my car.

Penjelasan:

Ini yg tepat karena

Subject => The mechanisHad Verb 3 => repaired Object => My car

B. The mechanic has repaired my car.

Penjelasan:Opsi ini salah karena menggunakan Has (Present Perfect)

C. The mechanic had repair my car.

Penjelasan:Opsi C juga salah karen menggunakan Verb 1 (repair)

D. The mechanic has been repairing my car.

Penjelasan:Opsi D salah karena bukan Past Perfect (Had + V3) tapi has+been+V-ing

DETAIL JAWABAN

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Materi: Past Perfect

Level: JHS

Kode Soal: 5

Kode Kategorisasi: 9.5

Kata Kunci: Had, Verb 3
