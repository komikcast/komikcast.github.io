Jawaban:

x1 = -2, y1 = 4, x2 = 3, y2 = -6

Now, we substitute that points to

(y - y1)/(y2 - y1) = (x - x1)/(x2 - x1)

(y - 4)/(-6 - 4) = (x + 2)/(3 + 2)

(y-4)/(-10) = (x+2)/5

5y - 20 = -10x - 20

5y = -10x

y = -2x

So, the equation of line that pass the point (-2,4) and (3,-6) is y = -2x

The equation of a line is y = 8.(a) State its gradient(b) Write down the coordinates of any two points that lie on the line y = 8.(c) Give an example of the equation of a straight line that is perpendicular to the line y = 8. ​


Jawaban:

(a)

(i) The gradient of a line segment joining two points (x1, y1) and (x2, y2) is given by the formula:

Gradient (m) = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1)

For points P(5, -2) and Q(7, 4):

Gradient of PQ = (4 - (-2)) / (7 - 5) = 6 / 2 = 3

(ii) The length of a line segment joining two points (x1, y1) and (x2, y2) is given by the distance formula:

Length = √((x2 - x1)^2 + (y2 - y1)^2)

For points P(5, -2) and Q(7, 4):

Length of PQ = √((7 - 5)^2 + (4 - (-2))^2) = √(2^2 + 6^2) = √(4 + 36) = √40 = 2√10

(b) The equation of a line with gradient m passing through a point (x1, y1) is given by the point-slope form:

y - y1 = m(x - x1)

Given point (-3, 6) and gradient of PQ = 3:

Equation of the line: y - 6 = 3(x - (-3))

Simplify: y - 6 = 3x + 9

2.

(a) The gradient of the line y = 8 is 0.

(b) Since the line y = 8 is a horizontal line, any point lying on this line will have the same y-coordinate, which is 8. For example, (1, 8) and (-5, 8) are two points on the line y = 8.

(c) A line perpendicular to y = 8 would be a vertical line. An example of the equation of a vertical line passing through a specific x-coordinate, say x = 3, is x = 3. Vertical lines do not have a slope (gradient), and their equation is of the form x = constant.

21. Find the equation of the line passing through the point (6,3) that is perpendicular to the line 4x−5y=−10. Step 1: Find the slope of the line 4x−5y=−10.What would the perpendicular slope be?Step 2: Use the slope to find the y-intercept of the perpendicular line.Step 3: Write the equation of the line that passes through the point (6,3) that is perpendicular to the line 4x−5y=−10​


Find the equation of the line passing through the point (6,3) that is perpendicular to the line 4x - 5y = -10?

Solution :

4x - 5y = -10

- 5y = 4x - 10

Divide both sides by -5

y = (4/-5)x - (10/-5)

y = (-4/5)x + 2

From the slope intercept form we get

m = - 4/5

The slope of a perpendicular is a negative inverse of - 4/5

m = (5/4)

Slope intercept form for the perpendicular line is

y = (5/4) x +b

Solve for b using (6, 3)

3 = (5/4) (6) + b

3 = 30/4 + b

b = 3 - 30/4

b = (12 - 30)/4

b = -18/4

b = -9/2

So the perpendicular line is y = (5/4)x - 9/2

The equation of the line passing through the point (6,3) that is perpendicular to the line 4x-5y=-10 is y = (5/4)x - 9/2.

40. Three are night that never die'' the line above used figurative language


"These are the nights that never die". kutipan kalimat tersebut jika diartikan dalam bahasa Indonesia memiliki arti "inilah malam-malam yang takkan pernah mati".

Pembahasan

Kutipan kalimat di atas merupakan potongan lagu yang berjudul "The Nights" karya Avicii. lagu ini merupakan salah satu lagu solo yang cukup terkenal setelah dinyanyikan kepada khalayak oleh Avicii.

Adapun kutipan lagu yang disebutkan pada soal di atas merupakan Figurative Language. Yaitu bahasa kiasan (atau disebut juga majas), merupakan cara mengekspresikan diri dengan tidak menggunakan makna kata yang realistis, bahasa kiasan ini biasanya menggunakan bentuk perbandingan yang sifatnya melebih-lebihkan.

Fungsi lain dari bahasa kiasan juga menunjukkan sebuah kreativitas seseorang dalam berbahasa dan juga menyampaikan ide-ide yang rumit.
