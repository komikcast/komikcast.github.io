2. Hasan is designing a gate for a minimalist type of house. The actual length and height of the gate is 6 m and 2 m. If the length of the gate on the sketch is 15 cm, then the height of the gate on the sketch is...


Jawab:

﻿Height gate on the sketch = 5 cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

﻿example;

﻿Height = H

﻿Length = L

no. 1 = real gate

no. 2 = gate on the sketch

﻿Length gate on cm = 6 m x 100 cm

﻿= 600 cm

﻿Height gate on cm = 2 m x 100 cm

﻿= 200 cm

Formula;

[tex]\frac{a1}{a2}[/tex] = [tex]\frac{b1}{b2}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{height 1}{height 2} = \frac{length 1}{length 2}[/tex]

[tex]\frac{height1}{200 cm}[/tex]= [tex]\frac{15 cm}{600 cm}[/tex]

﻿height 1 = 200 x [tex]\frac{15}{600}[/tex]

﻿height 1 = [tex]\frac{3.000}{600}[/tex]

﻿height 1 = 5 cm

So,  Height gate on the sketch = 5 cm

15. write the passive forms of sentence they don't allow parking in front of their gate​


PASSIVE VOICE

Parking in front of their gate is not allowed by them

EXPLANATION

Passive voice is used to emphasize the recipient of the verb and the action rather than the agent (the doer of action).

The main construction of passive-voice sentence is the combination of the auxiliary BE and past participle.

Here are steps how to change the active-voice to the passive-voice sentence :

~ Identify the object of an active-voice sentence and move it to the subject of passive form.

~ The agent (subject of active voice) is placed in the preposition object accompanied with the "by" preposition.

~ All of the verbs are conjugated in the past participle.

BASED ON YOUR QUESTION

SIMPLE PRESENT

A : S+do/does+not+Verb1+O

P : S(O)+to be(is/am/are)+not+Verb3+by Agent

They (Subject)+don't allow (Verb)+parking in front of their gate (Object)

changes to :

Parking in front of their gate (New subject)+ is not allowed(Verb)+ by+ them (Agent)
