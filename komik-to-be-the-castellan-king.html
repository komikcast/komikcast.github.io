8. resosolution of the story dari full monkey who wanted to be a king​


Jawaban:

The resolution of the story is that the monkey's ambition and lack of wisdom led to his downfall. He believed the false promises of the fox and did not use his critical thinking skills to assess the situation. As a result, he fell into the trap and was unable to escape. The story teaches a moral lesson that it is important to be careful and use good judgment, especially when someone tries to flatter or deceive us.

Penjelasan:

Artinya, resolusi cerita tersebut adalah bahwa ambisi dan kurangnya kebijaksanaan si monyet membawanya ke kehancuran.
