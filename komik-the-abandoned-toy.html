18. Question 3 A solid cylinder with radius 1.6 cm is attached to the hemisphere to make a toy. The total volume of the toy is 300 cm³. Amathematically similar toy has volume 19 200 cm³. Calculate the radius of the cylinder for this toy. ​


Materi : Perbandingan dan Skala

300 cm³ <=> r = 1,6 cm

19.200 cm³ <=> r = ? cm

___________________/

19.200/300 × 1,6 cm

= 192/3 × 1,6 cm

= 64 × 1,6 cm

= 102,4 cm
