16. the capital city was taken by the enemy


Materi : Passive voice and Active voice 
Kelas : IX SMP 

The capital city was taken by the enemy  ---> Passive 
⇒ Ibu kota tersebut direbut oleh musuh 

The enemy took The capital city ---> Active 
⇒ Musuh tersebut merebut ibu kota tersebut 

semoga membantu 


Answer;
_______
Passive =>The capital city was taken by the enemy

Active=> The enemy took the capital city

Keterangan :
Cara merubah kalimat passive menjadi kali kalimat aktiv yaitu dengan cara menukar posisi objek yang ada dikalimat passive yaitu yang ada diakhir kalimat ditukar posisinya menjadi subjek di awal kalimat dan posisi subjek yang ada pada kalimat passive menjadi objek dalam kalimat aktive(intinya tukar posisi aja). Lalu kata kerja bentuk ketiga/verb3 yang terdapat pada kalimat passive harus dirubah menjadi kata kerja bentuk kedua ketika mau dijadikan kalimat aktiv.

In a kingdom far, far away lived a king and his three daughters. The king loved them very much for they were good and beautiful girls, but one day he decided to test how much they loved him.

The king gathered his daughters around him and asked each of them the same question: "How much do you love me?"

"Oh, father," said the eldest daughter, "I love you as much as all the diamonds and all the rubies in the world and more!" "That is excellent," said the king happily.

"Dear father," said the second daughter, "I love you as much as all the gold and silver in the world and more!" "Wonderful!" said her father and smiled.

Then it was the youngest daughter's turn. "My father and king, I love you as much as all the salt in the world and more!" The king was very surprised at her answer. "What did you say? "How dare you! Your sisters love me as much as diamonds and gold, and you love me as little as salt," said the king angrily. "Leave this kingdom right now and never come back!"

The little princess hid herself in the woods, but she soon got very tired. She sat on a log and started to cry, when a prince, who was passing by on his steed, heard her. As soon as he saw the pretty princess, the young prince fell in love with her.
