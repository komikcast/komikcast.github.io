9. The distance between City X and City Y is 280 km. The distance between City Y and City Z is 238 km. A bullet train takes 54 minutes to travel from City X to City Y and another 51 minutes to travel from City Y to City Z. Find the average speed of the bullet train for the whole journey from City X to City Z in km/h. kak tolong jawab aku hari ini di kumpul


DISTANCE

CITY X TO CITY Y = 280 KM

CITY Y TO CITY Z = 238 KM

CITY X TO CITY Z = 280 + 238 = 518 KM

TIME

CITY X TO CITY Y = 54 MIN

CITY Y TO CITY Z = 51 MIN

CITY X TO CITY Z = 54 + 51 = 105 MIN = 1 HR 45 MIN (1,75 HR)

AVG. SPEED

518 ÷ 1,75 = 296 KM / H
