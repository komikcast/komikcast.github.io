The resolution of the story is that the monkey's ambition and lack of wisdom led to his downfall. He believed the false promises of the fox and did not use his critical thinking skills to assess the situation. As a result, he fell into the trap and was unable to escape. The story teaches a moral lesson that it is important to be careful and use good judgment, especially when someone tries to flatter or deceive us.

What is the main idea of the following paragraphs? One has been done for you.

b.

Paragraph 1 : Hughie Erskin was a poor, good-looking, and nice young man.

Paragraph 2: Hughie and Laura couldn't get married, unless he had ten thousand pounds.

Paragraph 3: Hughie met a beggar and gave him all he had.

Penjelasan:

a. Tentang apakah cerita itu?

5. Seorang pemuda miskin yang memiliki hati yang murah hati meskipun kekurangan.

b. Apa gagasan utama paragraf berikut?

Paragraf 1: Hughie Erskine adalah pemuda yang miskin, tampan, dan baik hati.

Paragraf 2: Hughie dan Laura tidak bisa menikah, kecuali dia punya sepuluh ribu pound.

Paragraf 3: Hughie bertemu dengan seorang pengemis dan memberinya semua yang dia miliki.

Paragraf 4: Hughie dan Alan bertemu dan berbicara tentang pengemis dan juga Alan memberi tahu pengemis tentang kehidupan pribadi Hughie.

Paragraf 5: Pengemis (Baron Hausberg dikenal sebagai jutawan) memberi Hughie sepuluh ribu pound.

Paragraf 6: "Model jutawan cukup langka tetapi jutawan model masih lebih jarang"

Hughie Erskine was a very good-looking young man. Men and women liked him because he never said an unkind word about anyone. But he was not rich and clever and always changing his job.

Hughie loved a girl named Laura Merton, the daughter of a retired colonel. Laura loved him too, but her father would not allow them to marry unless Hughie could provide ten thousand pounds.

One morning, Hughie visited his friend, Alan Trevor, a famous painter who was finishing a full-sized picture of a beggar man. The beggar was standing in the corner of the room. He looked so miserable that Hughie felt sorry for him. When Alan came out of the room, Hughie gave the beggar a sovereign, the only thing he had in his pocket. Then he said goodbye to Alan.

That night Hughie went to the Palette Club and met Alan. Alan told Hughie about the old beggar he had used as his model. The beggar asked Alan about Hughie. Then Alan revealed that the beggar was Baron Hausberg, one of the richest men in Europe. Hughie was very startled but also angry with Alan, because Alan had told Baron Hausberg about his private life. Hughie walked home unhappily, leaving Alan laughing loudly.

The next morning, while Hughie was having his breakfast, Baron Hausberg's messenger came and handed him a letter from the Baron. To his surprise, there was a cheque for ten thousand pounds as a wedding present to Hughie and Laura. Hughie could not convey in words how happy he was because he could marry Laura.

As a wedding party, Alan remarked, "Millionaire models are rare enough but model millionaire are rarer still".

Translation:

Hughie Erskine adalah pria muda yang sangat tampan. Pria dan wanita menyukainya karena dia tidak pernah mengatakan sepatah kata buruk tentang siapa pun. Tapi dia tidak kaya dan pintar dan selalu berganti pekerjaan.

Hughie mencintai seorang gadis bernama Laura Merton, putri seorang pensiunan kolonel. Laura juga mencintainya, tetapi ayahnya tidak mengizinkan mereka menikah kecuali Hughie bisa memberi sepuluh ribu pound.

Suatu pagi, Hughie mengunjungi temannya, Alan Trevor, seorang pelukis terkenal yang sedang menyelesaikan lukisan seorang pengemis berukuran penuh. Pengemis itu berdiri di sudut ruangan. Dia tampak sangat sedih sehingga Hughie merasa kasihan padanya. Ketika Alan keluar dari ruangan, Hughie memberi pengemis itu, satu-satunya yang ada di sakunya. Lalu dia mengucapkan selamat tinggal pada Alan.

Malam itu Hughie pergi ke Palette Club dan bertemu Alan. Alan memberi tahu Hughie tentang pengemis tua yang ia gunakan sebagai modelnya. Pengemis itu bertanya pada Alan tentang Hughie. Kemudian Alan mengungkapkan bahwa pengemis itu adalah Baron Hausberg, salah satu orang terkaya di Eropa. Hughie sangat terkejut tetapi juga marah dengan Alan, karena Alan telah memberi tahu Baron Hausberg tentang kehidupan pribadinya. Hughie berjalan pulang dengan sedih, meninggalkan Alan yang tertawa keras.

Keesokan paginya, ketika Hughie sedang sarapan, utusan Baron Hausberg datang dan menyerahkan sepucuk surat dari Baron. Yang mengejutkan, ada cek sepuluh ribu pound sebagai hadiah pernikahan untuk Hughie dan Laura. Hughie tidak bisa menyampaikan dengan kata-kata betapa bahagianya dia karena dia bisa menikahi Laura.

Pada saat pesta pernikahan, Alan berkomentar, "Model miliarder cukup langka tetapi model miliarder masih lebih jarang".
