1. The beads are 400 beads. 30% of beads are How many green beads? 30 out of 100 X 300​


It seems like you're trying to calculate the number of green beads based on a given percentage. The problem states that there are 400 beads and you want to find out how many of those beads are green, with 30% being the target percentage.

To calculate the number of green beads:

Number of green beads = Percentage (30%) * Total number of beads (400)

Number of green beads = 0.30 * 400 = 120

So, there are 120 green beads out of the total 400 beads.

19. Lucy has a necklace which has red beads and blue beads. For every red bead, there are four blue beads. There are 20 beads in total. How many of these are blue?


Jawaban:

Let's denote the number of red beads as "R" and the number of blue beads as "B."

From the information given, we can create two equations:

   R + B = 20 (There are 20 beads in total).

   For every red bead, there are four blue beads: R = 4B.

Now, we can use these equations to solve for the number of blue beads (B).

First, substitute the value of R from the second equation into the first equation:

4B + B = 20

Combine like terms:

5B = 20

Now, divide both sides by 5 to find the number of blue beads (B):

B = 20 / 5

B = 4

So, there are 4 blue beads.


20. Eva and Lily had a total of 3672 beads. When Eva gave Lily 560 beads, they had an equal number of beads. How many beads did Lily have at first?


Banyak manik-manik yang dimiliki Lily pada awalnya adalah 1276 manik-manik. Untuk menyelesaikan soal ini, kita gunakan metode eliminasi.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Diketahui:

Eva and Lily had a total of 3672 beads.

When Eva gave Lily 560 beads, they had an equal number of beads.

Ditanya:

How many beads did Lily have at first?

Jawab:

Langkah pertama, kita buat persamaannya.

Misalkan Eva = E dan Lily = L

Eva and Lily had a total of 3672 beads.

E + L = 3672 (persamaan 1)

When Eva gave Lily 560 beads, they had an equal number of beads.

E - 560 = L + 560

E - L = 560 + 560

E - L = 1120 (persamaan 2)

Langkah kedua, kita eliminasi E dari persamaan 1 dan persamaan 2, untuk mendapatkan nilai L.

E + L = 3672

E - L = 1120     -

  2L = 2552

     L = 2552 : 2

     L = 1276

Banyak manik-manik yang dimiliki Lily pada awalnya adalah 1276 manik-manik.
