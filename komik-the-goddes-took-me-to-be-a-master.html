18. "My father take me to school." The present perfect form of the sentence above is ... a.My father have taken me to school. b.My father has taken me to school. c.My father had taken me to school. d. My father took me to school.​


Jawaban:

The correct answer is b. My father has taken me to school.

The present perfect form indicates an action that occurred in the past but has a relevance to the present. In this case, the action of taking to school happened in the past but has an impact or connection to the present moment. Using "has taken" in the sentence represents the present perfect tense.
