13. a magician showed a magic trick where he picked one card from a stabdard deck. Determine what the probability is that the card will be a queen card?


Jawab:

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

A standard deck of playing cards contains 52 cards, which are divided into 4 suits: hearts, diamonds, clubs, and spades. Each suit contains 13 cards, including one queen card.

Since there are 4 queen cards in a standard deck (one for each suit), the probability of picking a queen card at random from a standard deck is:

Probability = (Number of Queen Cards) / (Total Number of Cards) = 4 / 52 = 1 / 13 ≈ 0.0769

So, the probability that the magician will pick a queen card from a standard deck is approximately 0.0769 or about 7.69%.

PASSIVE VOICE

Jika active voice menunjukkan subjek yang melakukan sebuah aksi, maka passive voice digunakan ketika subjek yang menerima sebuah aksi.

Rumus

Simple past:

S + Was/Were + V3

Simple present:

S + Is/Am/Are + V3

Present perfect:

S + Have/Has + Been + V3

Note:

Subjek dan objeknya ditukar apabila mengganti dari kalimat aktif ke pasif maupun sebaliknya.Perhatikan subjeknya, gunakan to be yang sesuai (singular/plural)Rumus di atas hanya yang berkaitan dengan tense yang digunakan di soal. Selebihnya silakan untuk dicari sendiri ya.

21. The hydrostatic pressure on A is 8,000 N/m2 and the hydrostatic pressure on B is 6,400 N/m2. If the density of water is 1000 kg/m3 and the acceleration due to gravity is 10 m/s2 , the difference between the depths of A and B is...


The difference in depths (h) can be calculated using the formula:

h = (P_A - P_B) / (density * g)

where P_A is the pressure at point A, P_B is the pressure at point B, density is the density of water, and g is the acceleration due to gravity.

Plugging in the given values, we get:

h = (8000 - 6400) / (1000 * 10) = 1.6 m

So the difference between the depths of A and B is 1.6 meters.
