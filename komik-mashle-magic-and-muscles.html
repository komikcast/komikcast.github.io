10. orientation,complication,resolution,and coda dari narrative text finding farmers magic box ​


Struktur tek pada cerita Finding Farmers Magic Box:

Orientation:

In the afternoon, there was a poor farmer. He lives with his wife.

Complication:

Apple began to disappear and the box full of gold coins. The farmer soon became rich.

Resolution:

As well as gold coins began to disappear and the box is filled with the dead. The dead bodies should be pulled out and buried. To do this, the farmer had to spend all the money that had been collected earlier.

Coda

When he had spent all his money, broke the box. The farmer was ugly as before, terrible ending.

Pembahasan

Terdapat 4 struktur dalam teks narasi, antara lain orientasi, komplikasi, resolusi, dan reorientasi:

1. Orientasi: Penulis membuat kalimat yang menjabarkan latar waktu, tempat, penjelasan tokoh dan wataknya. Teks narasi menceritakan sudut pandang setiap tokoh.

2. Komplikasi: Dalam teks narasi ada peristiwa atau kejadian penting. Peristiwa ini diceritakan secara detail mulai dari penyebab, pemicu, sampai timbulnya konflik antar tokoh. Konflik ini menimbulkan peristiwa lain hingga mencapai puncaknya.

3. Resolusi: Struktur ketiga menceritakan konflik cerita yang semakin menurun. Kemudian konflik bisa diselesaikan.

4. Reorientasi adalah struktur keempat adalah penutup teks. Dalam teks narasi, penutup menyampaikan pesan moral dari cerita tersebut. Namun, reorientasi atau disebut juga coda sifatnya opsional bisa tidak dihadirkan dalam teks.

32. Why we need muscles?


Jawaban:

Muscles are essential for various functions in the human body. They serve several critical purposes:

1. Movement: Muscles are responsible for enabling movement in our bodies. Skeletal muscles, which are attached to our bones, contract and relax to create motion. Without muscles, we wouldn't be able to walk, run, jump, lift objects, or even breathe.

2. Posture and Stability: Muscles play a crucial role in maintaining our posture and stability. They help us sit or stand upright and keep our body balanced. Without muscles, we would have difficulty maintaining an erect posture.

3. Heat Generation: Muscles generate heat as a byproduct of their activity. This heat is vital for maintaining our body temperature. When we shiver in the cold, it's our muscles contract rapidly to generate heat and keep us warm.

4. Protection and Support: Muscles protect and support vital organs. For example, the muscles of the abdominal wall help protect our internal organs, and the muscles of the back support the spine.

5. Metabolism: Muscles are metabolically active tissues, meaning they require energy to function. This energy demand can contribute to overall calorie expenditure, which can be important for weight management and metabolic health.

6. Blood Circulation: Smooth muscles in the walls of blood vessels help regulate blood pressure by constricting or dilating the vessels. This ensures proper blood flow to different parts of the body.

7. Digestion: Muscles in the digestive system, such as the smooth muscles in the stomach and intestines, help move food along the digestive tract, allowing for proper digestion and absorption of nutrients.

In summary, muscles are fundamental to our ability to move, maintain posture, generate heat, protect organs, support bodily functions, and play a role in various physiological processes. Without muscles, our bodies would be unable to perform essential tasks required for survival and daily activities.
