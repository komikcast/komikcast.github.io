8. The teacher is collecting the assignments. The passive voice is..... *


Jawaban:The assignments are being collected by the teacher

Penjelasan :

Subject + to be (conjugated) + past participle + rest of sentence

Simple present

is/are + past participle

Present continuous

is/are being + past participle

Simple past

was/were + past participle

Past continuous

were/was being + past participle

Present perfect

have/has been + past participle

Past perfect

had been + past participle

Future

will be + past participle

Future continuous

will be being + past participle

Present conditional

would be + past participle

Past conditional

would have been  + past participle

Modal

must/should/etc be + past participle

Jawaban:

Active: The teacher is collecting the assignments.

Passive: The assignments are being collected by the teacher.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat passive pada jawaban di atas menggunakan pola Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + being + Verb-3
