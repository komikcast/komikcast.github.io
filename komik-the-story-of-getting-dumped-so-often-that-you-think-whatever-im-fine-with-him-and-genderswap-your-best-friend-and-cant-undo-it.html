Making a dialogue based on the situation above

Lena: Hi, Ella. Have you watched this movie called Dilan?

Ella: Yes, I have. How about you?

Lena: Yeah, me too.

Ella: What do you think about that movie?

Lena: I think Dilan is a very good movie. I was very excited when I watched that movie.

Ella: That's true but I am afraid I have to disagree with you because I think the movie's plot is so boring.

Lena: Why do you think so?

Ella: Because the story is predictable and not as good as a theatre movie. I think Dilan is like an ftv.

Lena: Well, I think so too but it's still a good movie for me. I like romance genre.

Ella: Alright, then. Thanks for your opinion about Dilan. I have to go now. Bye, Lena.

Lena: Nevermind. Bye, Ella.

Penjelasan:

Dialog di atas mengandung expression of asking opinion and giving opinion.

Asking opinion digunakan untuk menanyakan pendapat kepada orang lain.

Contoh expression of asking opinion pada kalimat di atas:

What do you think about ...?

Giving opinion digunakan untuk memberikan pendapat yang ada di pikiran kita.

Contoh expression of giving opinion pada kalimat di atas:

I think ...
