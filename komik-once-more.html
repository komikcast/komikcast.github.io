29. mi. agus:who can answer the question?. Lorinda : what is your question sir? . mr agus: ........ I'ii read the quetion once more.​


Jawaban:

Mr Agus: Who can answer the question?

Lorinda: What is your question, Sir?

Mr Agus: Okay, listen for the question. I'll read the question once more.​

Penjelasan:

Pada kalimat "I'll read the question once more", menunjukkan bahwa Mr Agus akan membacakan sekali lagi soalnya. Jadi, kalimat sebelumnya dapat berhubungan dengan 'mendengarkan'.

Alternatif lainnya:

Mr Agus: Pay attention, please. I'll read the question once more.

33. i think i have...... him once more​


Jawab: met (bertemu)

I think, I have met him once more

(Saya pikir saya telah bertemu dengannya sekali)

Catatan Rumus:

Kalimat itu merupakan Present Perfect karena ada “once” maka Rumus yg digunakan:

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O

= I have met him once more

Catatan Subject + has/have:

I, you, we, they = have

He, she, it = has

Catatan verb:

V1 meet = V2 met = V3 met

____________________________

Materi: Present Perfect
