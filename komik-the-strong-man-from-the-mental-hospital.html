16. COMBINE THESE PAIRS OF SENTENCES. USE THE SECOND SUBJECT AS ADJECTIVE CLAUSE 1. The man is the director of a hospital. He is delivering a speech 2. The woman has been working for the hospital since 2001. Her husband passed 3. The boy suffers from dengue. I took care of him last night. 4. The woman finally passed away. She lost much blood. 5. The man works for general hospital. I saw him yesterday. 6. The car broke down yesterday. The mechanic is repairing it now. 7. The man is a lecturer. He graduated from outstanding university. 8. The pregnant woman finally underwent the cesarean operation. I took her to the hospital. 9. The man is a genacologist. He works for Persahabatan Hospital. 10. The building is a library building. It is located in the opposite of laboratory.


Jawaban:

1. The man who is delivering a speech is the director of a hospital.

2. The woman whose husband passed has been working for the hospital since 2001.

3. The boy whom I took care last night suffers from dengue.

4. The woman who lost much blood finally passed away.

5. The man whom I saw yesterday works for general hospital.

6. The car which the mechanic is repairing now broke down yesterday.

7. The man who graduated from outstanding university is a lecturer.

8. The pregnant woman whom I took to the hospital finally underwent the cesarean operation.

9. The man who works for Persahabatan Hospital is a genacologist.

10. The building which is located in the opposite of laboratory is a library building.

Penjelasan:

Pada perintah soal di atas diminta untuk menggabungkan kalimat dengan menggunakan kalimat kedua sebagai adjective clause. Kalimat yang bergaris bawah adalah second subject.

Adjective clause who, whom, dan whose digunakan untuk subjek orang. Sedangkan which digunakan untuk subjek benda.
