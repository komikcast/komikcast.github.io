1. We adopted these two cats. They are a bonded pair. Combine these two sentence using an adverbial clause above. A. We adopted these two cats after they are a bonded pair.V. We adopted these two cats if they are a bonded pair C. We adopted these two cats where they are a bonded pair D. We adopted these two cats since they are a bonded pair E. We adopted these two cats because they are a bonded pair​


We can combine the two sentences using an adverbial clause as follows:

E. We adopted these two cats because they are a bonded pair​.

Pembahasan

Pilihan yang paling tepat adalah E, karena berdasarkan konteks adverbial clause di atas berdasarkan fungsi menyatakan alasan (reason). Yaitu kalimat di atas menjadi bermakna:

Kami mengadopsi dua kucing ini karena mereka berpasangan.

Jadi alasan dari we adopted these two cats adalah karena they are a bonded pair.

Pilihan lain tidak tepat karena:

We adopted these two cats after they are a bonded pair, ini menggunakan keterangan waktu (after). We adopted these two cats if they are a bonded pair, menggunakan if (jika) tapi setelah if seharusnya past tense seperti frasa sebelumnya. We adopted these two cats where they are a bonded pair, menggunakan kata keterangan tempat (where) maka tidak tepat. Pelajari tentang adverbial clause di sini:https://brainly.co.id/tugas/6698874
