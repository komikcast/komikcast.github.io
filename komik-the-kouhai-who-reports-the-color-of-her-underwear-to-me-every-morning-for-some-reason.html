PEMBAHASAN Active VoiceKalimat aktif yang Subjeknya melakukan aksi dalam kalimatnyaPassive VoiceKalimat pasif yang Objeknya diberikan aksi oleh subjek dalam kalimatnyaPola PassiveBe + V3

Perintah soal

Change the following sentences into "passive sentence"

Ubahlah kalimat-kalimat berikut ke dalam "kalimat pasif"

Berdasarkan Rumus Present Tense Active: S + V1 (-s/es) + OPassive: O + Be (is/am/are) + V3 + By S

Maka berikut

1.They buy vegetables and fish in the market.

=> Vegetables and fish are bought by them in the market

2.The man builds a big house in the city

=> A big house is built by the man in the city

3.The children play kites in the field every afternoon

=> Kites are played by the children in the field every afternoon

4.The girl waters flower in front of her house every morning

=> Flower is watered by the girl in front her house every morning

5.Today, mother cook soup for us

=> Today, soup is cooked by mother for us

6.The little girl sweep the floor of her house in the afternoon

=> The floor of her house is swept by the little girl in the afternoon

7.Rita washes her dresses every Sunday

=> Her dresses are washed by Rita Every Sunday

8.Father drives his car to his office every morning.

=> His car is driven by father to his office every morning

9.Many boys and girl climb the mountain on sunday morning

=> The mountain is climbed by many boys and girls on Sunday morning

10.uncle sell his goats to the market

=> His goats are sold by uncle to the market
