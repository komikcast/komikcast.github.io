33. the man who(was/were) standing on the stage is my neighbour​


the man who (was) standing on the stage is my neighbour.

semoga membantuu

Jawaban:

the man who was standing on the stage is my neighbour

Penjelasan:

Was digunakan untuk subject I, She, He, It

Were digunakan untuk subject You, We, They

pada kalimat di atas subjectnya adalah the man subjek tersebut termasuk pada "It" maka penggunaan to be nya adalah Was

Semoga membantu

35. Arti who was the man on the text?


Soal:

Arti “who was the man on the text?”

Jawab:

Siapa PRIA yang ada dalam teks itu?

Perkata:

Who = siapa

Was = to be Past Tense-singular

The man = Pria itu

On = dalam

The text = teks tersebut

Berdasarkan Rumus Past Tense (nominnal)

(?) WH + was/were + S + O?

= what + was + the man + on the text?

(Siapa PRIA itu dalam teks tersebut?)

==== Semoga Membantu ====

Kelas: SMP
