Penjelasan:

PASSIVE VOICE

Jika active voice menunjukkan subjek yang melakukan sebuah aksi, maka passive voice digunakan ketika subjek yang menerima sebuah aksi.

Rumus

Simple past:

S + Was/Were + V3

Simple present:

S + Is/Am/Are + V3

Present perfect:

S + Have/Has + Been + V3

Note:

Subjek dan objeknya ditukar apabila mengganti dari kalimat aktif ke pasif maupun sebaliknya.Perhatikan subjeknya, gunakan to be yang sesuai (singular/plural)Rumus di atas hanya yang berkaitan dengan tense yang digunakan di soal. Selebihnya silakan untuk dicari sendiri ya.

14. Change the sentences from active to passive! 1. The magician held the show last week. 2. A plumber is going to fix the leaky faucet.​


Jawaban:

Sure, here are the passive voice versions of the sentences you provided:

 1.  The show was held by the magician last week.

 2. The leaky faucet is going to be fixed by a plumber.

Penjelasan:

Here are the rules for changing active voice to passive voice:

   Make the subject of the sentence the object of the sentence.    Add the word "by" before the subject of the sentence.    Change the verb to the past participle form.

Here are some examples of how to change active voice to passive voice:

Active voice: John is writing a book.

Passive voice: A book is being written by John.

Active voice: Mary is painting the house.

Passive voice: The house is being painted by Mary.

Active voice: The teacher is teaching the students.

Passive voice: The students are being taught by the teacher.

30. Exercise 1 Answer the following questions based on the story of "Chinese Dragon". Why did the man want to live forever? What did the man know about the dragon? What did the man want from the dragon? Why did people in the island buy poison? What happened to the man after the dragon take him to the island? Why did the man want the dragon take him back? Jawaban:

1. Why did the man want to live forever?

Answer: The man wanted to live forever because he desired eternal life and believed it would bring him happiness and fulfillment.

2. What did the man know about the dragon?

Answer: The man knew that the dragon possessed the ability to grant immortality.

3. What did the man want from the dragon?

Answer: The man wanted the dragon to grant him immortality so that he could live forever.

4. Why did people on the island buy poison?

Answer: The people on the island bought poison because they wanted to kill themselves. They believed that death was preferable to living forever, as they saw the man's eternal existence as a curse.

5. What happened to the man after the dragon took him to the island?

Answer: After the dragon took him to the island, the man realized the loneliness and despair of living forever. He witnessed the people on the island choosing death over immortality and realized the consequences of his desire.

6. Why did the man want the dragon to take him back?

Answer: The man wanted the dragon to take him back because he realized the folly of his wish for eternal life. He wanted to return to his mortal existence and live a finite but meaningful life.

7. What can we learn from the story?

Answer: We can learn that the pursuit of immortality without considering the consequences can lead to feelings of loneliness, despair, and a lack of purpose. The story highlights the importance of appreciating the transient nature of life and finding value in the limited time we have.

8. Give a title to the story!

Answer: "Desire for Immortality: The Lonely Journey of a Man and the Wisdom of Mortality"
