21. In a class of 16 girls and 20 boys, a pupil is selected at random.a) Find the probability that the pupils selected is a boy!b) If the boy selected leaves the class and a second pupils dan then selected at random, find the probability that the pupil selected is a girl!​


Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Sample size (nS) = 16 + 20 = 36

number of boy (nB) = 20

number of girl (nG) = 16

a)

Probably of boy being selected:

[tex]p(boy) = \frac{nb}{ns} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{20}{36} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{5}{9} [/tex]

[tex] = 0.556[/tex]

b) if the selected boy leaves then, the sample size will be

[tex]ns = 16 + 19 = 35[/tex]

therefore,

[tex]p(girl) = \frac{ng}{ns} [/tex]

[tex] = \frac{16}{35} [/tex]

[tex] = 0.46[/tex]
