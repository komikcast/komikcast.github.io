4. The Legend of Salatiga Many years ago there was a very honest sunan. He taught people about the meaning of life and about religion, he is known as Sunan Kalijaga. He traveled to spread his precious lesson. One day, Sunan Kalijaga arrived at a village. Sunan went to the village chief's house. Then Sunan Kalijaga asked for a job without telling his real name. The wife of the chief said he could help cut the grass. Sunan Kalijaga started working by cutting grass to feed the chief's horses. Soon, the chief of the village realized that his employee was Sunan Kalijaga. The chief was extremely surprised. Then he decided to follow the lesson of Sunan Kalijaga. To follow the lesson of Sunan Kalijaga, the chief had to leave everything behind. But the wife didn't agree so she brought her jewelry in bamboo. When the three of them were taking a rest in a village, a robber approached and took everything from the wife of the chief. Sunan said that this village would be full of people and really busy. Then Sunan named the place Salatiga which means three wrong people. The three wrong people were the chief, his wife, and also the robber who loves collecting things. And now, this village is known as Salatiga.tuliskan 10 kalimat dalam bahasa Indonesia yg menceritakan kembali cerita tersebut.​


Jawaban:

1. Sunan Kalijaga adalah seorang sunan yang sangat jujur dan membimbing orang tentang makna hidup dan agama.

2. Ia pergi berkeliling untuk menyebarkan ajarannya.

3. Suatu hari, Sunan Kalijaga tiba di sebuah desa dan meminta pekerjaan pada kepala desa tanpa menyebutkan namanya.

4. Ia mulai bekerja mencukur rumput untuk memenuhi kebutuhan kuda kepala desa.

5. Kepala desa akhirnya mengetahui bahwa pekerja barunya adalah Sunan Kalijaga dan memutuskan untuk mengikuti ajarannya.

6. Namun, istri kepala desa tidak setuju dan membawa perhiasannya dalam bambu.

7. Saat mereka beristirahat di sebuah desa, seorang pencuri datang dan mengambil semua harta istri kepala desa.

8. Sunan Kalijaga berkata bahwa desa itu akan dipenuhi oleh banyak orang dan sangat ramai.

9. Kemudian ia memberikan nama Salatiga yang berarti tiga orang yang salah: kepala desa, istrinya, dan pencuri yang suka mengumpulkan barang.

10. Dan sekarang, desa itu dikenal sebagai Salatiga.
