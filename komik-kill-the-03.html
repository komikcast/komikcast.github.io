(+) Monkeys hold things in their hands.

Penjelasan:

Karena pada soal tidak dilampirkan gambar binatang, saya mencontohkan gambar monyet. Pada contoh saya, monyet sedang memegang dahan dengan tangannya.

Soal di atas adalah mendeskripsikan gambar hewan dengan simple present tense.

Rumus simple present tense adalah

(+) Plural subject + verb 1.

Singular subject + verb 1 with s/es.

(-) Plural subject + don't verb 1.

Singular subject + doesn't + verb 1 without s/es.

(?) Do + plural subject + verb 1?

Does + singular subject + verb 1 without s/es?

19. My cat didn't kill the bird Ubah ke kalimat pasif


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS

MATERI : ACTIVE AND PASSIVE VOICE.


SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN

========================

My cat didn't kill the bird. (Active simple past)


Rumus simple past

(+) Subject + V2 + Object

(-) Subject + Did + Not + V1 + Object

(?) Did + Subject+ V1 + Object


Karena kalimat di atas merupakan kalimat negatif, maka berikut adalah rumus kalimat pasive dalam bentuk negatif.

(-) Subject + To be (was/were) + not + V3 + By...


CATATAN !!!

Subject kalimat aktif berubah jadi object kalimat pasif.


THE ANSWER

============

My cat didn't kill the bird… (active)

Passive nya :

=> The bird was not killed by my cat.

The bird was not killed by my cat
