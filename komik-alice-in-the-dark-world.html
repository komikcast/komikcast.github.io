24. kuis buleartikan- beauty and the beast- alice in the wonderland​


JAWABAN↓

- beauty and the beast

- alice in the Wonderland

Bahasa Indonesia:

- beauty and the beast

• Si cantik dan si buruk rupa

>> beauty = > Kecantikan

= > Si cantik

>> and = > dan

>> the beast = > buruk rupa

= > Si buruk rupa

Bahasa Indonesia:

- Alice in the Wonderland

• Alice di Negeri Ajaib

>> Alice = > Alice

>> in = > di

>> the Wonderland = > Negeri Ajaib
