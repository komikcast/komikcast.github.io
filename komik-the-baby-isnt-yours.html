Penjelasan: Verb berakhiran "y" diberi "s" diganti "ies". It's menunjukkan definisi, bukan suatu kepemilikan (its). Isnt menunjukkan pertanyaan, bukan suatu pernyataan. Huruf "a" sebelum joey tidak diberi "a" karena merupakan pernyataan umum.

d. Yes, the baby is

Penjelasan:

(?) To be + S + V-ing

Responses

☑ Yes, + S + to be[x] No, + S + to be + not

Contoh:

Are they eating bread now?

☑ Yes, they are[x] No, they are not

Is the baby drinking a milk now?

☑ Yes, the baby is[x] No, the baby is notD. Yes, the baby is
