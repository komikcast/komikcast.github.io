7. Still, the Queen was bothered. So bothered was she that the Queen decided to be rid of the girl, once and for all. tentukan action verb


Penyelesaian :

The action verb in the sentence "Still, the Queen was bothered. So bothered was she that the Queen decided to be rid of the girl, once and for all." is decided. It tells us what the Queen did, which was to decide to be rid of the girl.

The other verbs in the sentence are "was bothered" and "rid". "Was bothered" is a linking verb, which means it links the subject (the Queen) to the predicate adjective (bothered). "Rid" is a participle, which is a verb form that functions as an adjective or adverb. In this sentence, it is used as an adjective to modify the noun "girl".

Here is a breakdown of the sentence by verb type:

* Action verb: decided

* Linking verb: was bothered

* Participle: rid

Jawaban:

Did

Penjelasan: Did digunakan untuk menanyakan kejadian yang sudah terjadi di masa lampau atau yang telah terjadi ( digunakan sebagai past tense dalam pertanyaan). Karena 'did' sudah termasuk past tense, verb selanjutnya menggunakan verb 1 atau present tense (contohnya study, cook, play, bukan studied, cooked, played).

Contoh:

> Did your mom go to work yesterday? --> Benar

> Does your mom go to work yesterday? --> Salah, harus did (verb 2)

> Did your mom went to work yesterday? --> Salah, harus go (verb 1)

Untuk jawaban, verbnya harus menggunakan verb 2 karena kejadiannya telah terjadi, ditunjukkan dengan pemakaian "did" oleh yang bertanya
