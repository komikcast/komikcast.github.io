18. Matt is preparing to plant pineapples on his farm. His farm will have 1338 rows of pineapple with 45 plants in each rows. Matt estimates that his planting machine will be able to complete the planting process in 30 days. How many pineapples will the machine plant in a day?​


Jawaban :

Total pineapples = 1338 rows * 45 plants per row = 60,210 pineapples

Pineapples planted per day = Total pineapples / Number of days = 60,210 pineapples / 30 days = 2,007 pineapples per day

So, the machine will plant approximately 2,007 pineapples in a day.
