9. 26. The East End...the port of London.a. includesb. concludesc. concretesd. is lying​


Jawaban: A

Penjelasan:

The East End...the port of London.

East End (..) pelabuhan London.

a. includes = termasuk ✅

b. concludes = kesimpulan

c. concretes = erat, kuat

d. is lying​ = bohong

Alasannya memilih A

Karena terdapat pada teks di paragraph keempat, kalimat kedua:

“It includes the port of London.“

Kata “it” merujuk pada THE EAST END

The East End INCLUDES the port of London.

= East End termasuk pelabuhan London.

22. What is the climax of the strory? True Friends Once upon a time, there were two close friends who were walking through the forest together. They knew that anything dangerous can happen any time in the forest. So they promised each other that they would always be together in any case of danger. Suddenly, they saw a large bear getting closer toward them. One of them climbed a nearby tree at once. But unfortunately the other one did not know how to climb up the tree. So being led by his common sense, he lay down on the ground breathless and pretended to be a dead man. The bear came near the one who was lying on the ground. It smelt in his ears, and slowly left the place because the bears do not want to touch the dead creatures. After that, the friend on the tree came down and asked his friend that was on the ground, "Friend, what did the bear whisper into your ears?" The other friend replied, "Just now the bear advised me not to believe a false friend." Moral of the Story- A true friend in need is a friend indeed


Jawaban:

Apa klimaks dari cerita tersebut?

Teman sejati

Alkisah, ada dua orang sahabat yang sedang berjalan bersama di hutan. Mereka tahu bahwa segala sesuatu yang berbahaya dapat terjadi kapan saja di hutan. Jadi mereka berjanji satu sama lain bahwa mereka akan selalu bersama dalam keadaan bahaya apa pun.

Tiba-tiba, mereka melihat seekor beruang besar mendekat ke arah mereka. Salah satu dari mereka memanjat pohon terdekat sekaligus. Namun sayangnya yang satunya tidak tahu bagaimana cara memanjat pohon tersebut. Jadi dibimbing oleh akal sehatnya, dia berbaring di tanah dengan terengah-engah dan berpura-pura menjadi orang mati.

Beruang itu mendekati orang yang terbaring di tanah. Itu tercium di telinganya, dan perlahan meninggalkan tempat itu karena beruang tidak mau menyentuh makhluk mati. Setelah itu, teman di pohon itu turun dan bertanya kepada temannya yang ada di tanah, "Teman, apa yang dibisikkan beruang ke telingamu?" Teman yang lain menjawab, "Baru saja beruang itu menasihati saya untuk tidak mempercayai teman palsu."

Moral of the Story- Teman sejati yang membutuhkan adalah teman sejati.

23. how was the childhood of dewi sartika


Dewi Sartika was born on December 4th, 1884 in Cicalengka, Bandung. Her father named Raden Somanagara was a freedom fighter at that time. While her mother was Nyi Raden Rajapermas.

Her parents were fighting hard to send him to the School of the Netherlands. Although it is so contrary to traditional culture prevailing at the time.

 As a child, after school she often pretended to be a teacher while playing with her friends. After her father died, she lived with her uncle. She received an education of Sundanese culture while under his care, while her knowledge of Western culture was passed on to her from the wife of a resident assistant. In 1899, she moved to Bandung.
