36. A campsite is 9.41 miles from a point directly below the mountain top. If the angle of elevation is 12° from the camp to the top of the mountain, how high is the mountain?


angle of elevation along the horizontal distance (θ) = 12°

distance from the campsite a point directly below the mountain (adjacent) = 9.41 miles

the height of the mountain (opposite) = ?

Answer:

Tan θ = opposite / adjacent

Tan 12° = height / 9.41 miles

height = 9.41 miles x tan 12°

height = 9.41 miles x 0.2126

height = 2.000566 or 2 miles

The height of the mountain is 2 miles.
