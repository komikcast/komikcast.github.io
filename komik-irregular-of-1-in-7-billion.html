8. write down 10 examples of irregular verb​


IRREGULAR VERB

Irregular verb adalah kata kerja yang verb 1, verb 2 dan verb 3 nya berubah atau sama sekali tidak berubah. Kata kerja yang bentuk kedua dan ketiganya tidak mengikuti aturan penambahan -d dan -ed.

10 Examples of Irregular Verb

• Begin - Began - Begun → Memulai

• Break - Broke - Broken → Merusak

• Bring - Brought - Brought → Membawa

• Buy - Bought - Bought → Membeli

• Go - went - Gone → Pergi

• Write - Wrote - Written → Menulis

• Come - Came - Come → Datang

• Do - Did - Done → Melakukan

• Drink - Drank - Drunk → Minum

• Eat - Ate - Eaten → Makan

30. what are the different of regular and irregular?


- The regular verbs may be changed to the past tense by replacing the last vowel (a, e, i, o, u) with –d. To complete the word, a supporting verb is also added (has, have, had) in the case of the present perfect of past perfect tense.
- Those verbs which undergo a substantial change as they change forms, be it singular to plural or present to past or vice versa, fall into the category of irregular verbs. There is no other way but to memorize the changing tenses of irregular verbs.1. Regular Verbs are the verbs with usual simple past and past participle forms. Irregular Verbs refer to the verbs which have same or different present and past tense forms. (Regular verb merupakan kata kerja-kata kerja dengan bentuk V2 dan V3 yang sama. Irregular verb merujuk pada kata kerja yang pola V1-V2-V3-nya sama atau berbeda-beda satu sama lain.)

2. Regular Verbs have standard rules for conjugation. Irregular Verbs have special rules for conjugation. (Regular Verb memiliki aturan konjugasi standar. Irregular Verbs memiliki aturan konjugasi khusus.)

3. Examples(Contoh) =

Regular Verbs: stop-stopped-stopped. adjust-adjusted-adjusted. pull-pulled-pulled.

Irregular Verbs: cut-cut-cut. do-did-done. get-got-gotten.

37. suppose the banking system currently has $300 billion in reserves, the reserve requirement is 5 percent and excess reserves are $30 billion. What is the level of loans?


The level of loan is $5,400 billion.

Penjelasan:

The first step is to determine the multiplier using this formula

Multiplier = 1 Reserve Ratio

Let's enter the formula

Multiplier = 1 0.05

Multiplier = 20

The second step is to determine the mandatory reserve using this formula

Required backup = Current backup - excess backup

Let's enter the formula

Reserves needed = $300 billion - $30 billion

Reserves needed = $270 billion

Now let's calculate the loan rate using this formula

Loan rate = Required reserve × Multiplier

Let's enter the formula

Loan rate = $270 billion × 20

Loan rate = $5,400 billion

It concludes that the loan rate is $5,400 billion.
