(-) Subject + was/were + not + verb 1 + -ing

(?) Was/were + subject + verb 1 + -ing

3. passive voice The guards have protected the Queen.The Queen ... by the guards​


ANSWER

The Queen had been protected by the guards.

EXPLANATION

ACTIVE : The guards have protected the Queen.

PASSIVE : The Queen was protected the Queen.

Passive Voice adalah kebalikan dari Active Voice dimana subjeknya yang diberi tindakan sedangkan Passive Voice objeknya yang diberi tindakan.

Soal diatas Active Voicenya menggunakan pola kalimat Simple Past Tense, berikut adalah rumusnya :

• Passive Voice Simple Past Tense :

Subject + to be (was/were) + Verb3

Here's a list of ingredients and steps to make fried rice:

Ingredients:

2 cups of cooked rice

1 diced onion

2 diced carrots

1 diced green bell pepper

2 cloves of minced garlic

2 beaten eggs

2 tablespoons of oil (vegetable or canola oil)

Salt and pepper to taste

2 tablespoons of soy sauce

Steps:

Heat the oil in a large wok or pan over medium heat.

Add the garlic, onion, carrots, and green bell pepper, and cook until they are softened.

Push the vegetables to the sides of the pan and add the beaten eggs in the center. Scramble the eggs until they are cooked.

Add the cooked rice to the pan and mix everything together.

Season with salt, pepper, and soy sauce to taste.

Stir fry the rice for about 2-3 minutes or until the ingredients are evenly mixed and the rice is heated through.

Serve and enjoy!

34. Queen..... come in the forest​


Jawaban:

Queen didn't come in the forest. (simple past tense)

Queen doesn't come in the forest. (simple present tense)

Queen will come in the forest. (simple future tense)

Penjelasan:

Karena pada soal di atas tidak dilengkapi dengan perintah soal, ada tiga kemungkinan jawabannya.

Pada kalimat negatif simple past tense, subject + didn't + verb 1.

Pada kalimat negatif simple present tense, subject + doesn't + verb 1.

Pada kalimat positif simple future tense, subject + will + verb 1.
