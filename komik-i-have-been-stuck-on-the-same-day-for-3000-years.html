Apa perbedaan dari

- "I have worked at the Bayangkara  Hospital since 2017", dan

- "I have been working at the Bayangkara Hospital for two years"?

Materi: Present perfect tense

Rumus :

+) S + Have/ has + V3

-) S + Have/Has + not + V3

?) Have/has + S + V3

~Tense

Form of Present Perfect Tense :

S + have / has + Past Participle + Obj / Adv

(+) My parents have been married for 25 years

(-) My parents have not been married for 25 years

(?) Have My parents been married for 25 years ?


37. for years experts...the effect pt coaching and preparatory courses on test score a are examining b. had been examining c. have been examining d. having been examining


Jawaban:

C

Penjelasan:

Keterangan waktu 'for years' menunjukkan bahwa kalimat tersebut menggunakan Present Perfect Continuous using For.

Pola kalimatnya:

S + has/have + been + Ving

Subjek I, you, they, we menggunakan have, sedangkan subjek she, he, it menggunakan has.

Karena di soal subjek nya adalah experts, yang mana kalo ada noun berakhiran 's' menunjukkan bahwa orangnya lebih dari satu, maka jawaban yang benar adalah C.

Jawaban A salah, karena harusnya keterangannya now, just now, atau at the moment (Present Continuous)

Jawaban B salah, karena pola kalimatnya yang benar adalah had + past participle (Past Perfect)

Jawaban D salah, karena pola kalimatnya salah.
