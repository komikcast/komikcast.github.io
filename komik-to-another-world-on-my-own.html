Answer:

I protect my own traditional identity and deal with the people belonging to

another traditional identity by building mutual coordination and not considering our identity as the superior one.

Explanation:

On one hand we have a very forcible national and individual identity and whatever liberal, global and ideology we try and invent artificially, these identities are inscribed into our DNA and thus they can’t be erased or suppressed, as when we try to enforce it, we agonize violent rebound reactions as we can behold everywhere today.

On the other hand in the globally interdependent and integrated world, we required to fully build and integrate mutually complementing and mutually responsible global coordination in order to resolve our mounting, global problems and collectively survive as a species.
