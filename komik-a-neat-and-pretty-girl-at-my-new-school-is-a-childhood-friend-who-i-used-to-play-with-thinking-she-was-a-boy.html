Simple Present Tense:

1. He is man. He is not a boy.

2. She is a woman. She is not a girl.

3. He is a King. He is not a teacher.

4. She is a queen. She is not a teacher.

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb

(-) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + Adjective / Noun / Adverb
