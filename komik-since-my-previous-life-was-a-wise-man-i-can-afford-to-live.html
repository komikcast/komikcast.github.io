PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils?

Present Perfect Tense

Present perfect tense merupakan suatu tense yang menggambarkan suatu kejadian yang terjadi sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lalu tetapi masih berlanjut sampai sekarang. Tense ini terdiri dari gabungan aspek perfect (sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau) dan bingkai waktu present (masa sekarang).

Selain itu, tense ini juga bisa menggambarkan suatu kejadian yang dimulai dan berakhir di masa lampau tetapi efeknya masih dirasakan sampai saat sekarang. Lebih singkatnya, tense ini digunakan sebagai penghubung antara masa lalu dan masa sekarang.

Rumus Present Perfect Tense

Present perfect tense dibentuk dengan  auxiliary verb have atau has  dan past participle (verb 3). Have berpasangan dengan subjek  I, You, We, dan They sedangkan Has digunakan untuk subjek He, She, dan It.

Positif

I/You/We/They + have + verb 3

He/She/It + has + verb 3

Negatif

I/You/We/They + have + not + verb 3

He/She/It + has + not + verb 3

Interogatif

Have + I/You/We/They + verb 3?

Has + He/She/It + verb 3?

Contoh:

1. (+) They have lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time

   (-) They have not lived in Kuala Simpang for a long time

   (?)Have they lived in Jakarta for a long time?

2. (+) He has learned English for one  

    (-) He has not learned English for one year

    (?) Has he learned English for one year?

3. (+) I have written a poem

    (-) I have not written a poem

    (?) Have I written a poem?

4. (+) He has done his homework

   (-) He has not done his homework

   (?) Has he done his homework?

5. (+) They have played football

   (-) They have not  played football

   (?) Have  they played football?

6. (+) Laura has gone to Semarang by train

   (-) Laura has not gone to Semarang by train

    (?) Has Laura gone to Semarang by train?

7. (+) We have watched the concert together

   (-) We have not watched the concert together

   (?) Have we watched the concert together?

8. (+) Ammar has learnt English for five years

   (-) Ammar has not learnt English for five years

   (?) Has Ammar learnt English for five years?
