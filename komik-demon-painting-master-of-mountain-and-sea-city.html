19. What is the diferent of lake, river and sea


Jawaban:

Hi! I'd be happy to explain the differences between a lake, a river, and a sea.

1. Lake: A lake is a large body of water that is surrounded by land. It is usually freshwater, although there are some saltwater lakes as well. Lakes can be formed by various geological processes, such as tectonic activity, glacial activity, or volcanic activity. They are typically smaller than seas and oceans and can vary in size from small ponds to large bodies of water like the Great Lakes in North America.

2. River: A river is a natural flowing watercourse that is typically freshwater, although there are some rivers that contain brackish or saltwater. Rivers are formed by the accumulation of water from various sources, such as rainfall, melting snow, or underground springs. They flow downhill, usually towards a larger body of water, such as a lake, sea, or ocean. Rivers play a crucial role in the Earth's water cycle and are important for ecosystems, transportation, and human activities like irrigation and hydroelectric power generation.

3. Sea: A sea is a large body of saltwater that is partially enclosed by land. Seas are usually connected to oceans and are often partially or completely surrounded by landmasses. They are typically smaller than oceans and can vary in size. Seas can be formed by various geological processes, such as tectonic activity or the flooding of land by rising sea levels. They are important for marine ecosystems, transportation, trade, and recreational activities like swimming and fishing.

In summary, lakes are large bodies of freshwater surrounded by land, rivers are flowing watercourses that transport water from various sources, and seas are partially enclosed bodies of saltwater connected to oceans.

29. Jelaskan apa itu city of tomorrow, city of sorrow, metropolis, dan miseropolis


MISEROPOLIS : merupakan terminologi yang dipakai untuk menggambarkan kondisi suatu kota yang pengap, kotor, acak-acakan, melarat, tercemar dan menyedihkan, atau secara leksikal mengandung arti Kota yang Sakit (Ghetto)

METROPOLIS berasal dan bahasa Yunani, yaitu dari kata meter yang bermakna ‘ibu’ dan polis bermakna (1) ‘ibu kota’ atau ‘kota terpenting dalam negara atau wilayah’ dan (2) ‘kota yang menjadi pusat kegiatan perdagangan industri, dan pemerintahan.

CITY OF TOMORROW menggambarkan kota masa depan. Dimana di kota tersebut menggambarkan keteraturan, kenyamanan, kebahagiaan dan modernisasi.

CITY OF SORROW (sorrow artinya kesedihan) merupakan kota yang menggambarkan kesedihan dan kesemerawutan dan jauh dari kenyamanan.
