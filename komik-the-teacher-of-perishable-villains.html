1. Explain and give examples: 1. Perishable goods 2. Not perishable goods 3. Low of supply 4. Glut on the market 5. Elastic supply


1. Goods that must be used in a short period of time; goods that are able to perish, decay, or spoil rapidly.
ex: fresh meat, ripe fruits, seafood, etc.

2. Goods that have long shelf life and commonly don't require refrigeration.
ex: sugar, flour, rice, candies. etc.

3. Running out of supply; a supply shortage. 
ex: I am low on milk. I have to buy some soon.

4. Something that is on the market in a great abundance; a flood of something in the market; something that fill beyond the capacity of the market.
ex: It is the mango season. The mangoes are a glut on the market.

5. Supply of a good or service that increases or decreases as the price of an item goes up or down.
ex: The price of the stones has been high on the market nowadays. Therefore, there is an increase on the quantity of the stones supply.

Ps.
Mengasumsi bahwa semua pertanyaan menyangkut ekonomi, untuk nomor 3 mungkin seharusnya Law of Supply. Saya menjawab dengan pertanyaan Low of Supply yang mungkin seharusnya Low on Supply atau kekurangan barang. Saya ikutkan jawaban berikut JIKA ternyata pertanyaannya adalah Law of Supply:

4.5. Law of supply
Depiction of a producer behavior at the time of changes in the prices of goods or services. 
ex: When the price of a good rises, the supplier increases the supply in order to earn a profit because of higher prices.

3. the description of the teacher job​


Jawaban:

teacher's job is teaching, guiding, directing, training, assessing, and evaluating students.A teacher works in early childhood education, formal education, basic education and secondary education. Teachers must master competencies including pedagogic competence, personality competence, social competence, and professional competence. There are 2 type of work as a teacher, namely a permanent teacher who has a minimum status as a Candidate for Civil Servants, and has been assigned to a certain school as its parent agency and Temporary teachers who are often referred to as Honorary Teachers have the minimum status as Candidates for Civil Servants, and are paid below the minimum wage .Often they are paid less than the minimum salary that has been legally stipulated.

terjemahan:

Tugas guru adalah mengajar, membimbing, mengarahkan, melatih, menilai, dan mengevaluasi siswa. Guru harus menguasai kompetensi yang meliputi kompetensi pedagogik, kompetensi kepribadian, kompetensi sosial, dan kompetensi profesional. Pekerjaan sebagai guru ada 2 jenis, yaitu guru tetap yang berstatus minimal CPNS, telah ditugaskan pada sekolah tertentu sebagai instansi induknya dan Guru honorer yang sering disebut Guru Kehormatan mempunyai status minimumnya sebagai Calon Pegawai Negeri Sipil, dan dibayar di bawah upah minimum. 31. The average mass of 8 students is 35 kg. The average mass of 8 students and 1 teacher is 39 kg. What is the mass of the teacher? help<3


Average mass 8 student = 35kg

Average 8 student + 1 teacher = 39kg

Mass of the teacher?

Average 8 student formula :

(Summation mass of 8 student) ÷ 8 = 35

y ÷ 8 = 35

y = 35 x 8

y = 280

Average 8 student + 1 teacher formula :

(Summation mass of 8 student + mass teacher) ÷ 9 = 39

(280 + mass tecaher) = 39 x 9

280 + mass teacher = 351

mass teacher = 71

∴ mass of the teacher is 71 kg
