Jawaban:

Change into Active Voice and Passive Voice!

a. The team took the model out to Metro Tech commons.

Passive: The model was taken out to Metro Tech commons by the team.

b. I obtained a matter cartridge from the TA.

Passive: A matter cartridge from the TA was obtained by me.

c. The prom dress was hemmed by me.

Active: I hemmed the prom dress.

d. We mounted the model on the launch stand.

Passive: The model on the launch stand was mounted by us.

e. Albert put the stopper into the hole in the tank

Passive: The stopper was put into the hole in the tank by Albert.

f. When everything was in place, the rocket was launched.

Active: When everything was in place, we launched the rocket.

g. My brother broke the chain on my bike.

Passive: The chain on my bike was broken by my brother.

h. The package was advertised by the travel agent.

Active: The travel agent advertised the package.

i. The pencil on the floor was broken by him.

Active: He broke the pencil on the floor.

j. We snapped this cartridge into the model.

​Passive: This cartridge was snapped into the model.

Penjelasan:

Untuk mengubah dari kalimat pasif ke kalimat aktif, yang harus diperhatikan adalah

Auxiliary verb yang menyertai kata kerja. Jika kalimat terdapat auxiliary verb (was, were) maka kalimat tersebut adalah kalimat pasifPerhatikan tense yang ditunjukkan dari auxiliary verb tersebut. Karena pada kalimat-kalimat di atas menggunakan was dan were, maka tense yang digunakan adalah past tense.
