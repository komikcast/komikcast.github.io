9. A steel, with 120 meters of length and coefficient of linear expansion is 0.00002 / °C, is changed its temperature from 10°C to 60°C. The length expansion of the steel is...​


Untuk menghitung perluasan panjang baja, rumus yang digunakan adalah:

ΔL = α × L × ΔT

Dimana:

ΔL adalah perubahan panjangα adalah koefisien perluasan linearL adalah panjang awalΔT adalah perubahan suhu

Diketahui:

L = 120 meterα = 0,00002 / °CΔT = 60°C - 10°C = 50°C

Jawab:

ΔL = (0,00002 / °C) × 120 meter × 50°C

= 0,00002 × 120 × 50 meter

= 0,12 meter

Kesimpulan:

Oleh karena itu, perluasan panjang baja adalah 0,12 meter.

25. The density of steel is 7850 kgm Calculate themass of a steel sphere of radius 0.15 m. (Firstcalculate the volume of the sphere using theformula V = $rt and then use the density equation.)​


Penjelasan:

[tex]V sphere = \frac{4}{3} \pi {r}^{3} \\ = \frac{4}{3} \times 3.14 \times {(0.15)}^{3} = 0.01413 \: {m}^{3} [/tex]

[tex]ρ = \frac{m}{V} \\ 7850= \frac{m}{0.01413} \\ m = 7850 \times 0.01413 \\ = 110.9205 \: kg[/tex]

39. c) a sound wave of frequency 7.5 khz travels through a steel beam at a speed of 6100 m/s. (i) calculate the wavelength of this sound wave in the steel beam.


Jawaban:

0.8133 m

Penjelasan:

Known :

f : 7.5 kHz

v : 6100 m/s

Asked : wavelength (λ) = ?

Answer

λ = v/f = 6100 / (7500 Hz) = 61/75 = 0.8133 m
