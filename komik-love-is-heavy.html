38. bejamin is 3/4 as heavy as Ann.Siti is twice as heavy as Bejamin.if Ann's mass is 35 kg,find Siti's mass.


heavy = mass x gravity
mass = heavy / gravity
gravity = 10 m/s²

Ann's mass = 35 kg
Ann's heavy = 35 x 10 = 350 N

Bejamin's heavy = ¾ x Ann
= ¾ x 350
= 262,5 N

Siti's heavy = 2 x Bejamin's heavy
= 2 x 262,5
= 525 N

Siti's mass = Siti's heavy / gravity
= 525 / 10
= 52,5 kg
