2. The revolt of An Lushan dealt the Tang dynasty a blow from _____ it never fully recovered. (A) which (B) those (C) whom (D) whose


Jawaban:

The correct answer is (A) which.

The sentence is talking about the Tang dynasty being dealt a blow, and "which" refers back to that noun. "Those" refers to plural nouns, "whom" is used for objects of a verb or preposition, and "whose" is used to indicate possession. Therefore, (A) which is the correct choice.
