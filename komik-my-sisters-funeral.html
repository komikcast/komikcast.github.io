16. Present tenseMy sisters were drive a cars nowa) my sisters are drives a cars nowb) my sisters was driving a cars nowc) my sisters was droves a cars nowd) my sisters were drives a cars nowyang bisa jawab semoga nilainya baguskarena saya bingung s̮a̮m̮a̮ p̮r̮e̮s̮e̮n̮t̮ c̮o̮n̮t̮i̮n̮u̮e̮


"My sisters were drive a car now"

• Jika diubah ke bentuk present tense maka menjadi
✐ My sisters drive a car now

Formula → S + V1(s/es) + O/C

• Jika diubah ke bentuk present continuous tense menjadi
✐ My sisters are driving a car now

Formula → S + are/is + V1-ing + O/C
(tidak ada di opsi jawaban)
