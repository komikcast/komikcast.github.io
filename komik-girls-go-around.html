10. Which one is the correct sentence? A. Dion plans to go to a nice place around a lake in a small town in Sumatra. B. Dion plans to go to a nice place in small town in Sumatra around a lake. C. Around a lake Dion plans to go to a nice place in a small town in Sumatra. D. Dion plans to go to a nice place around a lake in Sumatra in a small town. E. Dion plans to go to a nice place in Sumatra in a small town around a lake.


Which one is the correct sentence?

A. Dion plans to go to a nice place around a lake in a small town in Sumatra.

Pembahasan

Untuk menjawab soal ini, perhatikan terjemahan pada setiap opsi.

A. Dion plans to go to a nice place around a lake in a small town in Sumatra. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di sekitar danau di sebuah kota kecil di Sumatra)

B. Dion plans to go to a nice place in small town in Sumatra around a lake. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di sebuah kota kecil di Sumatra sekitar sebuah danau)

C. Around a lake Dion plans to go to a nice place in a small town in Sumatra. (= Sekitar sebuah danau Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di sebuah kecil di Sumatra)

D. Dion plans to go to a nice place around a lake in Sumatra in a small town. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah sekitar sebuah danau di Sumatra di sebuah kota kecil)

E. Dion plans to go to a nice place in Sumatra in a small town around a lake. (= Dion berencana pergi ke sebuah tempat yang indah di Sumatra di sebuah kota kecil sekitar sebuah danau)

15. Ubah kalimat berikut ke dalam past tense "boys and girls come and go like mouth among the whisperings"


Jawaban=

"boys and girls came and went like mouth among the whisperings."

-semoga bener dan semoga membantu-

Bentuk past tense dari kalimat di atas adalah..

Present : Boys and girls come and go like mouth among the whisperings.

Past tense : Boys and girls came and went like mouth among the whisperings.

Rumus yang digunakan dalam past tense adalah :

S + V2 + O

Perubahan kata kerja

Come = Came = Come

Go = Went = Gone
