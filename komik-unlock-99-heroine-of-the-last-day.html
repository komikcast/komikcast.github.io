18. The average of the numbers 9, 99, 999, 9999, 99999, 999999, 9999999, 99999999 and 999999999 is ... .


Jawaban:

To find the average of a set of numbers, you add up all the numbers and then divide the sum by the total count of numbers. In this case, we have the numbers 9, 99, 999, 9999, 99999, 999999, 9999999, 99999999, and 999999999.

Let's calculate the average:

9 + 99 + 999 + 9999 + 99999 + 999999 + 9999999 + 99999999 + 999999999 = 1111111106

Since there are 9 numbers in total, we divide the sum by 9:

1111111106 ÷ 9 = 123456789.56

The average of the given numbers is approximately 123,456,789.56.

From clue c, we know that the sum of the middle two digits is 9 and is a multiple of 3. The only possible values for the middle two digits that satisfy this condition are 36 or 63.

So, we have the following possible codes:

4362

8634

Therefore, Sara has four possible codes to try: 4362, 4632, 8364, or 8634.
