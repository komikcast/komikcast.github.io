In the long run, wages and other input costs adjust so the economy always returns to the full-employment level of output. This means that the long-run aggregate supply (LRAS) curve is vertical at the full-employment output level (which is also called potential output).


22. Active : The teacher will return the students paper next week. Passive : ………………………………………………………………


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

Subject  = English

Grade = JHS / SHS

About = Active  / Passive

Passive voice adalah suatu kejadian yang dimana yang melakukan kejadian tersebut / pelaku adalah Object

Cth

He eats his lunch  ( Dia memakan makan siangnya )

S       V      O

Jadi jika diubah ke passive voice

His lunch is eaten by him ( Makan siangnya dimakan oleh dia )

Note :

kata " by " bole dihilangkan jika tidak perlu digunakan

Jika , Present Simple digunakan ( Passive Voice )

O + is/am/are + V3 + by + S

Jika , Past Simple menggunakan formula / rumus

O +  was / were + V3 + by + S

Jika , Present Continuous digunakan formula / rumus

O + is / am / are + be - ing + V3 + by + S

dan seterusnya , tinggal disesuaikan saja dgn rumus , bagaimana carannya utk meyesuaikannya

dimulai dari tenses Present Continuous yg menggunakan kata " be " jika Continuous maka menggunakan " be + V-ing " , jika Perfect maka menggunakan " be (V3 ) been " , jika Future Tense " be " tetap sama karena Future menggunakan V1

Answer

The teacher will return the paper next week

Temukan S , V , dan O dari kalimat berikut

S = The teacher

V = will return ( Future Simple )

O = the paper

Sesuaikan dengan bentuk Passive Voice

O + V + S

The paper will be returned by the teacher next week
