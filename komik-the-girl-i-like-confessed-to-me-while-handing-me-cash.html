3. A han1. The girl said to me, I have lost my dollThe girl told me that her doll​


Jawaban:

...has been lost.

Penjelasan:

The girl told me that her doll has been lost.

It's the passive form of a sentence with present perfect grammar.

Present perfect, passive =

I/we/you/they/(two objects or people) + HAVE + (v3) / been + (adjective)_______She/he/it/(an object or someone) + HAS + (v3) / been + (adjective)

example:

She + HAS + done her homework

Her doll + HAS + been lost.

Jawaban:

The correct description from the picture below is the man with orange shirt is on his bike while the girl with the purple shirt walks next to him (A).

___________________________Penjelasan:

Bisa dilihat di gambar, ada seorang pria memakai baju berwarna orange sedang bersepeda dan ada seorang wanita berbaju ungu berjalan di dekat orang tersebut.

Maka, pilihan yg tepat untuk mendeskripsikan hal tsb dalam bahasa inggris adalah pilihan A. Kita lihat pilihan lainnya:

B → salah, karena 'walks to him', seharusnya 'walks next to him'.

C → salah, karena 'walks next to her', seharusnya 'walks next to him', karena yg bersepeda adalah seorang laki laki.

D → salah, karena 'is in his bike', seharusnya 'is on his bike', karena bersepeda itu berarti menaiki sepeda, bukan berada di dalam sepeda (in his bike).

Jadi, bisa dilihat bahwa pilihan A lah yg tepat, karena di pilihan A:

- Menggunakan next to him (bkn her).

- Menggunakan 'is on his bike'.
