On the other hand, damselflies are smaller, have narrower wings, and hold their wings parallel to their bodies. They are also more delicate in terms of their strength and flying abilities. Both dragonflies and damselflies are skilled hunters that rely on their keen eyesight to catch their prey. Personification is the attribution of human characteristics to non-living objects.
