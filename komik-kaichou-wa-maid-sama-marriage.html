Dian: Thanks Budi! I decided to get married at the age of 19 because I think this is the best time for me to build a family and have children of my own.

Budi: I am so happy for you, Dian! I hope you build a happy family with your beloved husband!

Dian: Thank you for your support, Budi! My husband will always take good care of me!

Penjelasan:

Budi mengucapkan selamat kepada Dian yang baru saja menikah di usia muda, bahkan lebih muda daripada Budi. Budi merasa turut berbahagia tasa keputsan Dian tersebut dan berharap yang terbaik bagi Dian. Dian pun berterimakasih dan menyatakan bahwa suaminya akan selalu mengurus dan menjaganya dengan baik.
