(+)SUBJECT+TOBE PRESENT+VERB1+ING+OBJECT/ COMPLEMENT

(-)SUBJECT+TOBE PRESENT+NOT+VERB1+ING+OBJECT/ COMPLEMENT

(?) TOBE PRESENT+SUBJECT+VERB1+ING+OBJECT/ COMPLEMENT+?
