4. a) After being involved in an accident a year ago, Salim joined a jogging program. For the first week, the program trainer suggested him to jog slowly for 12 minutes each day. Then for each of the subsequent weeks, Salim has to increase the time by 6 minutes for his jogging. (i) Which week will Salim jog for 30 minutes?


Jawab:

Salim will jog for 30 minutes on the 4th week.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

1st week: 12 minutes

2nd week and on: + 6 minutes / week

Because the difference is constant (6 minutes), the number of minutes create an arithmetic sequence. Without using the arithmetic sequence formula, we can find:

[tex]\large\text{$\begin{array}{|r|c|c|c|c|}\cline{1-5}\rm Week & 1 & 2 & 3 & 4\\\cline{1-5}\rm Minutes & \bf 12 & \bf18 & \bf24 & \bf30\\\cline{1-5}\end{array}$}[/tex]

So, Salim will jog for 30 minutes on the 4th week.

Another Way (Using Formula)

The formula of arithmetic sequence is as follows:

[tex]\Large\text{$\displaystyle\boxed{a_n=a_1+(n-1)d}$}[/tex]

It's already known that:

aₙ = 30a₁ = 12d = 6

So, by occupying the formula and substituting the value of aₙ , a₁ and d:

[tex]\text{$30=12+(n-1)6$}\\\text{$30=12+6n-6$}\\\text{$30=6n+12-6\qquad\dots$(\sf arrange the right side)}\\\text{$30=6n+6$}\\\text{$24=6n\qquad\qquad\qquad\dots$(\sf substract both sides by 6)}\\\bf n = 4[/tex]

So, Salim will jog for 30 minutes on the 4th week.

11. what is the letter above?a. informing about seminarb. inviting someone to the seminarc. telling about past seminard. asking for permission to hold a seminare. thanking someone for being a speaker in the seminar​


Understanding Text - Formal Invitation

What is the letter above?

Jawaban: b. Inviting someone to the seminar

Penjelasan: “We would be greatly honored if you can spare some time from your schedule to attend the seminar”, dari kutipan tersebut dapat disimpulkan bahwa surat di atas adalah undangan formal.

Pembahasan

Invitation adalah sebuah teks yang bertujuan untuk mengundang seseorang atau lebih untuk menghadiri suatu acara atau mengajak seseorang untuk berpartisipasi dalam sebuah aktivitas.

Struktur invitation secara umum adalah:

1. Event title: tujuan dan jenis acara apa yang diselenggarakan.

2. Showing orientation: memberitahukan siapa saja yang diundang dalam acara tersebut.

3. Stating date and time: waktu, tanggal dan tempat acara.

4. Giving closing statement: menyatakan harapan dari kehadiran yang yang diundang.
