33. How do women's preferences for cosmetics and beauty care vary from country to country ?


Jawaban:

Tradition and beauty standards in each country influence what women choose for beauty care. For example in tradition, Chinese and Japanese women have been using face powder that is white in colour since BC. And modern asian women today still choose whitening beauty products like in the past because of tradition.

Beauty standards in asian countries are more fixed and strict, while in western countries like America, it has been more dynamic since there are so many varieties and complexions of people living in America today. So almost all asian women go for the same white glowing skin, soft and light makeup and skinny body, and for western women standards can be more variable from white to black, skinny to curvy and chubby body, etc


34. explain sound involved and how to produce sounds properly ?​


Speech is produced by bringing air from the lungs to the larynx (respiration), where the vocal folds may be held open to allow the air to pass through or may vibrate to make a sound (phonation). The airflow from the lungs is then shaped by the articulators in the mouth and nose (articulation).
