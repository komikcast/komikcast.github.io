27. positif from1.I am not happy now2.The child does not cry hard3.Does your father do his job well4.Mrs. Andara does not have a son.5.Does Shamira watch Korean Drama​


Jawaban:

Positive form of Simple Present TenseI am not happy now.
=> I am happy now.The child does not cry hard.
=> The child cries hard.Does your father do his job well?
=> Your father does his job well.Mrs. Andara does not have a son.
=> Mrs. Andara has a son.Does Shamira watch Korean Drama​.
=> Shamira watches Korean Drama.

Penjelasan:

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSEPositive

S + Verb 1 + Compliment or Object.

Negative

S + Do/Does + not + Verb 1 + Compliment or Object.

Interrogative

Do/Does + S + Verb 1 + Compliment or Object?

NB: Subjek tunggal pada verbnya ditambahkan imbuhan -s/-es.

38. apa kah itu game doom ? apa dampak dari game doom?
