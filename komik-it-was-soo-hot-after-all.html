1. was it hot last week​


Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense:

(+) It was hot last week.

(-) It was not hot last week.

(?) Was it hot last week?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Nominal dari Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple Past Tense:

Nominal: (+) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + Adjective / Noun / Adverb + Time Signal (last week)

(-) Subject + Tobe (was/were) + not + Adjective / Noun / Adverb + Time Signal (last week)

(?) Tobe (was/were) + Subject + Adjective / Noun / Adverb + Time Signal (last week) + ?

Semoga membantu ya.
