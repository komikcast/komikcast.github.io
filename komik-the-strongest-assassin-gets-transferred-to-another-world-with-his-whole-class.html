14. Mrs Maryam took 1/2 hour to travel 15km from his home to his office. She took another 1/4 hour to travel 6km from her office to the market. a) Find the total distance that Mrs Maryam travelled. b) Find the total time taken for the whole journey. c) What was the average speed for the whole journey in km/h.


Find the total distance that Mrs.Maryam travelled.

Total distance that Mrs.Maryam travelled = 15 km + 6 km

Total distance that Mrs.Maryam travelled = 21 km

Find the total time taken for the whole journey.

Total time taken for the whole journey = ½ hour + ¼ hour

Total time taken for the whole journey = [tex]( \frac{2}{4} + \frac{1}{4})hour[/tex]

Total time taken for the whole journey = ¾ hour or 45 minutes.

What was the average speed for the whole journey in km/h?

First travel, she took ½ hour to travel 15 km, it means the speed is 15 km/½h or 30 km/h.Second travel, she took ¼ hour to travel 6 km, it means the speed is 6 km/¼h or 24 km/h.

So, the average speed for the whole journey is :

[tex] = \frac{30 \: km \: per \: hour + 24 \: km \: per \: hour}{2} \\ = \frac{54 \: km \: per \: hour}{2} [/tex]

= 27 km/h

A Whole New World ini bukan lagu yang terbilang baru sebab sudah diperdengarkan sejak tahun 1992 ketika film Aladdin pertama kali rilis. Saat itu penyanyi lagu ini adalah LEA SALONGA dan BRAD KANE. Lea Salonga sendiri adalah penyanyi legendaris dari Filipina yang masih eksis berkarir hingga saat ini.

Lagu A Whole New World ini sangat populer sehingga berbagai versi dari penyanyi berbeda pun bertebaran dan siap dinikmati. Pada tahun 2019 ini, versi Zayn Malik yang berpadu suara dengan Zhavia cukup memikat banyak pendengar dan disebut-sebut sebagai versi cover terbaik.
