17. Will - a roundabout - see - at the end - you - of the road.


Jawaban:

You will see a roundabout at the end of the road.

Sedikit membingungkan, karena huruf "W" di kata "Will" itu dikapitalisasi, yang bisa membuat jawabannya "Will you see a roundabout at the end of the road?", yang merupakan question (pertanyaan) dan bukan statement (pernyataan) atau sebuah answer (jawaban) dari suatu pertanyaan.

Jawaban yang betul adalah "You will see a roundabout at the end of the road.", dan bukan "Will you see a roundabout at the end of the road?".

Jika jawaban salah, maaf.

29. If the length of a road in a map is 7 cm, and the scale used in the map is 1 : 35 000, find theactual length of the road.​


Dik:

Jarak pada peta = 7cm

Skala = 1 : 35.000

Dit:

Jarak sebenarnya?

Jawab:

Jarak sebenarnya

= Jarak pada peta ÷ skala

= 7cm ÷ 1/35.000

= 7cm × 35.000/1

= 245.000cm

= 0,00245km

If the length of a road in a map is 7 cm, and the scale used in the map is 1 : 35.000

find the actual length of the road!

answer:

scale = length of a road in a map/ length of a road in actual

= 1/35.000 = 7/ length of a road in the actual

= length of a road in the actual = 7 × 35.000

= 245.000 cm

= 2,45 km

so, length of a road in the actual is 2,45 km
