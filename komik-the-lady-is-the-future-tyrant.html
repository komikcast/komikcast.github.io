The passive voice is The old lady was bitten by the dog

Penjelasan:

Aktif = The dog bit the old lady → (V2 Past tense)

Passive = S2 + was/were + V3 + by + S1

S2 = Subject ke-2

S1 = Subject ke-1

was/were = to be

V3 = Verb 3

Karena, passive voice menggunakan V3 sebagai kata kerjanya dan to be tergantung pada tense masing-masing kalimat.

penjelasan:

Bentuk kalimat negatif ditandai dengan adanya kata tidak/bukan (NOT), yang menunjukan bahwa kalimat tersebut berlawanan dari kalimat positif.

Sehingga kalimat negatif dari soal adalah: wanita itu tidak memakai sepasang sepatu olahraga, kemudian diterjemahkan ke dalam bahasa inggris menjadi: the lady is not wearing a pair of sporty shoes, kemudian kalimat ini dapat disingkat menjadi the lady isn't wearing a pair of sporty shoes.

Jadi jawaban yang tepat adalah c. The lady isn't wearing a pair of sporty shoes.

31. what is the concept of future passive


umm... you mean, Future Passive Voice?

The format for Future Passive Voice is :

1. Future tense with 'will' :

Format : Subject + will be + Verb -3

ex : The email will be send by her.

2. Future tense with 'Be going to' :

Format : Subject + TO BE Going to + be Verb -3

Ex : The cake is going to be eaten by them.

3. Future Continuos tense

Format : Subject + will be + being Verb -3

Ex : The email will be being written by her.

4. Future Ferfect tense

Format : Subject + will + have been + Verb -3

Ex : The email will have been written by her.
