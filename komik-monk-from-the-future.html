9. sari will be being taught by her English teacher when her mother arrived from the office what tense ia used on the passive voice above?a. the simple future yg tenceb. the future continuous tensec. the future perfect tensed. the future continuous tense​


Tense yang digunakan untuk kalimat pasif tersebut adalah future continuous tense. Ini adalah pilihan jawaban b atau d. Kata kunci dalam kalimat yang menunjukkan penggunaan tense ini adalah "will be being taught ".

Pembahasan

Kalimat pasif merupakan kebalikan dari kalimat aktif. Dalam kalimat aktif, subjek menjadi pelaku terhadap objek. Sementara itu dalam kalimat pasif, subjek bertindak seolah-olah sebagai objeknya.

Contoh kalimat aktif dan pasif dalam bahasa Inggris (beserta terjemahan bahasa Indonesianya) adalah sebagai berikut.

Ani likes cats >< Cats are liked by Ani  (Ani suka kucing >< Kucing disukai oleh Ani)She is going to make a sandwich >< A sandwich is going to be made by her  (Ia akan membuat roti isi >< roti isi akan dibuat olehnya)dll.

Future continuous tense adalah penekanan kata kerja untuk waktu yang akan berlangsung pada masa yang akan datang. Ia meliputi berbagai peristiwa yang akan sedang berlangsung atau akan sedang terjadi.

Struktur kalimat aktif dengan penekanan future continuous adalah:

Subject + will + be + Verb-ing + Object.

Di sisi lain, struktur kalimat pasif dengan penekanan future continuous adalah:

Subject + will + be + being + Verb 3 + by + Object.

Explanation:

Kelas : IX

Jenjang : Sekolah Menengah Pertama

Keyword : Procedural text

PROCEDURAL TEXTTeks ProsedurDefinition

A procedural text instructs your audience on how to complete a specific task.  Generally, this then falls into two categories, how to make something and how to do something.

Purpose

The purpose of a procedural text is to provide sequenced information or directions so that people can successfully perform activities in safe, efficient and appropriate ways.

Forms of Procedural Texts

Contoh jenis teks prosedur

Directions

How do I get somewhere? Very specific instructions including location names and titles. Formal language is required and the addition of a map will make your instructions so much easier to understand.

Instructions

How do I do something? Your language must meet the needs of your audience and you may need to include a diagram if there are complex elements to complete.

Recipes

How do I cook something? Recipes are a universal text. There is a very clear expectation of the audience so never stray from the essentials. Ingredients, method and a few visuals are essential.

Manuals

How do I operate this? Are there any warnings I need to be aware of before proceeding? Be very specific in your explanation.

General Structure of Procedural Texts

Struktur general dari teks prosedur

Aim/Goal (Tujuan)

Usually located in the title, which shows us the goal of this text.

Example : To tell people how to cook lasagna

Ingredients/Materials

Ingredients, materials or tools required in the process of following the procedural text.

Example : The user is required to prepare salt, pepper and other ingredients to cook a portion of lasagna.

Steps/Methods

Steps and methods used in order to successfully attain the desired results of the procedure.

32. Make a dialogue 3 people on theme '' plan in the future after graduating from shs '' Add sentence: simple future, future continuous, future perfect.


Dialog 3 orang dengan tema "Plan in the Future After Graduating from Senior High School" dengan menggunakan simple future, future continuous dan future perfect adalah dengan memahami penggunaan tenses tersebut. Berikut ini adalah penjelasannya.


Simple future tense merupakan kalimat yang digunakan untuk menunjukan kejadian di masa depan atau yang belum terjadi.

Rumus:

S + will + verb 1 + OS + to be + going to + verb 1 + O

Contoh:

My mother is going to pick me up tomorrow after school.I will call you back.

Future continuous merupakan tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung, namun pada kondisi yang akan datang atau masa depan. Fungsinya adalah untuk menyatakan sebuah kegiatan yang akan dilakukan pada suatu waktu di masa mendatang dengan waktu yang spesifik.

Rumus:

S + will be + verb-ing

Contoh:

I will be baking cupcakes when you come tonightMy mom will be visiting you tomorrow at 10AM

Future perfect merupakan kata kerja yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan sebuah kegiatan yang akan sudah selesai pada suatu waktu di masa depan.

Rumus:

S + will + have + past participle (verb 3)

Contoh:

The kids will have come when you arrive at 10.I will have slept when my parents go to the party.
Pembahasan

Plan in the Future After Graduating From Senior High School


Anna: Hey guys, what are your plans after graduating from high school?

Luna: I'm going to continue my study at Universitas Gajah Mada in Yogyakarta.

Klara: I don't know yet. Maybe I'm going to some local university too in Kuala Lumpur. What about you Anna?

Anna: I am going for a Euro Trip. Maybe I will also look for part-time job while traveling. Still a plan, though.

Luna: That sounds fun!

Klara: Yeah indeed. Well hopefully if everything goes well, I will be visiting you Anna in Europe. I have never been there!

Anna: Oh my God, yes please come there! Hopefully I will have earned decent amount of money by the end of next year, so we can hangout!

Pembahasan Simple Future Tense

Future Tense adalah tenses yang menyatakan sebuah aksi atau tindakan akan dilakukan atau akan terjadi di masa depan. Future Tense bisa dikatakan sebagai sebuah rencana untuk dilakukan di masa depan.

Rumus Future Tense

1. Verbal Sentence

(+) S + Will + V1 + Complement

(-) S + Will + not + V1 + Complement

(?) Will + S + V1 + Complement?

2. Nominal Sentence

(+) S + will be + complement

(-) S + Will not be + complement

(?) will + S + be + complement?

39. 2.complete the following sentences white the correct verb from of future simple tender (no 1-5),future continuous(tenar 6-10)and future Perfect tender (11-15)


My sister and I will travel to Sumatra Island.I will visit my uncle in Sumbawa Island.They will not come to her party tonight.Will you register yourself in a university tomorrow?She will be happy if you give  her a rose.Tomorrow we will be leaving the town.My borther will be listening to the radio.He will be reading a book tonight.Will you be walking home this evening?You will be waiting here when the plane arrive tonight.I will have finished my exams by then, so we can go out.We will have been in Bandung  for two years next week.Will you have finished the assignment by the deadline?Will they have arrived by the time it gets dark?They will have come at six this evening.

Pembahasan

Simple Future Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan peristiwa di masa depan. Rumus: Subject + will + object + adverb of time.

Future Continuos Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan peristiwa yang sedang berlangsung di masa depan. Rumus: Subject + will + be + V1+ing + adverb of time.

Future Perfect Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan peristiwa yang akan, telah, dan masih berlangsung di masa depan. Rumus: Subject + will + have + V3 + object + adverb of time.
