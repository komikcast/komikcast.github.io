25. the doctor came to my class.the negative form of the sentence above is..​


Jawaban:

The doctor didn't ( did not ) come to my class

Penjelasan:

Subject = English

Grade = JHS / SHS

About = Past Tense

The doctor came to my class

Ubah ke kalimat negatif

Cara mengubah ke kalimat negatif

1 . Perhatikan dulu verb dalam bentuk apa

2. Ubahlah ke dalam bentuk kalimat negatif ( dilihat dari verb )

1. The doctor came to my class

Verb = came , V1 came adalah come . Jadi bisa diketahui bahwa kata " came " itu bisa masuk ke dalam tenses Past Tense

2. Formula Past Tense

( + ) S + V2 + O

( - ) S + did + not + V1 +  O

( ? ) Did + S + V1 + O + ?
