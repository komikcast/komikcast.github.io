24. Text:Angel FallsAngel Falls is located in the Guayana Highlands, and it is just one of the five great adventureregions in Venezuela. The water come in a free fall of 979 meters from the Churum River hovering of theedge of the "Auyantepuy" Table Mountain. This is the largest Tepuy (table mountain) in Canaima,Venezuela. This height makes it the highest waterfall in the world. Angel Falls is 15 times higher thanNiagara Falls with its total of approximately 52 meters.This fall is named for Jimmy Angel, an adventurous pilot fromMissouri, United States, who flew to the air circus Lindberg. JamesCrawford Angel (Jimmy Angel) is a modem legend. He saw thewaterfall for the first time in 1933 with his partner while searching forthe legendary McCracken River of Gold or the Golden CityJames returned again in 1937 with his wife and his friend,Gustavo Henry Gardener, where they had an emergency landing on​


Jawaban:

Air Terjun Angel

Air terjun angel terletak di Dataran Tinggi Guayana, dan itu hanya salah satu dari lima wilayah petualangan hebat di Venezuela. Air terjun bebas setinggi 979 meter dari Sungai Churum yang melayang di tepi Gunung Meja "Auyantepuy". Ini adalah Tepuy (gunung meja) terbesar di Canaima, Venezuela. Ketinggian ini menjadikannya air terjun tertinggi di dunia. Air Terjun Angel 15 kali lebih tinggi dari Air Terjun Niagara dengan total ketinggian kurang lebih 52 meter. Air terjun ini dinamai Jimmy Angel, seorang pilot petualang dari Missouri, Amerika Serikat, yang terbang ke sirkus udara Lindberg. James Crawford Angel (Jimmy Angel) adalah legenda modern. Dia melihat air terjun untuk pertama kalinya pada tahun 1933 bersama rekannya saat mencari Sungai Emas atau Kota Emas McCracken yang legendaris. James kembali lagi pada tahun 1937 bersama istri dan temannya, Gustavo Henry Gardener, di mana mereka melakukan pendaratan darurat.

Penjelasan:

Angel Falls

Angel Falls is located in the Guayana Highlands, and it is just one of the five great adventure regions in Venezuela. The water come in a free fall of 979 meters from the Churum River hovering of the edge of the "Auyantepuy" Table Mountain. This is the largest Tepuy (table mountain) in Canaima, Venezuela. This height makes it the highest waterfall in the world. Angel Falls is 15 times higher than Niagara Falls with its total of approximately 52 meters.This fall is named for Jimmy Angel, an adventurous pilot from Missouri, United States, who flew to the air circus Lindberg. James Crawford Angel (Jimmy Angel) is a modem legend. He saw the waterfall for the first time in 1933 with his partner while searching for the legendary McCracken River of Gold or the Golden City. James returned again in 1937 with his wife and his friend, Gustavo Henry Gardener, where they had an emergency landing on.
