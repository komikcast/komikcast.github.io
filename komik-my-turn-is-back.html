25. +) my father is gardening in the back yard (-,?)(-)(?)​


(+) My Father is gardening in the back yard

(-) My Father is not gardening in the back yard

(?) Is My Father gardening in the back yard ?

Semoga Bermanfaat ^_^

Jawaban:

(+) My father is gardening in the back yard

(-) My father is not gardening in the back yard

(?) Is my father gardening in the back yard?

Penjelasan:

Termasuk Present Continuous Tense

Pengertian Present Continuous Tense

Tense ini digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu perbuatan atau peristiwa yang sedang berlangsung dan belum selesai di waktu sekarang.

Rumus/Formula:

(+) S + to be (am, are, is) + V1(ing) + ....

(-) S + to be (am, are, is) + not + V1(ing) + ....

(?) To be (am, are, is) + S + V1(ing) + ....

------

Note : "....." merupakan pelengkap kalimat

Just remember this:

Pronoun possessive:

We use pronouns to refer ‘belonging’. There are two types:  

1. possessive determiners: we use it before a noun.  

2. possessive pronouns: we use it in place of a noun:

- possessive determiner: my, your, his, her, its, our, their

- possessive pronoun: mine, yours, his, hers, its, ours, theirs
