PEMBAHASANPresent Tense

Digunakan utk menunjukkan Peristiwa yg terjadi di masa sekarang (Present), Kegiatan berulang/rutin, kebiasaan (habitual) dan utk menyatakan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Rumus Present Tense

(+) S + Be (is/am/are) + Complement

(-) S + Be (is/am/are) + not + Complement

(?) Be (is/am/are) + S + complement?

Catatan

Subject + Be

I + amyou, we, they + areHe, She, it, the hospital + is ✔️

KESIMPULAN

(+) the hospital is behind my school

(-) the hospital is not behind my school

(?) is the hospital behind my school?
