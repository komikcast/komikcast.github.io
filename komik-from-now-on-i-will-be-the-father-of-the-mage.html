1. 6 years ago the total age of the father and mother was 11 times the difference. Now the age of the father is 7 out of 6 of the age of the mother. Five years from now, the age of the father and the age of the mother will be respectively


Jawab:

5 yrs from now f = 47 and m = 41

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

f-6 + m-6 = 11(f -m)

f + m = 11f - 11m + 12

7/6 m + m = 11 (7/6 m) - 11m + 12

13/6 m = 77/6 m - 11m + 12

13/6 m = 77/6 m - 66/6 m + 12

13/6 m = 11/6 m + 12

2/6 m = 12

m = 36 so f = 42

f = 7/6 x m (this equation has been substituted above, since line 3)

5 yrs from now f = 47 and m = 41

2. All rivers flow from the up stream to the down stream.  

=>Ini merupakan kalimat fakta jadi merupakan bagian dari Simple Present Tense.Jika kalimat apapun yang menyatakan fakta misalnya Matahari terbenam dari Timur ke Barat atau Barat Ke Timur ini dianggap sebagai kalimat fakta dari Simple Present Tense.

3. My brother was a senior high school student last year. now, he is a collage student.  

=>Jika dalam pertanyaann ada kata "BE" itu merujuk pada penggunaan Was,Were.Am itu digunakan jika subjeknya adalah "I,He,She,It" sedangkan Were itu digunakan pada subjek They,You,We.Ini juga termasuk dalam bagian penggunaan WAS,WERE(Simple Past Tense)
