6. It was the most strongest animal and land kalimat tersebut benar atau salah?


Jawaban:

bukan Karena itu g nyambung

Jawaban:

salah.

It was/is the strongest animal on land.

Penjelasan:

penggunaan kata strongest tidak perlu menggunakan most di depannya. biasanya, kata adjektif/adjective (kata sifat) yang suku kata 1-2 menggunakan -est, dan kata yang suku kata lebih dari 2 menggunakan most tanpa -est.

contoh:

pret.ty: prettiest

bea.u.ti.ful: most beautiful

penggunaan kata and yang berarti 'dan' salah. Yang seharusnya digunakan adalah on (di).

Untuk kata 'was' bisa benar ataupun salah. Jika binatang yang disebut masih ada, menggunakan kata 'is'. Jika binatang tersebut sudah punah, contohnya dinosaurus, menggunakan 'was'.

34. Why has digital literacy become the vital competency that students need to master before graduating?


Digital literacy has become a vital competency that students need to master before graduating due to several reasons:

Ubiquity of Technology: Technology has become an integral part of our daily lives. It is present in almost every aspect of society, from education and work to communication and entertainment. In order to thrive in the digital age, students need to be proficient with technology and understand how to effectively and responsibly use digital tools.

Future Workforce Demands: The job market is continually evolving, and digital skills are in high demand across various industries. Employers are increasingly seeking candidates who have a strong foundation in digital literacy. Proficiency in areas such as coding, data analysis, digital communication, and online collaboration are considered essential for many job roles.

Access to Information: The internet has democratized access to information. It allows individuals to explore a vast array of knowledge and resources. However, to navigate and evaluate this abundance of information effectively, students need digital literacy skills. They must be able to critically analyze and verify the credibility of sources, differentiate between reliable and misleading information, and understand how to ethically and responsibly use and share digital content.

Communication and Collaboration: Digital tools have revolutionized communication and collaboration processes. Students need digital literacy skills to effectively communicate and collaborate online, whether through email, social media platforms, video conferencing, or collaborative online platforms. Understanding digital etiquette, online security, and privacy issues are essential in maintaining effective and responsible digital interactions.

Empowerment and Innovation: Digital literacy empowers students to be creators rather than just consumers of digital content. Proficiency in digital tools and creative platforms allows students to express their ideas, solve problems, and innovate. Digital literacy enables students to engage in digital storytelling, content creation, coding, and designing, thus fostering creativity and innovation.

Subject : English

Category : Reading

Level : SHS

Keywords : recount, retell, social function, generic structure, language features


ANSWER :

The text below is a recount text.


The social function of a recount text is to retell a series of events in order to give information or to amuse the readers.


The generic stucture of a recount is :
1. orientation → introduction of the participants, the setting place and and the setting of time.
2. events → description of the events in a linear progression.
3. re-orientation → optional, personal comments from the writer regarding the story.


The language features of a recount :
1. using the first person POV (I, me, my, etc)
2. using chronological connectors such as : first, then, finally, etc.
3. using action verbs such as : go, take, etc
4. using the past simple tense
