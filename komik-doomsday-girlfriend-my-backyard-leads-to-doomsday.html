11. I'm writing a letter to my girlfriend, menggunakan present continuos


Kalimat "I'm writing a letter to my girlfriend" menggunakan bentuk present continious tense. Bentuk kalimat negatif dan kalimat tanyanya adalah:

(-) I am not writing a letter to my girlfriend.

(?) Am I writing a letter to my girlfriend?

Pembahasan

Present continious tense digunakan untuk kegiatan atau aktivitas yang sedang berlangsung. Rumusnya:

(+) S + Tobe (am,is,are) + Ving + O/C

(-) S + Tobe (am,is,are) + Not + Ving + O/C

(?) Tobe (am,is,are) + S + Ving + O/C + ?

Contohnya:

(+) She is cooking chicken soup.

(-) She is not cooking chicken soup.

(?) Is She cooking chicken soup?

40. my dogs run in the backyard(+)(-)(?)​


Jawaban:

+: my dogs run in the backyard

-: my dogs don't run in the backyard

? : in the backyard my dogs run?

Jawaban

(+) My dogs run in the backyard.

(-) My dogs don't run in the backyard.

(?) Do my dogs run in the backyard?

Penjelasan

Tense yang digunakan adalah simple present tense (menunjukkan aksi sekarang, kebiasaan, atau fakta umum)

Pola yang digunakan adalah:

(+) S + V1 (-) S + do/does + not + V1(?) Do + S + V1 + ...?

Contoh:

(+) I + eat + an egg every morning.(+) She + cooks + every night.(-) I + do + not + eat + an egg every morning.(-) She + does + not + cook + every night.Do + I + eat + an egg every morning?Does + she + cook + every night?

Catatan:

Gunakan do pada kalimat negatif dan interogatif subjek I/you/we/theyGunakan does pada kalimat negatif dan interogatif subjek he/she/itDo/does+not bisa disingkat menjadi don't atau doesn't.Jawaban menggunakan do/don't  dikarenakan subjek plural               (my dogs ➔ they)
