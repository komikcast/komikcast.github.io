3. What's your opinion about living in the village


Giving Opinion

Living in the Village

In my opinion, living in the countryside is a great opportunity and  has a lot of advantages. Personally, I would love in the village and I would like to present the reasons behind it.

First of all, undoubtedly, in the countryside, the air is way less polluted comparing with the city and the problem of traffic jam is not existing. It’s very important thing to be able to keep a fresh breath cause nowadays, many people suffers from lungs diseases caused by polluted air.

Second reason is picturesque landscape – there is a lot of trees, flowers, various kinds of plants and usually small building. In the village we are able to take a rest from  huge blocks of flats or  traffic jams which often exist in the city. It’s got a positive impact on human mental side.

Third reason is a safety. Living in the village, we’re not anonymous, like in the city. Everybody knows everybody, so the potential chance for being a victim of a burglary is significantly lower than in the urban area.

To sum up, I think that living in the village got so many advantages that it is almost impossible to present all of them. Beautiful landscape, fresh air, space, it’s crucial for me that’s why I would love to live in the village.

Pembahasan

Giving opinion adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk mengekspresikan pendapat atau pemikiran terhadap suatu hal yang kita rasakan atau percayai. Hal ini dapat mengenai isu, orang lain atau yang lainnya.

Ketika kita ingin menyampaikan pendapat, ada beberapa frasa yang umum digunakan. Berikut diantaranya:

Expressing strong opinion

I’m absolutely convinced….It’s obvious to me…..As far as I’m concerned….

Expressing a cautious or reluctant opinion

I suppose that…..As far as I understand it, …….It seems to me that ……I must admit that I’m not sure ……

Expressing objective opinion

Apparently…..I’ve heard that…..The research seems to suggest…..

Expressing subjective opinion

In my experience……I don’t know about other people, but I can say…….What I’ve found is…..

15. The visit my uncle in the village. (+)....... ..... (-) .............


Simple Present Tense (+) They visit my uncle in the village.(-) They do not visit my uncle in the village.(?) Do They visit my uncle in the village?

PEMBAHASAN Simple Present Tense

➡ Digunakan utk menunjukkan Peristiwa yg terjadi di masa sekarang (Present), Kegiatan berulang/rutin, kebiasaan (habitual) dan utk menyatakan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Rumus Present Tense

* (+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C

* (-) S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O/C

* (?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O/C?

Catatan

Subject + Auxiliary

➡ I, you, we, they + do

➡ He, She, it + does

34. A railway line connects the villagewith the town. What is the passiveform of this sentence?​


Jawaban:

A railway line connects the village with the town.

Passive: The village is connected with the town by a railway line.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat di atas menggunakan simple present tense.

Perhatikan cara mengubah kalimat di atas menjadi kalimat pasif.

A railway line (subject) connects (verb) the village (object) with the town (complement).

Passive: Object + am/is/are + verb 3 + by subject.

The village (object) + is + connected (verb 3) + with the town + by a railway line (subject)
