29. Change this sentence into the negative sentence. "The earth revolves around the Sun." a. The earth is not revolves around the Sun. b. The earth does not revolves around the Sun. c. The earth do not revolves around the Sun. d. The earth does not revolve around the Sun.​


Answer:

(+) The earth revolves around the Sun

(-) The earth does not (doesn't) revolve around the Sun

(?) Does the earth revolve around the Sun?

Present simple tense :

(+) S + V1

(-) S + do (don't)/does not (doesn't) + V1

(?) Do/Does + S + V1?

I/you/they/we = do

He/she/it = does

Maaf jika ada yang salah
