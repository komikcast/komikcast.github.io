10. how are the threads on the outside of the cocoon?​


Jawaban:

How are the threads on the outside of the cocoon ……..

A. Fine.

B. Soft.

C. Rough.

D. Strong.

E. Smooth

Penjelasan:

Silkworms

Silkworms live for only two or three days after laying eggs. About 36,000 to 50,000 eggs are laid, and these are carefully stored at the silkworm farm until they are ready to hatch. The eggs hatch into caterpillars, which feed on mulberry leaves. Soon, the caterpillars are ready to spin their cocoons. Not all caterpillars can spin silk cocoons. Only the caterpillars of a silkworm moth known as ‘Bombyx mari’ can do such spinning. This caterpillar has special glands which secrete liquid silk through its lower lip. The liquid produced later hardens to form tine strands.

The caterpillar makes its cocoons using these strands. The threads on the outside of the cocoon are rough, while those inside are soft and smooth.

Some fully-spun cocoons are heated. This kills the pupa inside. The cocoons are then put into hot water to loosen the fine threads. Finally, these threads are reeled off the cocoons.

The length of unbroken thread produced by a single cocoon measures about one-and-a-half kilometers. Being twisted together several of these threads make single woven materials.

19. There was a phone on the floor, so I picked it up and said, “… phone is this?”


Jawaban:

Whose

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran Possessive Pronoun (kata ganti kepemilikan) yaitu "Whose" dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Karena ada telpon di lantai, kemudian kuambil telepon tersebut dan bertanya, "milik siapakah (Whose?)" telepon ini?

Possessive Pronoun atau kata ganti kepemilikan yang berupa "Whose?" digunakan saat kamu bertanya (atau memberi tahu) milik siapa sesuatu itu. Contohnya: Whose phone is this?

21. What is the moral valve of the text A BRAVE GIRL


Jawaban:

The moral value of the story "A Brave Girl" can be interpreted as the importance of using one's abilities and powers for the greater good. Naomi, the protagonist, possessed supernatural powers, but she initially chose to isolate herself from the villagers out of fear. However, when faced with a crisis, she overcame her fears and used her powers to save the villagers from the giant.

The moral value lies in the idea that we should not let fear or self-doubt prevent us from using our abilities to help others. Naomi's bravery and willingness to confront her fears demonstrate the importance of using one's unique strengths for the benefit of others. It teaches us to be courageous, compassionate, and selfless in using our talents and powers to make a positive impact on the lives of others.
