30. The important package has been sent by my assistant. What is the active form of this sentence?AMy assistant has been sending the important packageBMy assistant was sending the important packageCMy assistant is sending the important packageDMy assistant sends the important packageEMy assistant has sent the important package​


Jawaban:

The important package has been sent by my assistant. What is the active form of this sentence?

E. My assistant has sent the important package​.

Penjelasan:

Perhatikan cara mengubahnya menjadi kalimat aktif.

The important package (object) has been sent (verb 3) by my assistant (by subject).

Untuk mengubahnya, kita mengetahui bahwa kalimat di atas adalah passive present perfect tense (has been sent).

Jadi, pola active present perfect tense adalah

Subject + have/has + verb 3 + object.

Sehingga, susunannya hanya tinggal diubah sesuai dengan pola active-nya saja.
