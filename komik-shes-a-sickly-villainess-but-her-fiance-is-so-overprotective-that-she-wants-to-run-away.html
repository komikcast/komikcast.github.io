24. I have a spesial friend she is my ... and sits besides me . her name is ratih she is ... girl and very simple to look . however i really ... her she is not only kind but also tough.. Ratih comes from a very ... familiy her father is a porter and her mother has passed away. she has a /// brother his name is ranu in order to ... their father,ratih and her brother work ... to earn some money . ratih sells food during our class break while her brother ... newspapers and magazines after school one thing that i alaways admire abouut her is that she can ... her time well, and she always ... cheerful


Berikut adalah paragraf tersebut dengan kata-kata dalam titik-titik yang sudah dilengkapi:

I have a special friend. She is my classmate and sits beside me. Her name is Ratih. She is a beautiful girl and very simple to look at. However, I really admire her. She is not only kind but also tough.

Ratih comes from a very poor family. Her father is a porter and her mother has passed away. She has a little brother. His name is Ranu. In order to help their father, Ratih and her brother work hard to earn some money. Ratih sells food during our class break while her brother sells newspapers and magazines after school.

One thing that I always admire about her is that she can manage her time well and she always is cheerful.

Pembahasan

Untuk dapat mengisi titik-titik dengan kata-kata yang tepat , kita terlebih dahulu harus memahami keseluruhan isi teks bacaan tersebut beserta konteksnya. Karena teks tersebut terdapat dalam bahasa Inggris, maka pembaca harus mengerti bahasa Inggris yang digunakan, dan apabila perlu menerjemahkannya secara sederhana ke dalam bahasa Indonesia untuk dapat memahami isinya.

Kata-kata yang tepat untuk digunakan untuk mengisi titik-titik adalah kata-kata yang sesuai dengan konteks kalimat terkait. Misalnya:

"One thing that I always admire about her is that she can ... her time well..."

kalimat tersebut berarti "Satu hal yang selalu aku kagumi tentang dia adalah bahwa dia dapat ... waktunya dengan baik..."

Ketika memahami kalimat tersebut, kita dapat mengetahui bahwa kosa kata yang tepat untuk mengisi bagian yang kosong adalah "mengatur", dalam bahasa Inggrisnya "manage".
