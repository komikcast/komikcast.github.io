Soal:

Make 5 questions and answer them

= Buatlah 5 Pertanyaan dan Jawablah

Jawaban:

1. When did the writer/he/she go to the dolphin show?

= The writer/he/she went to the dolphin show last weekend with his/her family.

2. Where did the writer/he/she take a seat?

= The writer/he/she took a seat in the first line of the show.

3. Why did the writer/he/she raise his/her hand?

= because The instructor of the dolphin was calling some volunteer to come forward. So The writer/he/she raised his/her hand and got called by him.

4. What did the instruction tell the dolphin?

= He told the dolphin to kiss the writer on his/her cheek.

5. Was the writer/he/she happy?

= yes The writer/he/she was so happy because the dolphin kissed on the cheek.

Rumus yg digunakan utk membuat Pertanyaan:

* Verbal Sentence (Active)

(?) WH + DID + S + VERB 1 + O?

1. When + DID + the writer/he/she + GO + to the dolphin show?

2. Where + DID + the writer/he/she + TAKE + a seat?

3. Why + DID + the writer/he/she raise his/her hand?

4. What + DID + the instruction tell the dolphin?

* Nominal Sentence (Active)

(?) Was/were + S + O?

5. Was + the writer/he/she + happy?

Catatan:

Menggunakan Subjek The writer/He/She karena Tokoh utama dalam teks hanya diketahui subjek I tanpa memberitahu gender yg dimiliki (wanita or pria)

Jika Tokoh I (saya/aku) dalam cerita:

* Dia laki2 = He

* Dia perempuan = She

Untuk umum, gunakan The writer (penulis)
