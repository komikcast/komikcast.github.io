15. The number of houses sold by an estate agent follows a Poisson distribution with a mean of 2 per week. Find the probability that in the next 4 weeks, the estate agent sells more than 6 houses. ​


Jawab:

An average of 2 per week means that the agent on average sells 8 houses per month (per 4 weeks). Therefore, we are going to compare the probability of the agent selling more than 6 houses per month given the average of 8 per month (λ = 8).

P(x = k) = λ^k × e^(-λ) ÷ k!

P(x = 0) = 8^0 × e^(-8) ÷ 0!

P(x = 1)  = 8^1 × e^(-8) ÷ 1!

P(x > 6) = 1 - P(x ≤ 6)

P(x > 6) = 1 - [P(x = 0) + P(x = 1) + P(x = 2) + P(x = 3) + P(x = 4) + P(x = 5) + P(x = 6)]

P(x > 6) = 1 -  P(x <= 6)

P(x > 6) = 1 - 0.31337

P(x > 6) = 0.68663

17. Make these sentences passive,omitting the agent phrase They fix the prices in advance


Active → Passive

They fix the prices in advance.

=

The prices in advance are fixed by them.

Penjelasan

Kalimat Pasif (passive voice) merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Pada soal di atas, kalimat menggunakan pola Simple Present tense. Yang dimana, untuk mengubah kalimat aktif ke pasif harus menggunakan rumus sebagai berikut:

Subject + to be (is/am/are) + V3 + Object.
