10. The foreigner is difficult to understand...his strange accent


The foreigner is difficult to understand Because Of his strange accent

Penjelasan:

Because Of adalah konjungsi bawahan atau konjungsi, yang digunakan di awal klausa atau klausa, sebelum subjek dan kata kerja.

Because Of adalah kata depan (preposition) atau konjungsi dua kata yang digunakan sebelum kata benda atau kata ganti. Untuk beberapa alasan, itu diikuti oleh objek berupa kata benda atau frase, kata ganti atau kata kerja.

"Karena" pola: karena + kata benda (frasa) / kata ganti / kata kerja

Contoh:

She was sleepy because of the melody. – Dia merasa ngantuk karena melodi itu.

George was dissappointed because of the result of exam. – John merasa kecewa karena hasil ujian itu.

36. Write  a dialogue of offering help based on the situation belowYou were walking  along the beach. You met an old foreigner who lose her daughter.


Offering Help

Ungkapan untuk menawarkan bantuan (offering help) bisa bermacam-macam tergantung situasi yang bersangkutan. Secara umum, ungkapan offering help kata-kata seperti:

Let me help you ...Let me ... for you ...Shall I ... for you ... ?(dan lainnya)
Pembahasan

Untuk situasi "You were walking along the beach. You met an old foreigner who lost her daughter.", ada kemungkinan dialog berikut:

You: Good day, Sir. You seem upset. Is there a problem?

Foreigner (F): I can't find my daughter. She was with me at the convenience store round the corner, but then she was gone.

You: Calm down, Sir. Let me take you to the police station. There is one nearby, just down the road.

F: Thank you, young man.

You: While we're walking, does your daughter have a cellphone with her?

F: Yes, she does, but I don't have one myself. Oh, what's her number now? I can't remember well.

You: Maybe you can try calling your wife or anyone at home to call her. Let's do that at the station. Ah here we are, Sir. Let's report this to the police and ask for their help.

F: You have been very helpful, young man. Thank you very much.

You: You're welcome, Sir. I hope everything will turn out fine.
