Pembahasan:

The Lord of the Rings adalah buku berjenis novel, yang ditulis oleh J. R. R. Tolkien, seorang penulis dari Inggris. Buku pertama kali diterbitkan pada tahun 1954, dan dibagi menjadi tiga bagian (atau volume), yaitu The Fellowship of the Ring, The Two Towers, dan The Return of the King.

The Lord of the Rings juga telah dibuat menjadi film, yang paling terkenal adalah film trilogi yang disutradarai oleh Peter Jackson yang dirilis tahun 2001-2003.

Ketiga bagian atau volume dari The Lord of the Rings ini adalah:

1. The Fellowship of the Ring,

Bagian pertama berkisah tentang awal mula cincin yang menjadi pusat ceira, serta bagaimana hobbit (kurcaci) Frodo Baggins bisa memilikinya. Fellowship atau persahabatan yang dimaksud adalah Frodo dan teman-temannya (termasuk Aragorn, Gimli dan Legolas) yang berupaya menghancurkan cincin agar tidak jatuh ke tangan Sauron yang jahat.

2. The Two Towers

Berkisah akan Sauron dan Saruman, dua tokoh antagonis (musuh utama) dalam cerita, serta upaya Aragorn, Gimli dan Legolas mempertahankan kerajaan Rohan dari serangan orc. Sementara itu Frodo dan Sam melanjutkan perjalanannya menghancurkan cincin itu.

3. The Return of the King.

Berkisah pertempuran mempertahankan kota Minas Tirith, ibukota Kerajaan Gondor, dan kembalinya Aragorn sebagai Raja Gondor. Frodo kemudian berhasil menghancurkan cincin di Gunung Doom, tempat cincin ditempa.

20. ubah lah dri kalimat aktif ke kalimat pasif 1. The manager will suspend their salary. 2. Who killed the doctor? 3. Some teachers proposed him headmaster.​


ACTIVE-PASSIVE VOICE

1) Their salary will be suspended by the manager.

2) By whom was the doctor killed?

3) He was proposed headmaster by some teachers.

EXPLANATION

Passive voice is commonly used to emphasize the recipient of the verb.

Here are steps how to change the active voice to the passive voice :

~ pay attention to the verb whether it's an intransitive or not. It's commonly we can't change an intransitive verb to the passive voice.

~ the object of an active voice is moved to the subject of a passive voice or instead.

~ all of verbs are conjugated to the past participle verb.

BASED ON YOUR QUESTIONS

1) SIMPLE FUTURE

A : S + will + verb¹+ object.

P : S(O) + will+be + verb³+ by+Agent.

Subject (the manager)+verb (will suspend)+Object (their salary).

changes to :

New subject(their salary)+verb(will be suspended)+by+Agent (the manager).

2) PAST SIMPLE [WH. QUESTION]

A : Who + verb² + object + ?

P : By whom + be verb + object of active + verb³+ ?

WH. word (Who)+verb (killed)+Object (the doctor)?

changes to :

By whom+ was (be verb) + the doctor (object of active)+ killed (3rd verb) ?

"Who" is used as a subject. But, because it is an interrogative sentence, we have to use the be-verb "Was" before the passive subject. The use of Be verb at the beginning is as a feature of a yes or no question. A WH-question must start with a WH word.

3) PAST SIMPLE (AFFIRMATIVE)

A : S + verb² + O

P : S(O) + BE (was/were) + verb³+by Agent.

Subject (some teachers)+verb(proposed)+Indirect Object(him)+ direct object (headmaster)

changes to :

New subject (he)+ verb (was proposed) + Direct object (headmaster) by + Agent (some teachers)

32. 1. Rumus Passive = Be + V3 **Simple present Tom Kills the ant (aktif) The ant is killed by Tom (pasif) be V3 **Present Continuous Tom is Killing the ants (aktif) The ants are being killed by Tom (Pasif) **Present Perfect Tom has killed the ans (aktif) The ants have been killed by Tom (Pasif) **Simple past Tom killed the ant (aktif) The ant was killed by Tom (pasif)​


Jawaban:

Tentu, berikut adalah penjelasan lebih rinci tentang rumus pasif dalam berbagai bentuk tense:

1. Simple Present Tense:

- Kalimat Aktif: Tom kills the ant.

- Kalimat Pasif: The ant is killed by Tom.

- Rumus Pasif: Be (is/am/are) + V3

2. Present Continuous Tense:

- Kalimat Aktif: Tom is killing the ants.

- Kalimat Pasif: The ants are being killed by Tom.

- Rumus Pasif: Be (is/am/are) + being + V3

3. Present Perfect Tense:

- Kalimat Aktif: Tom has killed the ants.

- Kalimat Pasif: The ants have been killed by Tom.

- Rumus Pasif: Have/has + been + V3

4. Simple Past Tense:

- Kalimat Aktif: Tom killed the ant.

- Kalimat Pasif: The ant was killed by Tom.

- Rumus Pasif: Was/were + V3

Rumus dasar untuk membentuk kalimat pasif adalah menggunakan kata kerja bantu "be" (sesuai dengan tense yang digunakan) diikuti oleh kata kerja bentuk ketiga (V3) dari kata kerja utama. Kata kerja bentuk ketiga (V3) adalah bentuk kata kerja yang digunakan dalam kalimat pasif. Dalam beberapa kasus, kata kerja bantu "be" juga dapat diikuti oleh kata kerja "being" dalam bentuk continuous atau "been" dalam bentuk perfect.
