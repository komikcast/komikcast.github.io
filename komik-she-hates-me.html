36. Arange the following words and phrases to make a meaning full sentencesListening to - Milea - likes - but -watching - she - television - hates - the radio​


Jawaban:

Ada 4 kemungkinan :

Milea likes listening to the radio but she hates watching television.Milea likes watching television but she hates listening to the radio.Milea hates watching television but she likes listening to the radio.Milea hates listening to the radio but she likes watching television.

Jawaban:

Listening to - Milea - likes - but -watching - she - television - hates - the radio​

Milea likes listening to the radio but she hates watching television.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat pada jawaban di atas bisa dibalik menjadi:

Milea likes watching television but she hates listening to the radio.

Jadi silakan pilih yang paling sesuai.

Kalimat pada jawaban di atas mengikuti pola Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

Kata like dan hate bisa langsung ditambahkan Verb-ing (Gerund):

Milea likes listening to the radio

She hates watching television.

Semoga membantu ya.
