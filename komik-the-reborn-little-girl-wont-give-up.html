20. The girl.....(give) her english speech for 30 minutes


the girl given her english speech for 30 minutesAnswer: 

The sentence above is about duration as there's a phrase 'for 30 minutes'. It can be in the past (which means past perfect is applied) or until now (which means present perfect continous will be applied) 

The girl had been giving her English speech for 30 minutes. (past perfect continous)
The girl has been giving her English speech for 30 minutes. (present perfect continous)

24. The little girl fell in the field this morning. (-) (?)


Jawaban:

The little girl fell in the field this morning.

(-) The little girl did not fall in the field this morning.(?) Did The little girl fall in the field this morning?

PEMBAHASAN Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yang dilakukan di masa lalu atau Peristiwa di masa lampau.

Ciri waktu Past Tense

=> Yesterday

=> This morning

=> Last night

=> Two weeks ago

Rumus Past Tense (+) S + Verb-2(-) S + did not + Verb-1(?) Did + S + Verb-1

Catatan Kalimat soal terdapat ciri Past Tense yaitu “this morning” maka pada kalimat Positive menggunakan Verb-2 “fell”Tapi pada kalimat Negative (-) dan Interrogative (?) menggunakan Verb-1 “fall” karena sudah ada DID
