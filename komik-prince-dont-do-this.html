31. they dont speak english in this shop buat klimat pasive​


English is not spoken in this shop by them.

Jawaban:

They don't speak English in this shop (Active).

English is not spoken by them in this shop (Passive).

Pembahasan :Kalimat Passive mempunyai rumus be + V3.subjek dikalimat aktif akan menjadi objek pada kalimat passive, dan objek dikalimat aktif akan menjadi subjek pada kalimat passive.dalam penulisannya, perlu diperhatikan saat mengganti pronoun (kata ganti) sebagai subjek (I, you, we, they, she, he) atau objek. (Me, you, our, them, her, him).kalimat passive diatas menggunakan rumus simple present, sehingga mempunyai rumus.Active : S + don't + V1+ OPassive : S + to be (is/am/are) + not + V3 + by + O

_____

Mapel : Bahasa Inggris

Kelas : -

BAB : Passive Voice

35. Bentuk pasif dari they dont speak english in this shop


In this shop, English didn't spoken by themSIMPLE PRESENT BENTUK NEGATIVE
Active : Subject + Do/Does + Not + V1 + Object
Passive : Subject + Am/Are/Is + Not + V3 + By...

NOTE !!!
Object dalam kalimat aktif berubah jadi subject di kalimat pasif, bgtu sbalik nya.

Aktif : They don't speak english in this shop.
Pasif : English isn't spoken by them in this shop.
