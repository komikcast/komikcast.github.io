3. ubahlah verba aktif menjadi verba pasif tulislah subjek dari kalimat pasifnya simple present a. the teacher helps me...... ................... by the teacher. b. the teacher heips jane...... .................. by the teacher. c. the teacher helps us...... ................... by the teacher. simple past a. the teacher helped me....... ................. by the teacher. b.the teacher helped them...... ................ by the teacher. present perfect a. the teacher has helped joe....... ................. by the teacher. b. the teacher has helped us....... .................. by the teacher. future a.the teacher will help me....... .................. by the teacher. b. the teacher is going to help me...... .................. by the teacher. c. the teacher will help tim........ ..................... by the teacher. d. the teacher is going to help tim........ ...................... by the teacher.


Passive Voice

JAWABAN:

Simple Present  Tense

a. The teacher helps me

=> I am helped by the teacher.

b. The teacher helps Jane

=> Jane is helped by the teacher.

c. The teacher helps us

=> We are helped by the teacher.

Simple Past  Tense

a. The teacher helped me

=> I was helped by the teacher.

b. The teacher helped them

=> They were helped by the teacher.

Present Perfect  Tense

a. The teacher has helped Joe

=> Joe has been helped by the teacher.

b. The teacher has helped us

=> We have been helped by the teacher.

Simple Future  Tense

a. The teacher will help me

=> I will be helped by the teacher.

b. The teacher is going to help me

=> I am going to be helped by the teacher.

c. The teacher will help Tim

=> Tim will be helped by the teacher.

d. The teacher is going to help Tim

=> Tim is going to be helped by the teacher.

PEMBAHASAN:

Passive voice merupakan suatu bentuk kalimat dimana subjek kalimat menerima aksi, bukan melakukan aksi. Tidak seperti active voice yang fokus terhadap pihak yang melakukan aksi (doer of action), bentuk ini lebih berfokus kepada pihak atau objek yang menerima hasil dari suatu aksi tersebut (receiver of action).

Rumus Kalimat Pasif ( Passive Voice)

Subject(object dari kalimat aktif) + to be ( disesuaikan dengan bentuk tensesnya + Verb 3 (past participle)

TENSES                                               RUMUS PASSIVE VOICE  

Simple present                               subject + is/am/are + Verb 3  

Present perfect                           subject + have/has been + Verb 3  

Simple past                                      subject + was/were + Verb 3  

Simple future                                     subject + will be + Verb 3  

Be going to                            subject + is/am/are + going to + be + Verb 3

14. Finley plat nursery implements the .…. latest agricultural techniques.


The correct answer is:

Finley plant nursery implements the very latest agricultural techniques.

The blank parts of the sentence, as you can see, contains the similar or word synonym of "the latest", so we can fill the blank with the very latest to emphasize the most modern agricultural techniques which the Finley plant nursery implements.

Pembahasan

Dalam mengisi bagian yang kosong dari kalimat rumpang di atas kita perhatikan kalimat tersebut memakai format simple present tense, terlihat dari penggunaan verb implements untuk subject "Finley plant nursery". Tanpa bagian yang kosong pun sebenarnya makna kalimat sudah benar, hanya saja bisa ditambahkan adverb "very" untuk menekankan makna kalimat bahwa yang digunakan adalah teknik yang paling terbaru (the very latest).
