Simple present

Active voice = S + V1 + O

Passive voice = S(O) + to be + V3 + by + O(S)

AV = She receives a letter.

S V1 O

PS = A letter is received by her.

S (O) to be V1 O (S)

Jawab:

Dalam AV terdeteksi bahwa kalimat bentuk Present Tense karena menggunakan verb 1 Setelah Subjek

(+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O

AV: I eat brown rice and green beans

PV: Brown ice and green beans are eaten by me
