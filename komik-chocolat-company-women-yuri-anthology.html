29. Diketahui perbandingan uang Kaka Eric dan kaka Anthology 2 : 3 ,dan uang kaka Anthology dan uang bg Melpin 2 : 5 ,jika jumlah uang ketiganya Rp. 50.000 ,maka besar uang bg Melpin adalah copas, ngasal => sangat tidak ramah._. ​


Jawab:

Besar uang Melpin adalah Rp30.000

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Perbandingan Baru :

 E    A   M

2     3  

       2   5

---------------------

4     6   15

Kaka Eric : 4Kaka Anthology : 6Bang Melpin : 15

Karena yang ditanya adalah Bang Melpin

[tex]\frac{Perbandingan Melpin}{Seluruh perbandingan} x seluruh uang[/tex]

yaitu sama dengan

[tex]\frac{15}{25} x 50.000 = 30.000[/tex]

SEMOGA MEMBANTU

Misal:

Kaka Eric = aKaka Antholoy = bBg Melpin = c

[tex]~[/tex]

Maka, diketahui:

a : b = 2 : 3

b : c = 2 : 5

a : b : c = (2 × 2) : (3 × 2) : (3 × 5)

a : b : c = 4 : 6 : 15

a + b + c = Rp. 50.000

[tex]~[/tex]

Ditanya:

uang Bg Melpin

[tex]~[/tex]

Penyelesaian:

c/(a + b + c) = 15/(4 + 6 + 15)

c/Rp. 50.000 = 15/25

c/Rp. 50.000 = 3/5

5c = (Rp. 50.000)(3)

5c = Rp. 150.000

c = Rp. 150.000/5

c = Rp. 30.000

Kesimpulan

Jadi, besar uang Bg Melpin adalah Rp. 30.000

33. procedur text How to make a chocolat ball


How To Make Chocolate Balls

Ingredients:
1. 250 gr cookies
2. 200 gr milk chocolate
3. 1/2 cup of full cream
4. To decorate: 100s and 1000s and crushed cookies

Steps:

1.Put various toppings in small containers on table
2.Put the cookies in a sturdy bag and crush them with rolling pin
3.Melt the chocolate in the cream until it has melted
4.Mix the chocolate and cream mixture with the cookie crumbs
5.Make little balls with the mixture
6.Roll the balls in one of the toppings
7.Put them in the fridge
8.Enjoy your chocolate balls !How to make a chocolate ball
Materials  :
-one pack of merry biscuit
-a sachet of chocolate cream
-1 pack of chocolate sprinkles
-one pack of colorful chocolate sprinkles

tools :
-a bowl
-a potato masher
-baking tray

Steps :
-First, pour one pack of merry biscuit into the bowl.
-Second, break the biscuit with a potato masher until it seems like powder.
-Third, add a sachet of chocolate cream and stir it thoroughly until the dough is soft.
-Fourth, use your hands to make the dough like a little balls
-Next, spill the colorful chocolate sprinkles on the little balls.
-Then, prepare the chocolate balls in the baking tray and put it on the refrigerate for a full day.
-Finally, a frozen chocolate balls ready to serve.
