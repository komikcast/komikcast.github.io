5. Rewrite the sentences to subjunctive and find the fact A. My teacher wish i come on time everyday B. The teacher wish the students pass the exam C. I wish i have more time for you last night D. I wish i see the footbal match last night E. We wish we will graduate together next year.


Subjunctive mood adalah ketika kita ingin mendeskripsikan harapan akan sesuatu namun berbeda dengan kenyataan. Penggunaannya adalah menggunakana kata wish, lalu diikuti oleh verb.

A. My teacher wish I  come on time everyday

Wish: My teacher wish I came on time everyday

Fact: My teacher  do not come on time everyday

B. The teacher wish the students pass the exam

Wish: The teacher wish the students passed the exam

Fact:  The students do not pass the exam

C. I wish i have more time for you last night

Wish: I wish I had had more time for you last night

Fact: I did not have more time for you last night

D. I wish i see the football match last night

Wish: I wish I had seen the football match last night

Fact: I did not see the football match last night

E. We wish we will graduate together next year.

Wish: We wish we would graduate together next year

Fact: We will not graduate together next year

Pembahasan

Ketika menggunakan ekspresi wishes, the subjunctive mood akan mengubah kata kerja utama mundur satu tenses ke belakang:

present simple tense menjadi past simple tense present continuous tense menjadi past continuous tense present perfect tense menjadi past perfect tense present perfect continuous tense menjadi past perfect continuous tense past simple tense menjadi past perfect tensepast continuous tense menjadi past perfect continuous tense past perfect tense menjadi past perfect tense (no further shift possible) past perfect continuous tense menjadi past perfect continuous tense (no further shift possible)Contoh:

Fact: We do not graduate together next year

Wish: We wish we graduated together next year

Penjelasan: karena situasinya terjadi di masa sekarang, maka wishes menggunakan simple past tense.

Fact: I did not see the football match last night

Wish: I wish I had seen the football match last night

Penjelasan: karena situasinya terjadi di masa lampau, maka wishes menggunakan past perfect tense.

14. the girls have been told the good news. the active sentence is A. someone told the girls the good news B. Someone have told the girls the good news C. Someone has told the girls the good news D. The good news has been told by the girls E. The girls have told the good news


Jawaban:

C. Someone has told the girls the good news

Pembahasan

Active Voice

Subject melakukan Aksi/tindakan/pekerjaan di dalam kalimatnya

Passive Voice

Object diberi aksi/tindakan/pekerjaan oleh Subject

Catatan:

Dalam Passive Voice. By Subj (By someone, By people, By him, By her) boleh dihilangkan dan tidak digunakan dalam kalimat passive.

Berdasarkan Pilihan Ganda:A. Past Tense

Active:

(+) S + V2 + O

someone + told + the girls the good news

Passive:

(+) O + Tobe (was/were) + V3 + [By Subj]

The good news + was + told + [by someone]Penjelasan:Opsi A secara structure benarTapi tidak cocok dengan kalimat soalMaka opsi ini belum tepat

B. Present Perfect

Active:

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O

Someone + have + told + the girls the good newsPenjelasan:Opsi B belum tepat, karena Someone seharusnya diikuti dengan has

C. Present Perfect

Active:

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O

Someone + has + told + the girls the good news

Passive:

(+) O + has/have + been + V3 + [By Subj]

The girls + have + been + told + the good news [by someone] Penjelasan:Opsi ini secara Structure bisa dianggap tepat,dan paling mendekati kecocokan dengan kalimat soal karena By Someone bisa dihilangkan atau tidak digunakan dalam kalimat PassiveJadi opsi C tepat,

D. Present Perfect

Passive:

(+) O + has/have + been + V3 [By Subj]

The good news + has + been + told [by the girls]Penjelasan:Opsi D adalah kalimat passive, sedangkan yg dicari Kalimat aktif/Active SentenceMaka opsi ini belum tepat

E. Present Perfect

Active:

(+) S + has/have + Verb 3 + O

The girls + have + told + the good news

Passive:

(+) O + has/have + been + V3 + By Subj

The good news + has + been + told [by The girls]Penjelasan:Opsi ini secara Structure benar, tapi yg dicari adalah kalimat Active.Jadi kurang tepat

Kesimpulan

Active Voice:

(+) S + has/have + V3 + O

Someone has told the girls the good news

Passive Voice:

(+) O + has/have + been + V3 + By Subj

The girls have been told the good news [by someone]Jadi:

The girls have been told the good news. the active sentence is Someone has told the girls the good news (C) ✅
