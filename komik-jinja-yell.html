36. Irregular verb dance,hate,hug,time,hook,yell,post,hum?


[tex]irregular \: verb[/tex]

Irregular Verb adalah kalimat dimana bentuk kata kerja Simple past dan past participlenya tidak berubah

Ket. : Verb 1 = Kata kerja yg sedang terjadi

Verb 2 = Kata kerja lampau

Verb 3 = Kata kerja yg sudah terjadi

Contoh :

Verb 1 : Rina does play badminton every Sunday

Verb 2 : Anto are bread yesterday

Verb 3 : Dono has taken a bath just now

Pertanyaan : Irregular Verb dari kalimat dance , hate , Hug , Hook , Yell , post , Hum , time

Verb 1 / Infinites : Dance , Hate , Hug , Hook , Yell ,Post , Hum , Time

Verb 2 / Simple past : Danced , Hated , Huged , Hooked , Yelled , Posted , Humed , Timed

Verb 3 / Past participle : Dancing , Hating , Huging , Hooking , Yelling , Posting , Huming , Timing.
