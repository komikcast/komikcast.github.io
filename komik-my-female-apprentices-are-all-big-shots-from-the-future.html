25. rubah lah ke kalimat passive 1) my parents are talking the future in the dining room 2) the student operate the computer


Subject : English

Category : Passive Voice

Level : JHS VIII

Code : 5

Keywords : Passive

-----------------------

The passive voice is one of the two grammatical voices.

In passive voice, the noun or noun phrase that is the object of an active voice becomes the subject.

Construction of passive voice : S + be + past participle  (v₃)

Function of the passive voice : to emphasize the object and the action instead of the subject of a sentence.

-------------------------

Answers :

1

Active : My parents are talking about the future in the dining room.

Passive : The future is being talked about by my parents in the dining room.


2

Active : The students operate the computer.

Passive : The computer is operated by the students.
