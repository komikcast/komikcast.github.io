6. i handed the telegram to the man. the telegram had just arrived.combine the following sentences from adjective clause dong​


Jawaban:

I handed the telegram which had just arrived to the man.

Penjelasan:

Adjective Clause

Merupakan sebuah klausa yang menunjukkan sifat dari suatu kata benda. Klausa tersebut akan muncul setelah noun. Biasa diawali dengan relative pronoun seperti who, which, dan that.

Jika benda tersebut adalah orang hidup, gunakan relative pronoun who atau that.

Jika benda tersebut adalah benda mati, gunakan relative pronoun which atau that.

Analysis:

I handed the telegram to the man. (main clause)

The telegram had just arrived. (adjective clause)

--> I handed the telegram which had just arrived to the man. (a sentence with two clauses: main and adjective)

32. Please explain the message contained in the song BILLIE EILISH:when the party's over ... please answer as accurately as possible ...


ANSWER:

“When the party’s over,” menceritakan tentang kisah si penulis lagu (Billie Elish) yang berusaha menjauhkan diri dari kekasihnya. Ia seolah merasa tak pantas, dan akan terus menyakiti kekasihnya jika terus berada disamping kekasihnya.

“when the party’s over,” juga dapat dimaknai sebagai lagu pertengkaran pasangan kekasih, dimana salah satu pasangan (Billie) harus mengalah, harus merendahkan dirinya, mencoba tak menyalahkan siapapun, menahan emosinya, dan berusaha menjauhi kekasihnya, agar tak terjadi lagi adu argumen yang saling menyakiti.

Dianalisis dari judul lagunya (when the party’s over), digambarkan dengan jelas, bahwa "emosi" itu layaknya sebuah pesta (party) yang ramai riuh. Jika kita berada di sebuah pesta yang ramai dan riuh, lalu tiba-tiba ada orang yang menelpon, maka kita akan mematikan handphone kita karena kita takkan bisa mendengar suara orang yang menelpon kita. barulah saat pestanya selesai, kita akan menelpon balik.  

Analogi pesta tersebut, sama persis seperti pertengkaran Billie dengan kekasihnya. kekasihnya sangat-sangat emosi bagai riuhnya pesta, sehingga jika Billie ikut-ikutan emosi dan berdebat maka akan terjadi miss komunikasi dengan kekasihnya. untuk itulah Billie pergi atau menjauhkan diri dari kekasihnya agar emosi kekasihnya padam. barulah saat emosi dari kekasihnya padam, billie akan menghubungi dan berbicara dengan baik-baik.

“When the Party’s Over” is a song recorded and performed by American singer Billie Eilish. In this song, Billie, who is apparently fed up with the behavior of her lover, uses the phrase “I’ll call you when the party’s over” as a metaphor to let him know she’s got better things to focus on than him and the drama accompanying him.

In the following lines from the song’s bridge, Billie reveals that the relationship is over. However, it appears her boyfriend still isn’t sure he wants to end it.

“Once we both said our goodbyes
Let’s just let it go
Let me let you go”

In an interview she had with coup de main magazine in 2018, she shed more light on the song’s meaning. According to her, the lyrics aren’t really about her being sad that the relationship has collapsed.

She likened the lyrics to being in a loud environment and being on the phone with someone. You both can’t hear each other due to the loud noise in the background and on top of that, the person is “mad at you for some reason” and is yelling. So you get fed up with the whole situation and tell them this: “F**king leave me alone”.

She later told coupe de main that the track is more like a sequel to her 2017 song titled “Party Favor”. In “Party Favor”, Billie is getting out of a toxic relationship with her possessive boyfriend. Her goal is to get him out of her life.
