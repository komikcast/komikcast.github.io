6. correct relative pronoun errors where necessary in the following sentences.1. There are eighteen different kinds of penguins whom live south of the equator2. The biologist which went to the South Pole is studying emperor penguins.3. Emperor penguins survive winds who blow at speeds up to ninety-five miles an hour in winter.4. Emperor penguins, who are the largest penguins do not make nests.5. Emperor penguins, who are the largest among penguins, do not make nests.6. The United States has sent researchers to Antartica which are making experiments to measure the energy expended by emperor penguins.​


Jawaban:

1. ERROR (WHOM diganti WHICH)

2. ERROR (WHICH diganti WHO)

3. ERROR (WHO diganti WHICH)

4. ERROR (WHO diganti WHICH, tambahkan koma sebelum DO)

5. ERROR (WHO diganti WHICH)

6. ERROR (WHICH diganti WHO)

Penjelasan:

1. There are eighteen different kinds of penguins WHICH live south of the equator

2. The biologist WHO went to the South Pole is studying emperor penguins.

3. Emperor penguins survive winds WHICH blow at speeds up to ninety-five miles an hour in winter.

4. Emperor penguins, WHICH are the largest penguins, do not make nests.

5. Emperor penguins, WHICH are the largest among penguins, do not make nests.

6. The United States has sent researchers to Antartica WHO are making experiments to measure the energy expended by emperor penguins.

RELATIVE PRONOUNS:

PERSON + WHO/WHOM

NON PERSON + WHICH
