PEMBAHASAN Present Continuous

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian atau Kegiatan yg sedang berlangsung saat ini.

Rumus Present Continuous

(+) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + V-ing + O/C

(-) S + Tobe (is/am/are) + not + V-ing + O/C

(?) Tobe (is/am/are) + S + V-ing + O/C?

CATATAN

Subject + Tobe

I/you/we/they/dua nama + areHe/She/it/satu nama/The man + is ✔️

KESIMPULAN

kalimat menggunakan menggunakan Tobe (is) dan V-ing yang dimaksud adalah “buying” bukan “bakeing”.

jadi kalimat tanya:Tobe => isSubject => The manV-ing => buying Object => mangoes Complement => In front of the store

Someone stole Barbara's wallet two days ago

Kalimat diatas menggunakan Simple Past Tense yang menunjukkan bahwa aktifitas terjadi di masa lampau.

Past Tense dalam Active Voice :

Subject + Verb 2 + Object

Past Tense dalam Passive Voice :

Object + Was/Were + Verb 3 + Subject

Jadi, kalimat Passive Voicenya adalah

Barbara's wallet was stolen by someone two days ago.

Pembahasan

Active Voice Adalah kalimat aktif Yang Subjek melakukan aksi.

Sebaliknya, Passive Voice adalah kalimat Pasif yang terjadi object diberi aksi oleh Subjek

Rumus Passive dari Past TenseActive: S + V2 + OPassive: O + Be (was/were) + Bu S

Maka berikut :

2.They interrupted the presentation.

a. The presentation was interrupted by them. ✔️

b. The presentation were interrupted by them.

3.Did you touch his things?

a.Was his things touched by you?

b. Were his things touched by you?✔️

4.She revealed the secret of her success.

a. The secret of her success was revealed by her.✔️

b.The secret of her success were revealed by her.

7.The convenience store sold fresh meat.

a. Fresh meat was sold by the convenience store.✔️

b.Fresh meat were sold by the convenience store.

8.Somebody left the gate open.

a. The gate was left open.✔️

b.The gate were left open.

9.The police caught the thieves.

a.The thieves was caught by the police.

b.The thieves were caught by the police. ✔️
