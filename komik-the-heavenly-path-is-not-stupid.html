10. A garden is surrounded by a path, n1m wide find the area of the path.


luas kanan= 5,5 m × 1 m = 5,5 m
luas atas = 6 m × 1 m = 6 m
luas kanan = luas kiri
luas atas = luas bawah
jadi, 5,5 + 5,5 + 6 + 6 = 23 m
so, the area of the path is 23 m
