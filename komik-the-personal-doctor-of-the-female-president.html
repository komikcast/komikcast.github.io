The ... are the presidents assistant.

a. vice president (wakil presiden)

b. ministers (para menteri)

The assistant of the president is the vice president or prime minister.

28. 4. The number of student in 3 Junior High School is 1,200 with the ratio of the number of male students to the number of female students is 3;2 The number of female students is …​


Jawab:

480

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Jumlah siswa di SMP = 1200

Perbandingan banyak siswa

Laki-laki : perempuan = 3 : 2

Laki-laki = 3n

Perempuan = 2n

Jumlah siswa

=> 3n + 2n = 5n = 1200

=> n = 1200/5 = 240

Perempuan = 2n = 2*240 = 480 orang.

The number of female students is 480.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Laki-laki : Perempuan = 3:2

Jumlah = 1.200

Male = 3/(3+2) × 1.200

= 3/5 × 1.200

= 720 students

Female = 2/(3+2) × 1.200

= 2/5 × 1.200

= 480 students
