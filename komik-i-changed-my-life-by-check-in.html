36. Tolong dibantu ya, buat composition 10 sentences using past tense about The best day in my life. Start with word "The best day in my life is when i met my boyfriend."


Jawaban:

10 kalimat dalam bentuk composition yang menggunakan past tense mengenai hari terbaik dalam hidup saya:

The best day in my life was when I met my boyfriend. He approached me and we started talking, and I immediately felt a connection.

We went out on a date a week later, and I was nervous but excited. We had a great time together and it was the start of something special.

After a few months of dating, my boyfriend surprised me with tickets to see my favorite band in concert. It was an incredible show and I felt so lucky to be there with him.

The best day in my life was also the day that my boyfriend and I took a trip to the beach together. We walked along the shoreline, took pictures, and had a picnic.

One year later, on the anniversary of the day we met, my boyfriend proposed to me. It was a beautiful moment and I will never forget it.

We got married six months later, and our wedding day was truly the best day in my life. We were surrounded by family and friends, and I felt so happy and loved.

After our wedding, we went on our honeymoon to Europe. We visited so many amazing places and had the time of our lives.

When we returned from our honeymoon, we moved into our first house together. It was a small but cozy place, and it felt like the start of a new chapter in our lives.

Last year, on our fifth wedding anniversary, my husband surprised me with a trip to Hawaii. We spent a week there, relaxing on the beach and exploring the island.

Looking back, the best day in my life was just the beginning of a wonderful journey with my husband. I feel grateful for every moment we've shared together, and I know there will be many more great days to come.

topic:present perfect tense

present perfect tense merupakan tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan sesuatu yang sudah terjadi atau belum terjadi sebelum sekarang.

Structure:

(+) S + have/has + V3 + O.

(-) S+ have/has + not + V3 + O.

(?) Have/has + S+ V3 + O.

Detail Tambahan :

Kelas : IX SMPMapel : Bahasa InggrisBAB : 6 - Present Perfect TenseKode Kategorisasi : 9.5.6

Yesterday, when i was waiting for a taxi, i was so bored. Then, i saw a girl sat at a bench near the town dustbin. I saw her rummage the dustbin with a voracious stare at the putrid food in the dustbin. Then, she got her hand with a full of trash. I was wondering why she do that. Now, my mind is so confuse. My mind become more confused when she ate those trash in front of my eyes. I thought that she was starving. But i am still confuse why she won't go to the restaurant and eat dinner. But then i realize that she probably has no money to get a better food. So, i came near her and sat at the same bench she used to sat down. She was smirked on me, but she keep ate those junk. I offer her my sandwich, she accepted it and express her gratitude to me. I ask her why she did that to make me sure that she do that because she has no money. My hunch was right, she told me that she is starving and have no choice, but eat the trash. So, i give her some of my money and ask where are her home. She told me that she have no place to be called as home. I remember that i need someone to help me with the house work such as cooking or cleaning. So i offer her a job to be my maid in my house. She accepted and we go home together. Now i understand how difficult to find money for a people like her. She doing well her job as my maid. I can pay her enough and i can fulfill her needs.  I thanks to god, because he already made me awake from my extravagant life. Now i understand that being an extravagant people is the biggest mistake ever that can deliver rich people became a poor people. So, i learn to save my money from that girl. I learn how to use my money when it's necessary only.
