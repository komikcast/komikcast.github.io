1. How did the early survive to live


Jawaban:

Early survival is the ability of humans to meet their basic needs such as food, water, shelter, and protection from harm. First of all, humans needed to find safe and sufficient food and water to sustain life. Furthermore, humans need to find safe and suitable places to live to protect themselves from inclement weather and other hazards.

Over the years, humans have developed various technologies and strategies for survival, such as farming, raising animals, and building construction. Communities have also formed social and economic structures that assist in managing resources and the division of labor to meet their needs.

In addition, human intelligence and cultural development also played an important role in survival. Humans use their knowledge and creativity to overcome challenges, solve problems, and adapt to changing environments.

Overall, early survival involves humans' ability to seek out and satisfy their basic needs using available knowledge, creativity, and resources.

10. what are three ways plants and animals have adapted to survive?


Jawaban:

apa tiga cara tumbuhan dan hewan beradaptasi untuk bertahan hidup?

-Morphological Adaptation in Animals

Why do bird's beak vary ?, the shape of the bird's beak varies according to the type of food. Its beak is suitable for eating grains. Hummingbirds, their beaks are suitable for sucking honey from flowers. Pelicans, their beaks are suitable for catching fish. Eagle, its beak is suitable for tearing the flesh of its prey. Woodpecker. its beak is suitable for sculpting tree trunks and catching insects in it. Morphological adaptations in birds can also be seen in various forms of the feet.

Morphological Adaptation in Plants

Based on the place of life, plants can be divided into as follows.

Xeroflt, namely plants that adapt to a dry environment, for example cactus. How to adapt xerophytes. among others, have small or even leafless leaves (modified into thorns), the stem is coated with a thick waxy layer, and has long roots so that the range is very wide.

Hydrophytes. namely plants that adapt to a watery environment, for example lotus. Hydrofit adaptation methods include broad and thin-leafed, and many stomata.

Higrofit, which is a plant that adapts to the humid environment, for example ferns and mosses.
