The line showing personification is A. "It's just the weight of the world." Personification is a literary device where non-human things are portrayed with human qualities. Here, the weight of the world is portrayed as if it is a human burden.

The word "it" in the sentence "I will break it for you" refers to the person's heart, which is a metaphorical way of saying that they will help relieve the individual's sadness or heaviness. The correct option is (E) your heart.

4 second

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

distance = 1.92 m = 192 cm

speed = 48 cm/s

time = distance ÷ speed

time = 192 / 48

= 4 second
