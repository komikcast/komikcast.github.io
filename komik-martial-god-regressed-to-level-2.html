1. She regressed to her old bad habits. Best word to replace "regressed" without changing the meaning of the sentence is A. returned an earlier state. B. progressed to a better situation C. transgressed set rules D. reclaimed lost possession


VOCABULARY AND READING COMPREHENSION

Regressed =  returned an earlier state

----------------

EXPLANATION

Regress is the preterite of the verb regress.

Regress is from the Latin present infinitive regradi.

The Latin prefix re- means back or backwards.

The Latin present infinitive gradi comes from the verb gradior that means (I) go.

So, regress means go back = return

Therefore, the best word to replace "regressed" without changing the meaning of the sentence is (A) returned an earlier state.
