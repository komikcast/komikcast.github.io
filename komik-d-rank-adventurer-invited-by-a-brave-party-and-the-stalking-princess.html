18. I wasn't invited by her to the party ubah dalam kalimat active


[Active Voice]

➡ She didn't invite me to the party.

PEMBAHASAN

Passive voice adalah kalimat yang subjeknya merupakan penerima aksi, sedangkan objeknya adalah pelaku dari aksi tersebut.

Sedangkan active voice merupakan kalimat yang subjeknya adalah pelaku dari suatu aksi, sementara objeknya adalah penerima aksi tersebut (kebalikan dari passive voice).

↬based on question

Past simple

(P) Subject + to be (was/were) + verb 3 + by agent

(A) Subject + verb 2 + O / C

passive

I wasn't invited by her to the party.

(Aku tidak diundang olehnya ke pesta itu)

subject: I

to be: was (not)

verb 3: invited

agent: (by) her

_

active

She didn't invite me to the party.

(Dia tidak mengundangku ke pesta itu)

subject: She

verb: (didn't) invite

object: me

↬verb on question

Regular verbs adalah kata kerja beraturan. Bentuk past tense dan past participle-nya ditandai dengan adanya akhiran -d atau -ed.

invite - invited - invited

mengundang

passive: I wasn't invited by her to the party.

active: She didn't invite me to the party.

_

Jawaban:

1. Passive: I was invited to the party by my teacher

Active: My teacher invited me to the party.

2. Passive: the burglar is being arrested by the police

Active: The police is arresting the burglar.

3. Passive: the house is painted blue by the students

Active: Students paint the house blue.

4. Passive: the party had been well planned by the committee.

Active: The committee had the party well planned.

Penjelasan:

Untuk nomor 1. menggunakan pola kalimat aktif Simple Past Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-2 + Object

Untuk nomor 2. menggunakan pola kalimat aktif Present Continuous Tense:

(+) Subject + Tobe (is/am/are) + Verb-ing + Object

Untuk nomor 3. menggunakan pola kalimat aktif Simple Present Tense:

(+) Subject + Verb-1 S/ES + Object

Untuk nomor 4. menggunakan pola kalimat aktif Past Perfect Tense:

(+) Subject + Had + Verb-3
