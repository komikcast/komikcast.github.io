Expressions to get﻿ someone's attention﻿ Expressing opinions﻿ Listen...﻿ Look...﻿ Have a look at...﻿ Pay attention...﻿ So now, listen to...﻿ Excuse me...﻿ Hey...﻿ Less Formal﻿ I’d say …﻿ It seems to me ...﻿ I think ... ﻿ I reckon ... ﻿ I feel ... ﻿ Formal﻿ Personally, I consider/believe...﻿ In my view...﻿ In my opinion...﻿ To my mindtolong KK ini di jwb kk​


Jawaban:

*Less Formal:

-Listen (to me, please) →Asking

(Yes Miss/Sir/Ma'am) →Giving

-Look (at me) →Asking

(Yes, what)? →Giving

-Have a look at (some references from the internet)? →Asking

(Yes, that's good idea)! → Giving

-Pay attention (now)! → Asking

(Yes, Ma'am) → Giving

-So now, listen to (me, please) → Asking

(Yes, Sir) →Giving

-Excuse me. (Attention, please) → Asking

(Yes, please) → Giving

-Hey, (pay attention to my explanation) → Asking

(Alright, I'm listening) →Giving

*Formal:

-I’d say (we should make a poster about giant squid) → Attention

(Yes, we can print out the picture and write the facts about a giant squid) → Response

-It seems to me (you just say the truth) → Attention

(Okay, I will try) → Response

I think (﻿it will be great)! → Attention

(That’s a good idea) → Response

I reckon (﻿we have to think of an animal underwater and make a poster about it) → Attention

(It’s a giant squid) → Response

I feel (a giant squid is very unique) → Attention

(Really? Wow)! → Response

*Formal﻿ Personally:

-I consider/believe (we should make a poster about underwater life) → Attention

(Maybe we can get some ideas) → Response

-In my view (that's a good dictionary) → Attention

(Thank you) → Response

-In my opinion (we should finish our Biology assignment before Sunday) → Attention

(Yes, you are right) → Response)

-To my mind (how interesting) → Attention

(Yes, sure) → Response

Penjelasan:

-Expressing Attention is a way used to ask someone to focus their attention on us or vice versa we focus attention on the person who is talking.

-Giving attention is an expression used to give attention/response.
