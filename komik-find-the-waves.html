2. Although thunder and lightening are produced at the same time, light waves travel faster ....., o we see the lightening before we hear the thunder. Than sound waves do Than sound waves are Do sound waves Sound waves


Jawaban:

Than sound waves do

Penjelasan:

Teks tersebut memiliki sifat komparatif digunakan untuk membandingkan perbedaan antara dua objek.

Analisis, perhatikan:

1. Faster (Artinya ini ada komparasi antara Light waves dan sound waves), jika ada faster, kita tambahkan "than" setelahnya.

2. Dan Verb yang digunakan adalah “travel” maka auxiliary yang digunakan adalah "do/does"

3. So, we see the lightening before we hear the thunder = Jadi, kita melihat kilat sebelum mendengar guntur.

Jadi artinya Light waves travel faster than sound waves do = Gelombang cahaya merambat lebih cepat daripada gelombang suara.


3. choose the best correct option :Although thunder and lightening are produced at the same time, light waves travel faster ......, so we see the lightening before we hear the thunder . A.than sound waves doB.than sound waves are C.do sound waves D.sound waves​


Jawaban:

A. Than the sound waves do.

Penjelasan:

Degree of comparison.

- Sebelumnya ada kata FASTER, yang umumnya diikuti dengan THAN.

- Karena predikat menggunakan kata kerja, yaitu TRAVEL, kalimat berupa VERBAL, sehingga auxiliary yang digunakan adalah DO/DOES

Jawaban:

They can be transverse or longitudinal. However, all waves have common properties—amplitude, wavelength, frequency, and speed. Amplitude describes how far the medium in a wave moves. Wavelength describes a wave's length, and frequency describes how often it occurs.
