10. why the woman who consumed alcohol will get menopause sooner?​


Alcohol can cause a decrease in progesterone levels and a spike in estrogen in premenopausal women. If women experience a decrease in progesterone, it will cause difficulties for women to get pregnant or give birth to the maximum age. A bad lifestyle does tend to cause health problems, as well as the menopause process which may occur more quickly.

31. the woman is my mom.i miss the woman. (adjective clouse)


Subject : English

Category : Adjective clause

Level : JHS  VIII

Code : 5

Adjective clause adalah klausa yang menerangkan kata benda. Adjective diletakan mengikuti kata benda yang akan diterangkan. Adjective clause diawali oleh relative pronoun that (untuk subject/object orang/bukan orang), who (untuk subject orang), whom (untuk object orang), which (untuk subject/object bukan orang), whose (untuk kepunyaan orang),  of which (untuk kepunyaan bukan orang), atau relative adverbs where (untuk nama tempat; pengganti there) dan when (untuk waktu; pengganti then).

ANSWER :

The woman whom I miss is my mom.
