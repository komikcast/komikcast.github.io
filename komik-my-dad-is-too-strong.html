1. my dad...(strong) than my brother​


Jawaban:

My Dad STRONGER Than My Brother

Penjelasan:

Pertanyaan Yang Diatas Ada Dalam Bentuk COMPARISON OF ADJECTIVES. Dalam Bentuk Tersebut Dibagi 3 Bagian, Yaitu Adjectives, Positive, Comparative, Dan Superlative.

• Adjectives Terbagi Menjadi 4, Yaitu

- 1 Syllable, 1 Suku Kata. Contoh : Tall, Short,...

- 2 Syllable, 2 Suku Kata. Contoh : Ugly, Tasty,..

- 3 Syllable And More, 3 Suku Kata / Lebih. Contoh : Comfortable, Beautiful,...

One Syllable

• Dalam Positive Form, Dipakai

AS ___ AS / Sama ___ Seperti

Contoh : As TALL As

• Dalam Comparative Form, Dipakai

___ER Than / Lebih ___ Daripada

Contoh : Taller Than

• Dalam Superlative Form, Dipakai

THE ___ EST / Paling

Contoh : The Tallest

YANG HARUS DIPERHATIKAN

•Jika Adjectives Lasted With __CVC Maka Harus

___CVC + C / Double

Contoh : BIG ( CVC ) Double Menjadi BIGGER Than

•Kecuali Lasted With __W

Contoh : Lower Than

Two Syllable

•Dalam Positive Form Sama

•Dalam Comparative Form Dibagi Menjadi 2, Yaitu

-Lasted With __Y

Eliminate (Hapus) Y Plus + (Tambahkan) IER

Contoh : Ugly > UGLIER Than

-Not Lasted With __Y

More ___ Than

Contoh : More Careful Than

• Dalam Suerlative Form Dibagi 2, Yaitu

-Lasted With __Y

Hapus Y Ganti Jadi IEST

Contoh : The Prettiest

-Not Lasted With __Y

The Most __

Contoh : The Most CareFul

Three Syllable And More

• Positive Form Sama, Yaitu As___As

• Comparative Form

More __ Than

Contoh : More Comfortable Than

• Superlative Form

The Most ___

Contoh : The Most Comfortable

29. My name is Toni,.....mom is Anita, and ....dad is dilan, I am.... Son


Jawaban:

My name is Toni, my mom is Anita, and my dad is Dilan. I am their son.

Penjelasan:

1. "My name is Toni" - In this part, the speaker introduces themselves by stating their name, which is Toni.

2. "my mom is Anita" - The speaker mentions their mother's name, Anita, using the possessive pronoun "my" to show that Anita is their mother.

3. "and my dad is Dilan" - Similarly, the speaker mentions their father's name, Dilan, using the possessive pronoun "my" to indicate that Dilan is their father.

4. "I am their son" - Finally, the speaker identifies their relationship with Anita and Dilan, stating that they are the son of both of them. The word "their" is used to indicate possession by both parents, making it clear that Toni is the child of Anita and Dilan.
