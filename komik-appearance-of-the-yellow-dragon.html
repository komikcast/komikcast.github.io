10. The ratio of the length of blue ribbon to the length of yellow ribbon is 7:9. The sum of the length of blue ribbon and the length of yellow ribbon is 272 cm. The difference in its length is .... ​


34cm

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

= 7 + 9

= 16

= (272 : 16)

= 17

Blue ribbon = 17 × 7

= 119 cm

Yellow ribbon = 17 × 9

= 153 cm

Difference in lenght = 153cm - 119cm = 34cm
