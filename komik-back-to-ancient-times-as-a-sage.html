4. tolong d jadikan pasif form verb 3 !!!1. people have worn perfume since ancient times​


Jawaban:

Perfume has been worn by people since ancient times

PEMBAHASAN

people have worn perfume since ancient times

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject => People Has/have => haveVerb-3 => wornObject => perfume Complement => since ancient times

Berdasarkan hal2 di atas:Kalimat adalah kalimat aktif karena Subjek melakukan aksi dalam kalimatnya Kalimat menggunakan since sebagai ciri Present Perfect dan terdapat Have+V3

Maka kalimat Passive adalah object (perfume) diberi aksi (has been worn) oleh Subjek (by People) Berikut Rumus Yang digunakan:

Present Perfect

Active: S + has/have + V3 + O

=> people have worn perfume since ancient times

Passive: O + has/have + been + V3 + By S

=> Perfume has been worn by people since ancient times

=== Semoga Membantu ===
