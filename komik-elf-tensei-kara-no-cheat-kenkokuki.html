16. Contoh kalimat past tense dan present perfect menggunakan kata wear, cheat, make, keep, fry


Past tense

Subject + verb + ed

Negative

Subject + did not + infinitive without to

Interrogative

Did + subject + infinitive without to

1.. I wore blue dress in Mely's birthday last Sunday.

2. Mother made a cheese cake last week.

3. He cheated me yesterday.

4. Mother kept money in the drawer yesterday

5. My servant fried fish for lunch this morning.

Present perfect

Subject + have/has + main verb (past participle form)

1.I have worn blue dress in Melly's parry.

2 He has cheated me

3..Mother has made chocolate cake.

4 Father has kept his money to hia wallet

5. Nanny has fried some chicken for our lunch
