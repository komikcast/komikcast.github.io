9. The higher the temperature of a molecule, a. than it has more energy b. the more energy it has c. more energy has it d. it has more energy


Jawaban:

Penjelasan:

The correct answer is b. the more energy it has.

As the temperature of a molecule increases, its kinetic energy also increases. Temperature is a measure of the average kinetic energy of molecules in a substance. So, when the temperature is higher, the molecules have greater average kinetic energy, indicating that they possess more energy.
