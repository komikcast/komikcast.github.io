The trip was enjoyable, or you can say the trip was impressive. The group got a general briefing about how to take pictures underwater. They saw  groups of tiny fish and they wanted to know what kind of fish was that. After they saw the group of tiny fish they wanted to know the information of that fish from a proper guide so they knew their habitat and behaviour. Pembahasan

Melihat dari bentuknya, teks yang dimaksud adalah recount text, isinya menceritakan pengalaman penulis dalam berwisata ke pulau Bunaken. Karena ini adalah berisi pengalaman penulis, di dalamnya fitur bahasa yang digunakan adalah past tense. Kita lihat struktur teks tersebut seperti ini:

Orientation:

Last year I left New Zealand for Bunaken Island. I went there with a group of New Zealand divers. Getting there was not quite easy.

Events:

Soon after our arrival at Bunaken, we got a general briefing. It included a description about how to take pictures under water.Then, we began our diving. In our diving, we saw groups of tiny fish. In order to identify them, we need a good guide. Without some knowledge of their habitat and behavior, it would be difficult.

Conclusion:

In summary, the trip was mostly enjoyable.
