34. Ubah ke pasif 1. The president is choosing the millenial to help him 2. The president should choose the millenial to help him 3. The president has choosen the millenialed to help him 4. The president chose the millenials to help him


PASSIVE VOICE

Answer  :

1. The millennials are being chosen {by the president} to help him

2. The millennials should be chosen {by the president} to help him

3. The millennials have been chosen {by the president} to help him

4. The millennials were chosen {by the president} to help him.

Note :

Dari kelima kalimat memiliki informasi yang sama terkait generasi milenial {millennials}. Kata "The millenial" dalam teks no.1, 2 dan 3 seharusnya tertulis bentuk jamaknya "millennials" karena disetarakan dengan penulisan "millennials" pada teks no.4

Explanation

Passive voice, menitikberatkan pada aksi dan obyek (si penerima aksi) sebagai pemberitaan utama.

Obyek dalam kalimat active berubah posisi menjadi subyek dalam kalimat passive. Subyek (si pelaku aksi) dalam kalimat active berubah posisi diletakkan sebagai obyek preposisi (by Agent) dalam kalimat passive dan atau bahkan tidak dimunculkan.

Penyusunan passive voice berdasarkan tenses dari masing-masing konteks kalimat.

Pola utama passive voice adalah penggunaan Be+Verb3

Pembahasan dari kalimat active diatas :

1. Penggunaan Present Continuous

Konstruksi Rumus :

Active => S+tobe(is/am/are)+verb-ing+O

The president is choosing the millennials to help him

Passive => S+tobe (is/am/are) + being+verb3 + by Agent

The millennials are being chosen {by the president} to help him.

Note :

"The millennials" is a plural countable noun=>tobe "are"

2. Penggunaan Modal Auxiliary "should."

Konstruksi Rumus :

Active=>S+should+verb-1 +O

The president should choose the millennials to help him

Passive=>S+should+be+verb-3+by+Agent

The millennials should be chosen {by the president} to help him.

3. Penggunaan Present Perfect

Konstruksi Rumus :

Active=> S+has/have+verb -3+O

The president has chosen the millennials to help him

Passive=>S+has/have+been+verb-3+by Agent

The millennials have been chosen {by the president} to help him.

Note  :

"The millennials" is a plural countable noun=>auxiliary verb"have"

4. Penggunaan Simple Past

Konstruksi rumus :

Active=>S+verb-2+O

The president chose the millennials

Passive=>S+was/were+verb-3+by Agent

The millennials were chosen {by the president} to help him

Note

"The millennials" is a plural countable noun=>tobe "were"
