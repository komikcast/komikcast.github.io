2. a child throws a baseball with a mass of 2 kg upwards with an initial speed of 40m / s if the value of the earth's gravitational acceleration at that location is 10 ms-2, calculate: a. the time it takes for the baseball to reach the highest point! b. the maximum height the baseball reaches!


Jawaban:

a. 4 seconds

b. 80 meters

Penjelasan:

To solve this problem, we can use the equations of motion for projectile motion.

a. The time it takes for the baseball to reach the highest point can be found using the equation:

v = u + at

Where:

v = final velocity (which is 0 m/s at the highest point)

u = initial velocity (40 m/s)

a = acceleration (acceleration due to gravity, -10 m/s^2)

t = time

Rearranging the equation, we have:

0 = 40 - 10t

Solving for t, we get:

10t = 40

t = 4 seconds

So, it takes 4 seconds for the baseball to reach the highest point.

b. The maximum height the baseball reaches can be found using the equation:

s = ut + (1/2)at^2

Where:

s = displacement (maximum height)

u = initial velocity (40 m/s)

t = time (4 seconds)

a = acceleration (acceleration due to gravity, -10 m/s^2)

Plugging in the values, we have:

s = 40(4) + (1/2)(-10)(4)^2

s = 160 - 80

s = 80 meters

Therefore, the maximum height the baseball reaches is 80 meters.

13. If the density of boat and people is.... And the density of boat and furniture is.... What is the density of the only boat?


Penjelasan:

The density of the boat only is the difference between the density of the boat and people and the density of the boat and furniture.

```

Density of boat only = density of boat and people - density of boat and furniture

```

Let

* ρb = density of boat only

* ρbp = density of boat and people

* ρbf = density of boat and furniture

Then

```

ρb = ρbp - ρbf

```

For example, if the density of boat and people is 1000 kg/m³ and the density of boat and furniture is 1200 kg/m³, then the density of the boat only is 200 kg/m³.

```

ρb = 1000 kg/m³ - 1200 kg/m³ = 200 kg/m³

```

In other words, the boat only is 200 kg/m³ less dense than the boat with people and 200 kg/m³ more dense than the boat with furniture.
