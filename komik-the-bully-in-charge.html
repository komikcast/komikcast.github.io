5. Please charge the positive sentence in-to negative and introgative!​


[Simple Past Tense] Change these  into Negative (-) and Interrogative (?) Sentences!

1. Dani studied English

(-) : Dani didn't study English

(?) : Did Dani study English?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to study - studied - studied = belajar

2. My father was a manager in this office

(-) My father wasn't a manager in this office

(?) Was my father a manager in this office?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to be - was/were - been

3. She had a baby

(-) She didn't have a baby

(?) Did she have a baby?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to have - had - had = mempunyai

4. Mother cooked fried rice

(-) : Mother didn't cook fried rice

(?) : Did mother cook fried rice?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to cook - cooked - cooked = memasak

5. My grandfather came to my house

(-) : My grandfather didn't come to my house

(?) : Did my grandfather come to my house?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to come - came - come = datang

6. Bagas fixed his toy car

(-) : Bagas didn't fix his toy car

(?) : Did Bagas fix his toy car?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to fix - fixed - fixed - memperbaiki

7. Sari threw a basket of rubbish

(-) : Sari didn't throw a basket of rubbish

(?) : Did Sari throw a basket of rubbish?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to throw - threw - thrown = melempar

8. Mr. Hadi saw some dogs in the garden

(-) : Mr. Hadi didn't see some dogs in the garden

(?) : Did Mr. Hadi see some dogs in the garden?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to see - saw - seen = melihat

9. I slept in your room yesterday

(-) : I didn't sleep in your room yesterday

(?) : Did I sleep in your room yesterday?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to sleep - slept - slept = tidur

10. Dian closed the window

(-) : Dian didn't close the window

(?) : Did Dian close the window?

V1 - V2 - V3 = to close - closed - closed = tutup

Pola Simple Past Tense menggunakan VERB (kata kerja):

Positive (+) : S + V2 + O

Negative (-) : S + DID NOT + V1 + O

Interrogative (?) : DID + S + + V1 + O + ?

Pola Simple Past Tense menggunakan TO BE :

Positive (+) : S + WAS/WERE + O

Negative (-) : S + WAS/WERE NOT + O

Interrogative (?) : WAS/WERE + S + O + ?

WAS digunakan untuk subject HE, SHE, IT, dan SINGULAR NOUN (kata benda tunggal)

WERE digunakan untuk THEY, WE, I, YOU, dan PLURAL NOUN (kata benda jamak)
