1. Mrs Rohit gave a total of $64.90 to her three sons. The eldest son received $4.30 more than the youngest son. The second son received $7.80 less than the youngest son. How much did the eldest son receive?


Let's call the amount of money that the youngest son received "x".

According to the problem, we know that:

The eldest son received $4.30 more than the youngest son, which means he received x + 4.30 dollars.

The second son received $7.80 less than the youngest son, which means he received x - 7.80 dollars.

We also know that the total amount of money given to the three sons was $64.90:

x + (x + 4.30) + (x - 7.80) = 64.90

Simplifying this equation:

3x - 3.50 = 64.90

3x = 68.40

x = 22.80

So the youngest son received $22.80, the eldest son received $4.30 more than that, which is $27.10, and the second son received $7.80 less than that, which is $19.30.

Therefore, the eldest son received $27.10.

39. i am the youngest in the class, it is degree comparison of​


Jawaban :

I am the youngest in the class. It is degree comparison of...

Answer :

superlative degree.

Penjelasan :

Youngest adalah bentuk superlative dari kata Young. Dimana perbandingannnya adalah antara satu orang dengan lebih dari 2 orang lainnya. Dan dalam hal ini yang dibahas dalam kalimat di atas adalah perbandingan satu orang dengan seluruh orang yang ada dikelas tersebut.

Berikut ini adalah bentuk perubahan dari kata Young

Young - Younger - Youngest

Young (positive) = Muda

Younger (comperative) = Lebih muda

Youngest (Superlative) = Paling muda.

=================================

Mapel : Bahasa Inggris

Materi : Comparison Degree

Kelas : 8

Kode Soal : 8.5

Kode Kategorisasi : 8.5.2
