18. the surgeon will be help the nurse ubah ke kalimat passive voice​


Soal bentuk Future Tense:

(+) S + will + V1 + O

= The surgeon will help the nurse

Ubah ke kalimat passive voice​

Jawab:

S + will be + V3 + by (agent)

Passive: The nurse will be helped by the surgeon

Catatan:

Perubahan Passive dengan cara menukar posisi antara Subject (The surgeon) dan Object (The Nurse), karena ini Future maka gunakan Will be + Verb3

=====Semoga Membantu======

Mapel: Bahasa Inggris

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Passive Voice

Kode Mapel: 5

a. skilled

b. shoemakers

c. scarce were

d. what is​

During the 1600's, skilled shoemakers were scarce in what is now the United States.

Kata scarce merupakan kata sifat, sehingga letaknya seharusnya setelah auxiliary verb (were) atau sebelum kata benda (shoemarkers).
