22. There are four sources of self-concept _______ there is only one which influences you the most in your life.


Jawaban:

Self-concept is distinguishable from self-awareness, which refers to the extent to which self-knowledge is defined, consistent, and currently applicable to one's attitudes and dispositions.[4] Self-concept also differs from self-esteem: self-concept is a cognitive or descriptive component of one's self (e.g. "I am a fast runner"), while self-esteem is evaluative and opinionated (e.g. "I feel good about being a fast runner").

Self-concept is made up of one's self-schemas, and interacts with self-esteem, self-knowledge, and the social self to form the self as a whole. It includes the past, present, and future selves, where future selves (or possible selves) represent individuals' ideas of what they might become, what they would like to become, or what they are afraid of becoming. Possible selves may function as incentives for certain behaviour.[3][5]

The perception people have about their past or future selves relates to their perception of their current selves. The temporal self-appraisal theory[6] argues that people have a tendency to maintain a positive self-evaluation by distancing themselves from their negative self and paying more attention to their positive one. In addition, people have a tendency to perceive the past self less favourably[7] (e.g. "I'm better than I used to be") and the future self more positively[8] (e.g. "I will be better than I am now").


23. 38. Change the sentence into a Non-finite clause “People power, which toppled the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986, became very popular” A People power became very popular in toppling the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986. B People power became very popular which toppling the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986. C People power became very popular which toppled the corrupt regime Ferdinand Marcos in 1986. D People power toppled the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986, became very popular. E People power, toppling the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986 became very popular.


Jawaban:

“People power, which toppled the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986, became very popular”

E. People power, toppling the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986 became very popular.

Penjelasan:

non-finite clause adalah clause yang bebas dari tanda-tanda penggunaan tense (entah present, past, atau future). Jadi di dalam non-finite clause itu kita tidak bisa menentukan apakah dia kalimat present, atau past, atau future, karena clause itu tidak mengandung tense sama sekali.

non-finite clause dipakai untuk menyederhanakan sebuah kalimat, menghindarinya dari menjadi kalimat yang ribet atau kompleks.

Contoh dalam bahasa Indonesia:

Sri pernah mengikuti kompetisi antar sekolah.

Sri kali ini diminta untuk mengikuti kompetisi tingkat nasional.

Jika kedua kalimat ini kita gabung menjadi sebuah kalimat majemuk, maka menjadi:

Sri, yang pernah mengikuti kompetisi antar sekolah, kali ini diminta untuk mengikuti kompetisi tingkat nasional.

Terdengar sudah pas, tapi sebenarnya masih bisa disederhanakan lagi, menjadi:

Pernah mengikuti kompetisi antar sekolah, kali ini Sri diminta untuk mengikuti kompetisi tingkat nasional.

Nah, bagian Pernah mengikuti kompetisi antar sekolah inilah yang disebut non-finite clause.

Kembali ke soal:

“People power, which toppled the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986, became very popular”

Kekuatan rakyat, yang menggulingkan rejim korup Ferdinan Marcos di tahun 1986, menjadi sangat populer.

Kalau kita perhatikan bagian which toppled the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986 di sana masih ada jejak tense yaitu past tense (terlihat dari penggunaan verb 2 toppled (dari verb 1 topple, artinya menggulingkan).

Maka kita buat menjadi non-finite clause menjadi:

People power, toppling the corrupt regime of Ferdinand Marcos in 1986, became very popular.


Sama seperti contoh dalam bahasa Indonesia di atas, di sini kalimatnya lebih diringkas dengan merubah ke non-finite clause.

Contoh lain:

I thought about my safety, so I ran toward the house. (Aku memikirkan keselamatanku, maka aku berlari ke rumah)

Dengan non-finite clause:

Thinking about my safety, I ran toward the house (Memikirkan keselamatanku, akupun berlari ke rumah)

Was digunakan untuk menunjukkan atau menyatakan orang pertama tunggal atau dikenal dengan first person singular dan orang ketiga tunggal atau third person singular. Orang pertama tunggal yakni I (saya) dan orang ketiga tunggal terdiri dari He (dia – laki-laki), She (dia – perempuan), dan It (itu).

Was adalah bentuk V2 (past tense/lampau) dari is/am (to be), dan were adalah bentuk V2 dari are (to be).
