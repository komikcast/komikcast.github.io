20. Struktur teks dari Origin of Snail House In ancient times, the snails did not bring their home anywhere. In the beginning, snails lived in an abandoned bird’s nest in the trees. The night was warm and the day was cool because the leaves shaded the nest where the snails lived. But when the rains came, the leaves could no longer block the rain that fell. Snails caught cold and wet when the rainy season arrived. Then the snails moved into the holes in the trunks of trees. When the day was hot, the snails were well protected, even if it rained. It looked like that I had found a suitable home for me, the snail said in his heart. But on a sunny day, there came a woodpecker. Tok..tok … tok … woodpecker kept pecking the trunks where the snails lived. Snails became very distracted and could not sleep. With annoyed heart, snails went out of the hole in the trunk and looked for a place to stay next. Snails found a hole in the ground, which seemed to be warm when the night came, thought snails. Snails cleaned up those holes and decided to stay in it. But it turned out when the night came. The rats came from all directions to damage the hole. Again and again, the snails had to leave the hole to find a new home. Snails then moved on to the edge of the beach filled with coral reefs. He thought that the sidelines of the corals might be his home. Snails could take a rest peacefully in this place. But when the tide rose up to the top of the rock, snail was swept away along with the surge of the waves. As usual, the snails had to go away to find a new home. When he was walking away from the beach, the snail found an empty shell. This shape was beautiful and very light. Being tired and cold, snails went into the shell. Snails felt warm and comfortable then he slept in it. When the morning came, snails realized that he had found the best home for himself. He did not need to go home in hurry if it rained. He also would not get heat anymore, and no one would bother him. He then brought it home with him wherever he went.

Narrative Text

Narrative text adalah teks yang bertujuan untuk menghibur pembacanya.

Jenis-jenis narrative text antara lain:

FableLegendFairy taleFictionScience FictionHorror story

Generic Structure dari Narrative text adalah:

Orientation: Mengenalkan tokoh utama, tempat dan waktu kejadian. Biasanya hanya diceritakan secara singkat sajaComplication: Masalah atau problem dalam cerita mulai muncul.Resolution: Masalah yang timbul sudah mulai bisa diselesaikan.Re-Orientation: Berisi moral value atau hal yang dapat dipetik dari cerita tersebut. Bagian ini terkadang ditiadakan.

Language features dari Narrative Text adalah:

Menggunakan past tenseMenggunakan  action verbMenggunakan kata keterangan waktu: once upon a time, one day, long time ago, dan lain lain
Pembahasan

Karena jenis teks Origin di atas adalah Narrative text (fable), maka structurenya adalah: Orientation, Complication, Resolution dan Re Orientation. Penjelasan detilnya bisa dilihat di bawah ini.

Structure yang ada pada cerita Origin of the Snail:

Orientation: In ancient times, the snails did not bring their home anywhere. In the beginning, snails lived in an abandoned bird’s nest in the trees. Complication: Snail looked for the best home.Resolution: Snail got the best home for him.Re-orientation: Tidak ada re-orientation di cerita ini.
