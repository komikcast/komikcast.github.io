7. "i said im blinding by the light, i can't see until i feel your touch. i'm drowning in the night cause when im like this, you the one i touch"sebutkan tenses yang digunakan dalam kalimat tersebut. dan jelaskan apa makna dari kalimat diatas​


Jawaban:

Lyrics sebenarnya (grammar nya lebih tepat):

I said I'm blinded by the lights

I can't sleep until I feel your touch

I'm drowning in the night

When I'm like this, you're the one I touch

"I sai I'm blinded by the lights" (Aku bilang aku terbutakan oleh cahaya) maksudnya silau.

Ini adalah passive voice karena penggunaan "blinded" (verb 3/ past participle) setelah "am" (to be).

"I can't sleep until I feel your touch" (Aku tak bisa tidur hingga merasakan sentuhanmu).

Ini adalah negative simple present tense karena penggunaan "can't/cannot" (verb 1) tepat setelah subject "I".

"I'm drowning in the night" (Aku tenggelam di gelap malam)

Ini adalah present continuous tense karena bentuknya:

I + am + drowning  (Subject + to be + verb + ing). Continuous karena ada -ing nya, menandakan verb sedang terjadi.

"'Cause when I'm like this, you're the one I touch" (Karena ketika aku seperti ini, hanya kamu yang kupercaya)

Ini adalah simple present tense.

9. I.Change the following sentences in to Direct Speech. 1. She said ''I go to school today.''. 2. John said. ''They played football last week.''. 3. '' We will write some letters tomorrow.'' They said. 4. He said.''Who wrote the news?''. 5. He said. ''She is listening to the radio.''.


Jawaban:

1. She said that she went to school that day.

2. John said that they had played football the previous week.

3. They said that they would write some letters today.

4. He asked who had written the news.

5. He said that she was listening to the radio.

Penjelasan:

Untuk mengubah direct speech menjadi indirect, kita menggunakan tense bentuk lampau dan juga memperhatikan kata ganti orang (pronoun).

1. Simple present tense (go) menjadi simple past tense (went).

2 dan 4. Simple past tense menjadi past perfect tense.

3. Present future tense (will + verb 1) menjadi past future tense (would + verb 1).

5. Present continuous tense (is + verb -ing) menjadi past continuous tense (was + verb -ing)
