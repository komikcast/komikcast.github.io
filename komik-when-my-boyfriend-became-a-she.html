30. Apa yang salah dari "she became well -known since junior high school when she sings in front of her friends.


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: grammar review
Pembahasan: 

The mistake of the sentence:
'She became well-known since junior high school when she sings in front of her friends.' is that
- the use of since should be only for present prefect tense as it means 'until now' 
- the phrase 'when she sings ...' should be in past tense as it refers to her past experience.

So, the right one is 
' She has become well-known since junior high school when she sang in front of her friends.'

35. If her boyfriend (phone / not) ___ today, she (leave) ___ him.


If her boyfriend (phone / not) ___ today, she (leave) ___ him.

kata yang digunakan untuk melengkapi kalimat tersebut adalah (not call her) dan (will leave).

Hal ini karena kalimat tersebut merupakan bentuk kalimat conditional sentence tipe 1 yang dimana rumusnya sebagai berikut:

if + simple present tense, simple future tensesimple future tense + if + simple present tense

Susunan kalimat pada soal, sesuai dengan bentuk rumus conditional sentence tipe 1 yang pertama. Sehingga kalimat tersebut menjadi:

If her boyfriend not call her today, she will leave him.

Pembahasan

Conditional sentence tipe 1 digunakan untuk mengekspresikan suatu hal yang sangat besar kemugkinannya terjadi di waktu yang akan datang jika kita melakukan sesuatu.

Rumus yang digunakan pada jenis kalimat ini yaitu:

if + simple present tense, simple future tensesimple future tense + if + simple present tense

39. my boyfriend gives me a flower.Ubahlah dalam passive voice​!

A flower was given by my boyfriend

Penjelasan:

Jawaban:

Active: My boyfriend gives me a flower. (Pacarku memberikan Aku bunga)

Kalimat tersebut adalah Simple Pesent Tense.

Pola kalimat Passive:

Subject + to be (is, am, are) + Verb 3 + by + Object.
