16. the sum of three number is 68. the largest number is 6 less than twice the smallest , and the middle number is 10 less than the largest . find the tree numbers!


eg: smallest = x
middle = y
largest = z

z = 2x-6
y = z-10

x+y+z = 68
x+z-10+2x-6 = 68
x+2x-6-10+2x-6 = 68
5x-22 = 68
5x = 68+22
5x = 90
x = 90:5
x = 18

z = 2x-6
z = 2.18-6
z = 30

y = z-10
y = 30-10
y = 20

The smallest is 18
The middle is 20
The largest is 30
