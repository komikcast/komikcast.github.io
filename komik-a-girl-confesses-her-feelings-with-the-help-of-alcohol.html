1. The main idea of the story is the consequence of arrogance and disrespect towards one's mother and the moral lesson of valuing and respecting one's parents.

2. The characters in the story are a mother and her daughter who live in a small village.

3. The daughter in the story is portrayed as very beautiful but also very lazy and disrespectful towards her mother. She is focused on her own appearance and refuses to help her mother in their daily work.

4. The mother asks God to punish her daughter because she is hurt by her daughter's constant disrespect and lies about her identity.

5. The moral value of the story is the importance of respecting and valuing one's parents. Children should not take their parents' love and care for granted and should always be grateful and show respect. The story also emphasizes the consequences of arrogance and lying, which can lead to punishment and regret.

6. The daughter's final destiny is to turn into a stone, known as the crying stone or batu menangis. Her whole body becomes a big stone, and people can still see tears falling down the stone, a symbol of her regret and sorrow.
