Little Boy and Apple Tree

Once upon a time there was a huge apple tree. A little boy loved to come and lay around it. Every day He climbed to the top of tree to put some fruits and ate them then took a nap under the shadow of it. He loved the tree and the tree loved to play with him. (Also read : The Legend of Timun Mas)

Come and play with me,” the tree asked the boy. “I am no longer a kid, I don’t play around trees anymore.” The boy replied. ”I want toys. I need money to buy them.”

“Sorry, I don’t have money…. but you can pick all my apples and sell them. So, you will have money.” Said the tree.

The boy was so excited. He grabbed all the apples on the tree and left happily. The boy never came back after he picked the apples. The tree was sad. (Also read other article at : Bright Sunshine Vanish Darkness)

One day, the boy returned and the tree was so excited. “Come and play with me” the tree said.

“I don’t have time to play. I have to work for my family. We need a house for shelter. Can you help me?” replied the boy.

“Sorry, I don’t have a house. But you can chop off my branches to build your house.” So the boy cut all the branches off the tree and left happily. The tree was glad to see him happy but the boy never came back since then. The tree was again lonely and sad.

One hot summer day, the boy returned and the tree was delighted. “Come and play with me!” the tree said.

“I am sad and getting old. I want to go sailing to relax myself. Can you give me a boat?” asked the boy.

“Use my trunk to build your boat. You can sail far away and be happy.” Said the tree.

So the boy cut the tree trunk to make a boat. He went sailing and never showed up for a long time. Finally, the boy returned after he left for so many years.

“Sorry, my boy But I don’t have anything for you anymore No more apples for you…” the tree said.

”I don’t have teeth to bite” the boy replied. “No more trunk for you to climb on” “I am too old for that now” the boy said. (Also read other article at : Pencarian Sahabat Sejati)

“I really can’t give you anything … the only thing left is my dying roots” the tree said with tears.

“I don’t need much now, just a place to rest. I am tired after all these years.” The boy replied.

“Good! Old tree roots is the best place to lean on and rest. Come, Come sit down with me and rest.” The boy sat down and the tree was glad and smiled with tears…….

35. Hardly ever … the same world twice alike. a. spell the little boy b. spelled the little boy c. the little boy spelled d. did spell the little boy e. did the little boy spell


The correct answer for filling the blank is the option E. Hardly ever did the little boy spell the same word twice.

Pembahasan:

Dari kaidah grammar yang benar adalah jawaban E. Hardly ever did the little boy spell the same word twice. Artinya: Hampir tidak pernah anak laki-laki itu mengeja kata yang sama dua kali.Kenapa jawaban A dan B salah?

Hardly ever spell the little boy the same word twice alike tidak benar dalam kaidah arti dan susunan kata.

Artinya adalah sebagai berikut: Hampir tidak pernah mengeja anak laki-laki kecil itu dengan kata yang sama dua kali

Susunan Kata: Dalam menyusun sebuah kalimat, dimulai dengan Subjek diikuti Predikat, bukan kebalikannya (A dan B)
