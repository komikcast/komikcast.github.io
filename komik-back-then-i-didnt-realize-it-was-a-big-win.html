17. kalimat passiv i didnt make that pie. Did mrs french make it?


Answer:


Passive sentence is a form of sentence which focuses on object, so it starts with an object at the beginning of the sentence instead of using subject. The pattern is : O + auxiliary verb + V3 + by + S

The sentence above is past tense so the past passive of it is

O + was/were + V3 + by + S

The answer of such exercise is

'That pie wasn't made by me. Was it made by Mrs.French?'


Hopefully that is clear enough for you.

Mapel: English

Kategori: passive voice
