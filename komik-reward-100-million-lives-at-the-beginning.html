9. do you give information about woh wrere,and when at the beginning?,


Answer: 

In writing information, like news, articles so on, you need to add some details such as the place, time and situation etc. The guidance to write such kind you only need to apply 5W+ 1H questions (who, where, what, when, why and how). When you write news, for example, you can start it with 'what' then 'where', 'when', 'who' then followed by 'how' and 'why' to make it stand out in the crowd. Without writing about 'what' or the event first, it doesn't have the attraction to the reader.

25. Andrew deposited some money in the bank at the beginning of the year. At the end of one year, he earned an interest and had $3045 in the bank. This was 101.5% of the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year. a) How much did Andrew deposit in the bank at the beginning of the year? b) What was the amount of interest Andrew earned?​


Jawab:

a) Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

b) The amount of interest he earned was $45. The interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

The amount of Andrew's money in the bank at the end of one year is 101.5% of the amount of money he deposited at the beginning of the year:

$3045 = 101.5% × m

(m = the amount of money Andrew deposited at the beginning of the year)

$3045 = 101.5% × m

m = $3045 ÷ 101.5%

   = $3045 ÷ (101.5/100)

   = $3045 × 100/101.5

   = $3000

∴ Therefore, Andrew deposited $3000 in the bank at the beginning of the year.

The amount of interest he earned:

$3045 – $3000 = $45

Because this is the amount of interest in one year, we can say that the interest rate in that bank is 1.5% per year.

37. Buatlah kata simple past atau present perfect dari sales/drop/at the beginning of the year


Jawaban:

1. Simple Past Tense:

- Sales: sold

- Drop: dropped

- At the beginning of the year: at the beginning of last year

2. Present Perfect Tense:

- Sales: have/has sold

- Drop: have/has dropped

- At the beginning of the year: at the beginning of this year

Please note that the choice between "have" and "has" depends on the subject of the sentence. Use "have" for plural subjects (e.g., "we," "they") and "has" for singular subjects (e.g., "he," "she," "it").
