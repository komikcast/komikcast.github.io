Jawaban:

In the given diagram, angle GXY is formed by the straight line EF crossing the parallel lines GH and JK. To label the angle that is alternate to angle GXY, we need to find the alternate interior angle on the other side of the transversal line EF.

The alternate interior angle to angle GXY can be found on the other side of the transversal line EF and parallel line GH. It is the angle that is congruent (equal in measure) to angle GXY.

Let's label this angle as angle A.

11. Describe the strengths and weaknesses of primary and secondary data in Sociological Research.


Jawaban:

The advantages and disadvantages of primary data and secondary data are

1. Primary Data

a. The advantages of primary data:

The data obtained is more reflective of the truth based on what the researcher sees and hears directly.

b. Disadvantages of primary data:

Requires a relatively long time and the costs incurred are relatively large.

2. Secondary Data

a. The advantages of secondary data:

• Already available.

• Easy to get.

• A researcher can get information other than the main information.

b. Lack of secondary data:

• If the data source is wrong, out of date, or is no longer relevant it can affect the research results.

• There is dependence on the party issuing the data.

Yankee memiliki beberapa arti yang saling berkaitan, terutama digunakan untuk orang dari Amerika Serikat. Di luar Amerika Serikat, istilah ini secara umum dipakai untuk menyebut orang dari Amerika Serikat. Di dalam negeri Amerika Serikat, istilah Yankee dipakai untuk penduduk dari bagian timur laut Amerika Serikat, atau secara spesifik dipakai untuk orang dari New England yang menunjuk kepada keturunan-keturunan pendatang dari Inggris .[1]
Dalam bahasa Indonesia , dikenal istilah jengki yang berasal dari kata
yankee . Istilah jengki berasal dari sebutan untuk tentara Amerika Serikat yang mendukung pemerintah Vietnam Selatan pada tahun 1960-an. Mereka membawa serta budaya serta penampilan baru. Pada masa itu, produk model baru disebut dengan embel-embel jengki , misalnya celana jengki (celana jins ), sepeda jengki, dan
arsitektur jengki. [2] Pada tahun 1960-an, banyak rumah-rumah penduduk dibangun dengan gaya jengki, gaya populer yang memiliki keserupaan dengan gaya streamlining.[3]
Arti kata Yankee berbeda-beda menurut zamannya. Pada abad ke-18, istilah ini dipakai untuk penduduk New England keturunan pendatang asli dari Inggris yang bermukim di wilayah itu. Pada abad berikutnya, Mark Twain menggunakan istilah ini dalam novelnya A Connecticut Yankee in King Arthur's Court yang diterbitkan tahun 1889. Setidaknya pada tahun 1770-an, istilah Yankee sudah digunakan untuk menyebut orang dari negara yang kemudian disebut Amerika Serikat. Pada akhir abad ke-19, orang Amerika penduduk
Selatan Amerika Serikat menyebut orang Amerika di Utara Amerika Serikat sebagai Yankee. Namun istilah ini tidak digunakan untuk imigran pendatang baru dari Eropa. Oleh karena itu seorang wisatawan yang mengunjungi Richmond, Virginia pada tahun 1818 berkomentar, "Orang-orang yang mau berusaha [di sana] sebagian besar orang asing; orang Skotlandia, orang Irlandia, dan terutama orang-orang New England, atau Yankee, sebagaimana mereka biasanya disebut"). 31. What are the strengths and weaknesses of integrating ICT in teaching and learning activities in higher education. ​


Jawaban:

ICT, or Information and Communication Technology, can bring about numerous benefits to teaching and learning activities in higher education. However, there are also potential challenges that must be considered. Here are some of the strengths and weaknesses of integrating ICT in teaching and learning activities in higher education:

Strengths:

Improved access to information: The integration of ICT in teaching and learning activities provides access to a wealth of information and resources that might not be available through traditional teaching methods. This can greatly enhance the learning experience for students.Increased engagement and interactivity: ICT can enable interactive learning experiences, which can promote engagement and active participation among students. This can lead to increased motivation and interest in the subject matter.

Enhanced communication and collaboration: ICT tools can facilitate communication and collaboration among students, as well as between students and instructors. This can foster a sense of community and support among learners.Flexibility and convenience: ICT can provide learners with flexible and convenient access to learning materials and resources. This can be especially beneficial for students who have busy schedules or who live far away from campus.

Weaknesses:

Technical issues: The integration of ICT in teaching and learning activities can sometimes be hindered by technical issues such as hardware or software failures, slow internet connections, and other technical problems.

Lack of digital literacy: Students and instructors who are not familiar with ICT tools may struggle to use them effectively. This can lead to frustration and reduced learning outcomes.

Potential distractions: ICT can provide students with easy access to distractions such as social media, games, and other non-academic websites. This can negatively impact student performance and engagement.

High initial costs: The implementation of ICT in teaching and learning activities often requires significant investment in hardware, software, and training. This can be a barrier for some institutions or instructors who lack the necessary resources.

Overall, the strengths of integrating ICT in teaching and learning activities in higher education can outweigh the weaknesses, provided that the potential challenges are carefully considered and addressed.

38. Change the sentences into Present Perfect TenseA. My parents (work) in Sumatra for ten years.(+) …(-) …(?) …B. JK. Rowling (write) many novels since she was young.(+) …(-) …(?) …tolong di jawab!​


Present perfect tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb "have" atau "has".

have untuk I, you, we, they

has untuk she, he, it

A. My parents (work) in Sumatra for ten years

(+) My parents have work in Sumatra for ten years

(-) My parents have not work in Sumatra for ten years

(?) Have my parents work in Sumatra for ten years?

B. JK. Rowling (write) many novels since she was young

(+) JK. Rowling has write many novels since she was young

(-) JK. Rowling has not write many novels since she was young

(?) Has JK. Rowling write many novels since she was young?
