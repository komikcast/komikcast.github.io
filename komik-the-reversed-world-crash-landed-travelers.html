17. The driver of the truckwas not injured in the crash. The driver of the small car was not injured in the crash. Neither nor


neither ... nor ... merupakan contoh dari correlative conjunction. Correlative conjunction adalah kata sambung yang digunakan untuk menggabungkan kata atau frasa yang memiliki kepentingan yang sama dalam suatu kalimat.Hal yang perlu diingat ketika membuat kalimat dengan menggunakan correlative sentence adalah memastikan bahwa kata atau frasa yang dihubungkan harus seimbang atau sejenis.
Pembahasan:Kalimat: The driver of the truck was not injured in the crash. The driver of the small car was not injured in the crash.
kata-kata yang dapat dihubungkan adalah the driver of the truck dan the driver of the small car. Sehingga kalimatnya dapat dijadikan seperti ini:
neither the driver of the truck nor the driver of the small car was injured in the crash.


Demikianlah pembahasannya.

19. If all seeds landed on the ground beneath the parent plant, .... will occur


Seed Dispersal aka How Plants Spread

Before they can grow into new plants, seeds need to leave the seed pod. If all the seeds a plant produced landed just underneath the parent plant, they would be too crowded, and the established large plant might not leave them enough light or water for them all to develop properly.

Penjelasan:

The King of the Monkeys and the Two Travelers

Two men were travelling together through a distant land. They made rather odd travelling companions, because one of them was incapable of ever telling the truth and could only tell lies, whereas the other could not tell a lie if his life depended on it, but could only ever say the truth as he saw it. They had been travelling for some time, when they came to the land of the monkeys. The king of the monkeys heard that two strangers had arrived in his realm, and he was very keen to impress them with his magnificence and power, so he ordered to have them brought before him.

He arranged to receive them seated on his throne in his state room, in the middle of his palace, and had a large retinue of courtiers and servants lined up in long rows, both inside the room and along all the passages on the way in. As they made their way towards the state room, the travelers were quite nervous, and they bowed when they came before the king.

The king welcomed them heartily, and then asked them if they’d ever been in the presence of a mightier king. The traveler who always lied spoke first. “Sire, I have traveled all over the world, and never before have I been so impressed by the splendour and might of your court!” The king seemed to be quite pleased to hear this, and he asked, “And what do you think of my subjects?”

“Sire,” replied the lying traveler, “your subjects must be the most fortunate people I have ever come across in all my extensive travels, fortunate to be ruled by such a splendid and mighty monarch as you are!” The monkey king was delighted with this answer, he clapped his hands and called on his servants to prepare a magnificent feast for this guest, and to ensure that his every need was taken care of. Then he turned to the other traveler and asked him the same question.

This was of course the traveler who could only tell the truth as he saw it, and he said; “Sire, I think you are a fine ape, and all the subject you rule over are very fine apes too!” The king of the monkeys was enraged when he heard this, and he jumped from his throne. He ordered his guards to seize the luckless traveler and to beat him to death.

*TRAIN COLLISION*
Train collision is the encounter of two trains inadvertently and caused the train to tumble or get out of the railroad tracks.

There are many factors that cause the collision of the train. It usually happens due to miscommunication, like when a moving train meets another train on the same line so that a collision can not be avoided. The train wheels will be out of the railway. Train collisions also occur because there is no latch on some train tracks. it become one of the causes of the collision of the train.

Train collisions sometimes occur because of Human Error because there are some machinists who don't implement the standard operating procedures established or due to the occurrence of setting a poor work schedule resulting in physical fatigue. Because of physical fatigue, Some Machinists are sleepy and even fall asleep when they drive the trains.
