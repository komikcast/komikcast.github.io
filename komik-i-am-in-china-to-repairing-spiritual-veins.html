27. Is someone repairing your shoes? make it in passive voice​


Is your shoes being repaired by someone? ✅

PEMBAHASAN

What is 'passive voice'?

Passive voice adalah suatu bentuk kalimat di mana yang melakukan aksi adalah objeknya, sedangkan yang menerima aksi adalah subjeknya. Sebagai penalaran, berikut ini contoh dalam Bahasa:

– Buku sedang dibaca oleh saya.

Buku merupakan subjek yang menerima aksi, sedangkan saya merupakan objek yang melakukan aksi.Tenses yang digunakan pada kalimat di atas merupakan Simple Present Continuous Tense bentuk Interrogative.

What is 'simple present continuous tense'?

Simple present continuous tense merupakan tenses yang digunakan untuk membicarakan suatu aksi yang sedang berlangsung pada waktu tertentu di masa kini. Rumus kalimat pasifnya adalah sebagai berikut:

(+) S + to be + being + V3 + by agent.(–) S + to be + not + being + V3 + by agent.(?) To be + S + being + V3 + by agent + ?

Pembahasan soal

Active Voice

Is someone repairing your shoes?

– Di mana:

Is ⇄ To be presentsomeone ⇄ Subjectrepairing ⇄ Verb + ingyour shoes ⇄ Object

Passive Voice

Is your shoes being repaired by someone?

– Di mana:

Is ⇄ To be presentyour shoes ⇄ Subjectbeing ⇄ beingrepaired ⇄ V-3by someone ⇄ by Agent

Sehingga, kita dapat mengetahui bahwa someone yang semula merupakan subjek pada active voice berubah menjadi agent (objek) pada passive voice. Begitupula dengan your shoes yang semula merupakan objek pada active voice berubah menjadi subjek pada passive voice.

Soal:

The Great Wall, one of the greatest wonders of the world, was listed as a World Heritage by UNESCO in 1987. Just like a gigantic dragon, the Great Wall winds up and down across deserts, grasslands, mountains and plateaus, stretching approximately 8,851.8 kilometers (5,500 miles) from east to west of China. With a history of more than 2000 years, some of the sections are now in ruins or have disappeared. However, it is still one of the most appealing attractions all around the world owing to its architectural grandeur and historical significance.

Great Wall of China is the longest structure ever built. It was erected entirely by hand. The main part of the wall is about 3,460 kilometres long. One of the highest sections of the Great Wall, on Mount Badaling, near Beijing, rises to about 11 metres high. This section is about

7.5 metres wide at its base and nearly 6 metres at the top. Watchtowers stand about 90 to 180 metres apart along the wall.

A. To identify Great Wall of China
