Construct words

(Menyusun kata)

~

I - looking - to - am - forward - a - share - great - time - together

Answer (Jawaban):

I am looking forward to share a great time together

Explanation (Penjelasan):

Subject + To be (is,am,are) + Verb-ing

"I am looking forward to share a great time together"

Tenses yang digunakan pada kalimat diatas adalah Present Continuous.

25. everybody belives that indonesia will become a great country someday and .... a. i am too b.i will too c.so am i d.i do too


b. i will too.

kalimat di atas disebut eliptical setences yg memiliki rumusan berbentuk positif, dg rumus: subject (everybody believes that indonesia) + WILL (aux verb) + object, AND + subject + aux verb + too.
atau: subject (everybody believes that indonesia) + WILL (aux verb) + object, AND + so+ aux verb + subject.
