dilihat dari teks tersebut setiap kalimat menggunakan simple present tense :
rumus :
*untuk kalimat nominal : subject + To be (am, is, are) + kt. sifat, kt. benda. kt. keterangan
 *untuk kalimat verbal : subject  + Verb 1 + (s/es) 
utk I, YOU, THEY, WE (ingat AYU DEWI) (pokoknya yg jamak)+ verb 1
utk she, he, it (pokoknya yg tunggal) setelah verb ditambah S/ES

kalimat nominal :
-My school is on Jalan Kartini.
-It is nice place.
-There is library, art room, hall room, mosque, computer room, and 18 room classes.
-There are many big trees.
-There is also garden. 
-There are many kinds of flowers. 
-There is pond to fish too. 

kalimat verbal :
-All of students can read book in the library, and they can play music in the art room. 
-We use hall room to morning meeting with all of students. 
-My school has green wall. 
-My friends and I like discuss everything under the tree. 
-I like my school.
possesive(kepunyaan) yang terdapat pd klimat pertama (My) school, modal auxiliaries pd paragrag ke3 trdapat kalimat auxiluaries yaitu:can
present continuouns tense pd kalimat meeting karena berimbuhan ing, degree of comparison atau perbandingan pd kalimat trees karena berimbuhan es/s obejects pd kalimat it is nice place/org ke 3 tunggal (orang yang kita bicarakan)
we adalh jamak dri kami ialah org yg berbicara
