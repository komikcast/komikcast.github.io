4. I. Choose one of the words in brackets.1. Jack was ill last night (because, because of, due to) he did not eat anything a whole day.2. I love Switzerland (as, because, because of) the weather.3. The increase of the cars on the road resulted (in, to, about) a more traffic jams.4. Victor finished his job earlier (due to, because, since) the bad weather.5. She will stay at home (because of, due to, since) she does not have anything to do outside.6. He did not attend the exam, (as a reason, consequently, for) he failed."7. We come to the school late (due to, because, since) the fact that we missed the first bus.8. They can win the competition (because of, due to, as) they always have a very hard exercise every day.9. I cannot go to my teacher's house (as the result, because of, because) she will have another appointment.10. I cannot eat the seafood (as a consequence, since, because of) my allergy11. Dania spends too much time on her gadget (as a reason, consequently, because of) she has to wear glasses.12. I come early intentionally (in order to, because, due to) I want to prepare my presentation.13. I eat organic food (in order to, because, for) stay healthy.14. All flights to and from China were cancelled (so, therefore, due to) the corona virus pandemic.15. Mr. President will pass this road (because of, therefore, since) many security services standing by.​


Jawaban:

1. Jack was ill last night because he did not eat anything a whole day.

(because digunakan jika lanjutannya subject atau kalimat penjelas sebagai penyebab)

2. I love Switzerland because of the weather.

(because of digunakan jika lanjutannya object)

3. The increase of the cars on the road resulted in a more traffic jams.

(in diikuti object a more traffic jams)

4. Victor finished his job earlier due to the bad weather.

(sama dengan because of, digunakan jika lanjutannya object)

5. She will stay at home since she does not have anything to do outside.

(fungsinya sama seperti because, digunakan jika dilanjut dengan subject)

6. He did not attend the exam; consequently, he failed."

(consequently=karena itu)

7. We come to the school late due to the fact that we missed the first bus.

(karena lanjutannya noun phrase)

8. They can win the competition as they always have a very hard exercise every day.

(fungsinya sama seperti because, digunakan jika dilanjut dengan subject)

9. I cannot go to my teacher's house because she will have another appointment.

10. I cannot eat the seafood because of my allergy

11. Dania spends too much time on her gadget; consequently, she has to wear glasses.

12. I come early intentionally because I want to prepare my presentation.

13. I eat organic food in order to stay healthy.

(krn lanjutannya infinitive clause)

14. All flights to and from China were cancelled due to the corona virus pandemic.

15. Mr. President will pass this road; therefore, many security services standing by.

(therefore=karena itu)

Penjelasan :

-Due to, in, dan becuse of adalah preposition yang diikuti objek berupa noun/noun phrase.

-Because, in order to, as adalah subordinating conjunction. yang diikuti clause (S+V).

-Therefore dan consequently adalah conjunctive adverb. Jika digunakan di tengah kalimat (tanpa didahului and), diapit dengan ; dan ,

Penjelasan:

1. What do you know about

They are words used to express a reason in a different ways, based by their grammar.

a. Due to = dikarenakan oleh

b. Because of = karena

c. Thanks to = Terima kasih kepada / karena mu

2. What is the social function of :

a. Due to = dikarenakan oleh

• Berbentuk adjektiva / kata sifat

• Frasa ini digunakan untuk memodifikasi kata benda. Dengan kata lain, karena digunakan untuk menyajikan alasan untuk kata benda

• selalu digunakan setelah form "to be" (is, am, are, was, were, etc)

Ex : The traffic jam was due to a terrible accident at the intersection.

.

Kata traffic jam dijelaskan dengan due to

b. Because of = Karena

• Frasa ini digunakan untuk memodifikasi tindakan. Dengan kata lain, karena digunakan untuk menyajikan alasan suatu tindakan

• tidak digunakan setelah form "to be"

Ex : The traffic jam happened because of a terrible accident at the intersection.

.

Kata happened dijelaskan dengan because of

c. Thanks to = Terima kasih kepada / karena mu

• Digunakan untuk ungkapan kepada sesuatu/seseorang yang bertanggung jawab terhadap suatu hal (bisa jadi ungkapan positif / negatif)

Ex :

(+) My face is a lot better, thanks to the skincare product

(-) I lost all of my money, thanks to the robber

3. Make 3 senteces using

a. Due to :

• My fitness is due to regular exercise

• Her frustation was due to numerous school homeworks

• His happiness is due to his success of getting 100 at Math

• My low score is due to lack of study

b. Because of :

• I wasn't concentrating because of my sleepiness

• We stopped playing football because of the rain

• Stacey moved to Los Angeles because of her job

c. Thanks to :

• The baby has woken up, thanks to your loud scream

• My skin has gotten a lot better, thanks to the serum

• I didn't wake up late, thanks to my mother
