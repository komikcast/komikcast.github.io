14. 3. My hair ......black and long.4. Roberto ........handsome. He...handsome. He..........twelve years old.​


Jawaban:

3. My hair is black and long.

4. Roberto is handsome. He is handsome. He is twelve years old.

Penjelasan:

3. My hair is black and long.

Meskipun rambut (hair) banyak, tapi jumlahnya tidak dapat dihitung (uncountable). Jadi, kita harus menggunakan is.

Semua yang jumlah nya banyak dan jelas (disebutkan ada berapa), harus menggunakan are.

Misalnya: There are three bikes.

Jumlahnya jelas ada tiga, jadi kita harus menggunakan are.

Namun, semua yang jumlahnya banyak, namun tidak jelas, harus menggunakan is.

Misalnya: Rice is healthy, but eat rice too much would cause you get fat.

Kita tidak mengetahui jumlah pasti nasi tersebut. Yang kita tahu, nasi itu banyak, hanya itu. Jadi, kita harus menggunakan is.

4. Roberto adalah seorang lelaki, jadi kita akan menggunakan kata is.

Rule:

I: am

You, they, we: are

He, she, it: is

—

Sekian, hopefully I could help.

Jadikan jawaban terbaik yaa!


15. Apa yang salah dari "she became well -known since junior high school when she sings in front of her friends.


Kelas: SMA
Pelajaran: English
Kategori: grammar review
Pembahasan: 

The mistake of the sentence:
'She became well-known since junior high school when she sings in front of her friends.' is that
- the use of since should be only for present prefect tense as it means 'until now' 
- the phrase 'when she sings ...' should be in past tense as it refers to her past experience.

So, the right one is 
' She has become well-known since junior high school when she sang in front of her friends.'
