The narrator tells us about 3 (three) elements of a story. They are characters, conflict, and resolution.

[tex] \\ [/tex]

PEMBAHASAN

Three elements of story (tiga struktur dalam cerita):

characters (karakter) → merupakan bagian perkenalan karakter, dari sikap, watak, hingga yang lainnya.conflict (konflik) → awal dan akhir permasalahan atau perseturuan cerita.resolution (resolusi) → penanganan konflik cerita sekaligus ending-nya.

A phrase is a group of words that forms a unit of meaning within a sentence. Phrases can be short or long, and they can function as nouns, verbs, adjectives, or adverbs. There are several different types of phrases, including:

Noun phrases: A noun phrase consists of a noun and any words that modify it. For example, "the tall oak tree" is a noun phrase because "tree" is the noun and "tall" and "oak" are adjectives that modify it.Verb phrases: A verb phrase consists of a main verb and any auxiliaries that accompany it.
