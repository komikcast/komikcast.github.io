34. Danada says i like music. I can play the piano and violin but i cannot paly the guitar according to danada she? The piano and violin however she us? Paly the guitar


Penyelesaian :

According to Denada, she can play the piano and violin, however, she cannot play the guitar.

The word "can" is used to indicate the ability to do something. In this case, Denada is saying that she is able to play the piano and violin. The word "however" is used to introduce a contrast. In this case, Denada is saying that she is not able to play the guitar, even though she is able to play the piano and violin.

The word "us" is not grammatically correct in this sentence. The correct verb form is "can" or "cannot".

Here is the corrected sentence:

> Danada says, "I like music. I can play the piano and violin, but I cannot play the guitar."
