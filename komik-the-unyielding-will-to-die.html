Maap klo slh:v

FORM

In a Type 3 conditional sentence, the tense in the 'if' clause is the past perfect, and the tense in the main clause is the perfect conditional or the perfect continuous conditional.

1. If clause (condition)

2. Main clause (result)

3. If + past perfect

perfect conditional or perfect continuous conditional

If this thing had happened

that thing would have happened.

As in all conditional sentences, the order of the clauses is not fixed. You may have to rearrange the pronouns and adjust punctuation when you change the order of the clauses, but the meaning is identical.

40. If they had taken him to hospital earlier, he ...a. would have die b. would have diedc. wouldn’t have diedd. will diee. would die


MAPEL : BAHASA INGGRIS
MATERI : CONDITIONAL SENTENCES

SOAL DAN PEMBAHASAN
=============================
If they had taken him to hospital earlier, he...

=> Dari kalimat di atas kita sudah bisa ketahui bahwa ini merupakan conditional sentence type 3 karena IF clause nya menggunakan past perfect, yaitu "IF THEY HAD TAKEN", yang mana rumus dari CONDITIONAL SENTENCE TYPE 3 adalah :
If + past perfect, conditional II (=would + have + past participle)

Jadi jawabannya adalah (C)
WOULDN'T HAVE DIED

dimana artinya adalah :
Jika mereka membawanya ke RS lebih awal/cepat, maka dia tidak akan mati.
