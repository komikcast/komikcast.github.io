7. the coach looks so upset because of his team fail the game. simple past tense dari teks tersebut adalah​


Jawaban:

The coach looked so upset because his team failed the game.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat dalam bentuk simple past tense yang tepat dari teks tersebut adalah "The coach looked so upset because his team failed the game."

Dalam kalimat tersebut, kata kerja utama yang menunjukkan tindakan adalah "looked" yang merupakan bentuk lampau dari kata kerja "look" (melihat). Kata kerja "looked" menunjukkan bahwa tindakan tersebut terjadi di masa lampau.

Selanjutnya, kata keterangan waktu "so upset" menyatakan keadaan emosional yang dialami oleh pelatih (coach) pada saat itu. Kata sifat "upset" menggambarkan perasaan atau emosi yang tidak senang atau kecewa.

Terakhir, frasa "because his team failed the game" menjelaskan alasan mengapa pelatih terlihat begitu sedih. Kata kerja "failed" merupakan bentuk lampau dari kata kerja "fail" (gagal). Frasa ini menyatakan bahwa tim pelatih gagal dalam pertandingan tersebut.

Dengan demikian, kalimat "The coach looked so upset because his team failed the game" menggambarkan keadaan pelatih yang sedih karena timnya kalah dalam pertandingan.

PEMBAHASAN Simple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian di masa lampau. Keterangan menunjukkan waktu lampau adalah this morning, yesterday, last, ago dll.

Rumus Past tense

1. Verbal sentence adalah kalimat yang ada kata kerja

(+) Subject + Verb2 + Object(-) Subject + did + not + Verb1 + Object(?) Did + Subject + verb1 + object

2. Nominal sentence adalah kalimat yg tak ada kata kerja

(+) Subject + was/were + Complement (-) Subject + was/were + not + Complement (?) was/were + Subject + Complement

KESIMPULAN

Dad eats his breakfast in the yard(this morning) menjadi past tense

➡ Dad ate his breakfast in the yard this morning Keterangan:Subject => DadV1s eats => V2 ateObject => his breakfast Adverb of place => in the yard Adverb of time => This morning

34. put each verb between brackets in the PAST SIMPLE or in the PAST PERFECT TENSE.- after he (have) his breakfast he (go) away.


JAWAB:

put each verb between brackets in the PAST SIMPLE or in the PAST PERFECT TENSE.

After he had had his breakfast he went away.

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

 

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent?

10. (+) My father was in the office yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office yesterday afternoon?  

Past Perfect Tense

Past perfect tense adalah suatu tense yang menggambarkan suatu kejadian yang terjadi sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau dan telah selesai sebelum kejadian lain yang terjadi di masa lampau juga. Tense ini terdiri dari gabungan aspek perfect (sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau) dan bingkai waktu past (masa lampau).

Rumus Past Perfect Tense

Tense ini dibentuk dengan bentuk masa lampau dari auxiliary verb have atau has, yaitu had dan past participle (verb 3). Semua subjek baik singular atau plural menggunakan had.

Positif

Subjek +  had + verb 3  

Contoh :

They had lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time He had learned English for one year

Negatif

Subjek +  had + not + verb 3  

Contoh:

They had not lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time He had not learned English for one year

Interogatif

Had+ subjek + verb 3?  

Contoh:

Had they lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time Had he learned English for one year?

Joko and his friends were not swimming in the river (simple past continuous tense) Joko and his friends didn't swim in the river (past tense)

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

PEMBAHASANSimple Past Tense

▓ Definisi ▓

Simple Past Tense adalah bentuk waktu yang digunakan untuk mengungkapkan suatu aktifitas/peristiwa yang dilakukan di masa lampau. Contoh:

Aku belajar bahasa Inggris kemarin (I studied English yesterday) Aku membuat bubur ayam tadi pagi (I made chicken porridge this morning)

▓ Pattern ▓

(+) S + V2 + O/C(–) S + did + not + V2 + O/C(?) Did + S + V2 + O/C+ ?

⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜⚜

Simple Past Continuous Tense

▓ Definisi ▓

Simple Past Tense adalah bentuk waktu yang digunakan untuk menyatakan/mengungkapkan suatu kegiatan/pekerjaan yang sedang dilakukan di masa lampau. Contoh:

Aku sedang belajar bahasa Inggris kemarin (I was studying English yesterday) Aku sedang membuat bubur ayam tadi pagi (I was making chicken this morning)

▓ Pattern ▓

(+) S + was/were + V-ing + O/C(–) S + was/were + not + V-ing + O/C(?) Was/Were + S + V-ing + O/C + ?

▓ Keterangan ▓

To be past terbagi menjadi dua, yaitu:

Was ➻ I, He, She, It, dan Nama orang/benda tunggalWere ➻ You, We, They, dan Nama orang/benda banyak
