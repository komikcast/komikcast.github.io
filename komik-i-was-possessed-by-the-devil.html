20. A ball has a mass of 10 Kg, suppose it travels at 100 m/s. Find the kinetic energy possessed by it​


Answer:

Kinetic Energy = 50.000 Joule or 50 KiloJoule

Explanation:

mass=10 kg

velocity=100m/s

Kinetic Energy = [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex]×m×v²

Kinetic Energy = [tex]\frac{1}{2}[/tex]×10 kg×(100m/s)²

Kinetic Energy = 50.000 Joule = 50 KJ
