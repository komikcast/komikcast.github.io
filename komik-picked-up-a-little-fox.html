29. simple past tense+my father picked me up-?bantu jawab ya teman² plis​


Jawaban:

Simple Past Tense:

(+) My Father picked me up.

(-) My Father did not pick me up.

(?) Did my Father pick me up? / Did your Father pick you up?

Penjelasan:

Jawaban dicetak tebal dan merupakan materi pembelajaran bentuk kalimat Simple Past Tense karena menjelaskan aktivitas kegiatan di masa lampau dalam Bahasa Inggris.

Simple Past Tense:

Kalimat Verbal Positif:

(+) Subject + verb 2 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(+) We attended the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Negatif:

(-) Subject + did not + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)

(-) We did not attend the festival last week

Kalimat Verbal Interogatif:

(?) Did + subject + Verb 1 + object + adverb of time (yesterday / last week)?

(?) Did we attend the festival last week?
