Saya tidak bisa melafalkan motor saat saya kecil.Translate the sentence above into English!Answer:5. Stating capability adalah kalimat yang menyatakan kemampuan melakukan sesuatu, sedangkan stating willingness adalah kalimat yang menyatakan kemauan melakukan sesuatu.

1. How do we say our willingness in the present?

Answer:  I will attend her birthday party

2. Make a sentence about your abilities in the present or future!

Answer:  Can you play the guitar?

3. Mention the football player abilities!

Answer:  He can run fast, he can pass and dribble the ball, he can shoot the ball.

4. Saya tidak bisa melafalkan motor saat saya kecil.

Translate the sentence above into English!

Answer:  I could not ride a motorcycle when I was kid

5. Saya ingin membeli komik Naruto tetapi saya tidak punya uang.

Translate the sentence above into English!

Answer:​ I buy Naruto comics but I don't have money.

Pembahasan

Stating capabilities

1. Dalam bentuk present tense/masa sekarang. (Subject+can+verb 1)

She can win the singing contestHe can play the badminton

2. Dalam bentuk past tense/bentuk lampau. Selain past tense, could dimaksudkan supaya lebih sopan (polite) bukan bentuk past.  (Subject+could+verb 1)

Mother could make delicious cakesThey could play the instrumentsCould they sing?

Stating willingness

Will dan would memiliki makna yang sama. Perbedaannya adalah Will digunakan untuk bentuk present tense dan would untuk past tense.

I will go to the schoolHe will play footballI could make a poemJohn could count until ten
