9. A tennis ball is thrown upward with initial velocity of 30 m/s, if g = 10 m/s2. Determine: a. height of the tennis ball at the first 2 seconds.b. velocity of the tennis ball at the first 2 seconds.c. time required by the tennis ball to reach the highest point.​


Jawaban:

a. 40m

b. [tex]40m/s[/tex]

c. 3 second

Penjelasan:

KINEMATICS : PROJECTILE MOTION

Known :

Vo = [tex]30 m/s[/tex]

g = [tex]10 m/s^{2}[/tex]

a. height at t=2

[tex]h=Vot-\frac{1}{2}gt^{2}[/tex]

[tex]h= (30)(2) -\frac{1}{2}(10)(2)^{2}[/tex]

[tex]h=40m[/tex]

b. Vt at t=2

[tex]Vt= Vot-gt[/tex]

[tex]Vt = (30)(2) - (10)(2)[/tex]

[tex]Vt = 40 m/s[/tex]

c. time required to reach its highest point (t)

[tex]t= \frac{Vo}{g}[/tex]

[tex]t = 3s[/tex]

terjadi pada masa lalu (in the past);adanya penggunaan time signal yang menunjukkan suatu peristiwa di masa lalu (cth: once upon a time)didasari penggunaan past tense (cth: there lived a beautiful girl and her name was Cinderella)isi dapat berupa fakta/fiksidisajikan secara kronologis (cth: dari Cinderella sebagai pembantu bisa menjadi tuan putri) dan sering ditemukan konjungsi temporal (cth: one day, when, then, after that, so, and so next)terdapat nilai-nilai di dalamnya dan unsur intrinsik dan ekstrinsik

36. During a sale, Mike paid $72.90 for a tennis racket which was sold at a discount of 10%. What was the usual price of the tennis racket?​


Jawaban:

$81

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

To find the usual price of the tennis racket, we can use the formula:

Usual Price = Sale Price / (1 - Discount Percentage)

In this case, the sale price is $72.90 and the discount is 10%.

Let's calculate the usual price:

Usual Price = $72.90 / (1 - 0.10)

Usual Price = $72.90 / 0.90

Usual Price = $81

Therefore, the usual price of the tennis racket is $81.
