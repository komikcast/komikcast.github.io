1 kg = 10 ounces
2:3 have 5 as a comparative amount

type of sugar 6000/10 = 600 per ounce
2/5 x 10 ouces = 4 ounces
4 ounces x 600 = 2400

type of sugar 6400/10 = 640 per ounce
3/5 x 10 ounces = 6 ounces
6 x 640 = 3480

the price mixtured sugar:
2400 + 3480 = 6240
profit 20% = 20% x 6240 = 1248
so buying price of 1 kg mixtured sugar is
6240 + 1248 = 7488

23. in a solution of sugar, sugar and water are sequentially called


Jawaban:

In a solution of sugar, the sugar and water are sequentially called:

1. Solvent: Water is considered the solvent in this solution. The solvent is the component in a solution that dissolves the other substance, which, in this case, is sugar. Water has the ability to dissolve many substances, making it a universal solvent.

2. Solute: Sugar is the solute in the solution. The solute is the substance that is dissolved in the solvent to form a homogeneous mixture. In this case, sugar is being dissolved in water.

Together, the combination of the solvent (water) and the solute (sugar) forms a sugar solution.

37. To make 25 cakes, you need :200 g sugar 3/4kg flourHow much sugar and flour would you need to make 30 cakes?A. 250 g sugar and 4/5kg flour B. 250 g sugar and 9/10kg flour C. 240 g sugar and 4/5kg flourD. 240 g sugar and 9/10kg flour


Jawaban:

gula : 200 g ÷ 25 = 8g

tepung : 3/4 kg = 750 g

750 g ÷ 25 = 30 g

jadi untuk 30 kue =

gula : 8g x 30 = 240g

tepung : 30g x 30 = 900g

900g : 1000 g = 9/10 kg

jawabannya yang D

Jawaban:

d. 240 g sugar and 9/10kg flour

Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

dalam satu cake membutuhkan :

200 : 25 = 8 g sugar

3/4 = 0,75 : 25 = 0,03 kg flour

penyelesaian :

8 × 30 = 240 g sugar0,03 × 30 = 0,9 = 9/10

jadi, untuk membuat 30 cake, membutuhkan 240 g sugar dan 9/10 kg flour
