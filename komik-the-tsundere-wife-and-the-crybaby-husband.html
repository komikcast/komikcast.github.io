10. One day there were a husband and wife. They have agreed that tomorrow is the day that they will commit suicide. they also have agreed that they will bring the things which they love most on a bag. Finally both of them looked for a very high building and they also have agreed to fall together. Eventually her husband jumps up but his wife does not, because his wife was afraid. His wife only saw her husband fall from the building. But her husband pulled out a parachute inside her bag. The question is who cheated whom?


Her husband is the one who cheated, cause his wife is afraid.Answer:
In essence, they both cheat. The reason, Both have agreed suicide but when her husband had jumped from the top of the building, his wife did not jump for fear. And logically it must be only her husband who died and his wife lives. But on the contrary, if his wife jumps off the top of the building with her husband, the one who will die is his wife, because her husband has prepared a parachute to anticipate that he does not fall to the bottom of the building.
In conclusion, both of them are cheat.

Intinya, mereka sama-sama curang. Alasannya, Keduanya telah menyepakati bunuh diri namun saat suaminya meloncat dari atas gedung, istrinya tidak loncat karena takut. Dan secara logika pastilah hanya suaminya yang meninggal dan istrinya tetap hidup. Tapi sebaliknya, jika istrinya melompat dari atas gedung dengan suaminya, orang yang akan mati adalah istrinya, karena suaminya telah menyiapkan parasut untuk mengantisipasi bahwa ia tidak jatuh ke dasar bangunan.
Kesimpulannya, keduanya curang.
