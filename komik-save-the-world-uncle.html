8. The visit my uncle in the village. (+)....... ..... (-) .............


Simple Present Tense (+) They visit my uncle in the village.(-) They do not visit my uncle in the village.(?) Do They visit my uncle in the village?

PEMBAHASAN Simple Present Tense

➡ Digunakan utk menunjukkan Peristiwa yg terjadi di masa sekarang (Present), Kegiatan berulang/rutin, kebiasaan (habitual) dan utk menyatakan kebenaran umum (fakta).

Rumus Present Tense

* (+) S + V1 (-s/es) + O/C

* (-) S + Do/Does + not + V1 + O/C

* (?) Do/Does + S + V1 + O/C?

Catatan

Subject + Auxiliary

➡ I, you, we, they + do

➡ He, She, it + does

40. People, clothes, gucci, the world, houses, a man, leather bags, police, suitcases, the shop, father, uncle, wife, products, members, family, company.manakah yg termasuk countable namun dan uncountable ?​


Jawaban:

Countable:

- people

- clothes

- the world

- houses

- a man

- leather bags

- police

- suitcases

- the shop

- father

- uncle

- wife

- products

- members

- family

- company

Uncountable:

- gucci

Penjelasan:

Gucci is divined as "uncountable" karena adjective. Kata tersebut merujuk pada sebuah ekspresi yang bermakna "fancy" (bagus, keren, dsb) and "very exclusive" (sangat exclusif). Jadi, kata "guci" adalah kata sifat (adj) yang secaamra otomatis tidak dapat dihitung (uncountable).
