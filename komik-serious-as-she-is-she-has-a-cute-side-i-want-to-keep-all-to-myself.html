She (forgot/ forget) to take an umbrella, so she (have/had) to use a big leaf to keep dry. Then, she got very thirsty. She forgot to take her water bottle, so she (had/ has) to (use/ used) her hat to get water from a waterfall. She was tired. She () ... (forgot/ forget) to take a sleeping bag, so she (7) (need/needed) to put her (blanket sleeping bag) between two trees. She (try/tried) to go home, but she got lost because she didn't have a map. She needed to (went/go) West, so she looked at the sun to find her way home.Carol is a very clever girl! 19 (10)​


Jawaban atas teks rumpang di atas:

Started forgotHad Use Forgot BlanketTriedWent

Pembahasan:

Teks lengkapnya:
Carol is a clever girl, but she always forgot things. One day, she went hiking in the jungle. It started to rain. She forgot to take an umbrella, so she had to use a big leaf to keep dry. Then, she got very thirsty. She forgot to take her water bottle, so she had to use her hat to get water from a waterfall. She was tired. She forgot to take a sleeping bag, so she needed to put her blanket between two trees. She tried to go home, but she got lost because she didn't have a map. She needed to went  West, so she looked at the sun to find her way home. Carol is a very clever girl! Dalam teks tersebut menceritakan kejadian masa lampau yang dialami oleh Carol dan kejadian tersebut sudah selesai di masa lampau pula. Maka rumus grammar atau tata bahasa yang digunakan adalah Simple Past tense. Dalam simple past tense pola kalimat yang digunakan adalah:

                          Subjek + Verb 2 + Objek
Kata kerja atau verb yang digunakan pada kalimat past tense adalah kata kerja bentuk kedua (V2)Nomor 4 menggunakan use karena di depan sudah menggunakan had (menunjukan bentuk lampau).
