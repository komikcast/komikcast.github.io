34. Task 1: Study the following pairs of sentences to identify the use of be (am, is, arel and have (have, has) Do you notice the differences? Examples: 1. a. I am sixteen years old b. 1 have an older sister and a younger brother ​


Jawaban:

i am a student

she is a student

you are a student

i have a dog

she/he has a dog

Jawaban :

TO BE

I am a teacher.She is at homeThey are twins

VERB

She has a new bag.I have enough money.

PEMBAHASAN

To be am/are/is adalah jenis to be yang dipakai dalam present tense. Dimana to be tersebut memiliki perbedaan pemakaian tergantung dari subject yang dipakai. Perhatikan berikut ini :

To be am digunakan jika subject nya "I".To be are digunakan jika subject nya you/they/we/plural subject.To be is digunakan untuk subject kata ganti orang ketiga tunggal seperti she/he/it/nama orang/benda tunggal.

Contoh :

I am a student.

They are friends.

You are studying English.

We are at the library.

John and Lina are reading a book.

She is a beuatiful girl.

He is fourteen years old.

It is an apple.

John is a pilot.

Selain to be dalam present tense juga ada pemakaian auxiliary verb have/has yaitu di present perfect. Atau kata ini juga bisa dijadikan sebagai verb di kalimat simple present verbal. Untuk penggunaannya yaitu :

Gunakan have jika subject nya I/you/they/we/plural subject.Gunakan has jika subject nya orang ketiga tunggal seperti she/he/it/nama orang tunggal.

Contoh sebagai auxiliary verb dalam "present perfect".

They have studied English.She has cooked a delicious food.

Contoh sebagai verb dalam "simple present".

You have a new bag.She has a lot of friends.

Jadi, kesimpulannya :

Untuk to be "am" hanya untuk subject "I".To be "are" untuk subject you/they/we/plural subject.To be "is" untuk subject orang ketiga tunggal seperti she/he/it.Gunakan have jika subject nya I/you/they/we/plural subject.Gunakan has jika subject nya orang ketiga tunggal seperti she/he/it/nama orang/benda tunggal.
