17. story telling about the youth pledge


Jawaban:

The Youth Pledge is a major milestone in the history of the Indonesian Independence movement. October 28 is celebrated as Youth Pledge Day, this is because the Youth Pledge was held on October 27-28, 1928 in Batavia (Jakarta). The Youth Pledge was a decision of the Second Youth Congress and this pledge was considered as a spirit to affirm the confiscation of the founding of the Indonesian state. The Youth Pledge itself was formulated and written by Mr. Prof. Mohammad Yamin, S.H. on a sheet of paper.

Fill in the Youth Pledge

The second youth congress was attended by various participants, namely representatives of Indonesian students (PPPI), Jong Java, Jong Sumatranen Bond, Jong Bataks Bond, Jong Islamienten Bond, Indonesian Youth, Jong Celebes, Jong Ambon and others.

The building, which is located on Jl Keramat Raya 106 Central Jakarta, is the venue for the congress, which currently houses the Youth Pledge Museum.

In the Congress there were 3 important decisions, namely the Youth Pledge which read:

1. Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, mengaku bertumpah darah yang satu tanah air Indonesia.

2. Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, mengaku berbangsa yang satu bangsa Indonesia.

3. Kami putra dan putri Indonesia, menjunjung bahasa persatuan bahasa Indonesia.

( 1. We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, claim to be spilled by the blood of one Indonesian homeland.

2. We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, claim to be one Indonesian nation.

3. We, the sons and daughters of Indonesia, uphold the unified Indonesian language.)

The Purpose of the Youth Pledge

After knowing the brief history and contents of the Youth Pledge, we must also know what the true purpose of the Youth Pledge is.

This time, Geniora news will summarize the purpose of holding the Youth Pledge:

1. Awakening the spirit and attitude of nationalism of the Indonesian youth and all Indonesian people to fight, expel and oppose the colonialists.

2. Expanding activities to finance activities in order to achieve Indonesian Independence.

3. Carry out the aspiration to gather all Indonesian youth.

4. Creating a strong and thick sense of unity and integrity of the Indonesian nation.

The Meaning of the Youth Pledge

The meaning of the Youth Pledge is of course very important to be implanted in the soul, the contents of the Youth Pledge affirm the ideals of the existence of "Indonesian Homeland", "Indonesian Nation", "Indonesian Language".

Here are some of the meanings of the Youth Pledge that has been summarized by Geniora News:

1. Teach the values of national unity.

2. A form of love for the country.

3. Encourage youth fighting spirit.

4. As an invitation to maintain the integrity of the Indonesian nation

5. Fostering pride as the Indonesian nation.

6. Emphasize pride in the Indonesian language.

27. Contoh kalimat Superlative dari kata The Strongest​


Adjective: Strong
Comperlative: Stronger
Superlative: Strongest

Contoh kalimat menggunakan (strongest):

He is the strongest man at this place.
(Ia adalah org yg paling kuat di tmpt ini)

Comparative and superlative degree .

Comparative > to compare one person or thing to another . Comparative is usually followed by "than"

Superlative > the only one .


We put -er at the end of adjective for one syllable. Otherwise, we put most at the begining of adjective if the adjective has more one syllable.

Example for one syllable :

Cheap, strong, fast, slow and many more.


Example for more one syllable :

Expensive, beautiful and many more.



Let's take a look at your question, thereby you will understand it well.


Strong>>>>stronger>>>strongest

(1) >>>>>>>>(2)>>>>>>>>>(3)


1. The lion is strong

2. The lion is stronger than a deer .

3. The lion is the strongest animal in the jungle.

Dengan menggunakan set persamaan yang diberikan:

2x + 2y - z = 8 ...(1)

3y - z = 10 ...(2)

5x - 2y = -2 ...(3)

Pertama-tama kita dapat menyelesaikan y dalam hal z dari persamaan (2):

3y - z = 10

3y = z + 10

y = (1/3)z + (10/3)

Selanjutnya, kita dapat mengganti persamaan di atas ke dalam persamaan (3) dan menyederhanakannya menjadi:

5x - 2((1/3)z + (10/3)) = -2

5x - (2/3)z - (20/3) = -2

5x - (2/3)z = (2/3)

Akhirnya, kita dapat mengganti nilai y dalam hal z dari persamaan (2) ke dalam persamaan (1) dan menyederhanakannya untuk mendapatkan:

2x + 2((1/3)z + (10/3)) - z = 8

2x + (2/3)z + (20/3) - z = 8

2x - (1/3)z + (20/3) = 8

2x - (1/3)z = (4/3)

Sekarang kita memiliki dua persamaan dalam dua variabel, yang dapat diselesaikan dengan metode apa pun yang sesuai. Sebagai contoh, kita bisa mengalikan persamaan kedua dengan 3 dan menambahkannya ke persamaan pertama untuk mengeliminasi z:

5x - (2/3)z + 9y - 3z = (2/3) + 30

5x + 9((1/3)z + (10/3)) - 4z = (68/3)

5x + (3/3)z + (30/3) - 4z = (68/3)

5x - z + (10/3) = (68/3)

5x - z = (18/3) = 6

x : y : z = 2 : (1/3)z + (10/3) : z

x : y : z = 2 : 1/3 : 1
