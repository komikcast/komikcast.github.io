35. Contoh legend,fable,fairy tale,folk tale,myth


Subject : English
Category : Reading (narrative)
Keywords : Legend, fairy tale, folktale, myth, fable

Legends are made up stories about historical figures.
Examples of legend :
1. King Arthur and the Knights of the Round Table
2. Queen Boadicea
3. Robin Hood

Fables are stories that feature animals with human-like qualities. Fables are usually used to deliver moral lesson.
Examples of fables :
1. The Tortoise and The Hare
2. The Ant and The Grasshopper
3. The Lion and The Mouse

Fairy tales are stories that features magical creatures like fairies, elves, goblins, etc and magical events. Fairy tales happen in magical lands.
Examples of fairy tales :
1. Cinderella
2. Rumpelstiltskin
3. Beauty and The Beast

Folktales are stories that are passed down from generation to generation. Like fables, folktales also teach moral lesson. However the main characters are human.
Examples of folktales :
1. The Boy Who Cried Wolf
2. Daedalus and Icarus
3. Issunboshi

Myths are traditional stories usually about the early history of humans, explaining natural phenomena or about gods and deities.
Examples of myths :
1. Izanagi and Izanami
2. Thor
3. Mulajadi na Bolon

39. What is the defonion of a fairy tale


A fairy tale is a type of short story that typically features folkloric fantasy characters, such as dwarves, dragons, elves, fairies, giants, gnomes, goblins, griffins, mermaids, talking animals, trolls, unicorns, or witches, and usually magic or enchantments. Definition of fairy tale
1
a : a story (as for children) involving fantastic forces and beings (such as fairies, wizards, and goblins) enjoyed the fairy tale “The Little Mermaid” —called also fairy story
b : a story in which improbable events lead to a happy ending
those balletic fairy tales in which a new corps member steps in for an injured principal at the last minute and delivers a splendid performance — Margaret Willis
2
: a made-up story usually designed to mislead
an old-fashioned fairy tale depicting revolutionists as demigods — Jonathan Zimmerman
