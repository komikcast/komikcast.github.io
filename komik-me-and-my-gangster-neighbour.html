3. 4. When my father locked the door, ....a. I am talking to my neighbour.b. I was talking to my neighbour.c. I will be talking to my neighbour.d. I talk to my neighbour.​


Jawaban:

b. I was talking to my neighbour.

Pembahasan

Past-Past Continuous Tense

Past Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg terjadi di masa lampau. Rumusnya:

(+) S + V2 + O/C(-) S + did not + V1 + O/C(?) Did + S + V1 + O/C?

Past Continuous Tense

Digunakan untuk menyatakan Kegiatan yg sedang berlangsung di masa lampau. Rumusnya:

(+) S + was/were + V-ing + O/C(-) S + was/were + not + V-ing + O/C(?) was/were + S + V-ing + O/C?

Kesimpulan Past Tense

(+) S + V2 + O/C

=> my father + locked + the door,

Past Continuous Tense

(+) S + was/were + V-ing + O/C

=> b. I was + talking + to my neighbour.

Jadi

When My father locked the door, I was talking to my neighbor.
