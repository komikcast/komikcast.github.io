27. 11.(+) Lyla is my friend(?) .....?a. Are Lyla my friend?b. Did Lyla my friend?c. Do Lyla my friend?d. Does Lyla my friend?e. Is Lyla my friend?​


Jawaban:

E. Is Lyla my friend?

Penjelasan:

Simple Present Tense

(+) S + is/am/are + Complement

(-) S + is/am/are + Not + Complement

(?) Is/am/are + S + Complement

(+) Lyla is my friend

(-) Lyla isn't my friend

(?) Is Lyla my friend?
