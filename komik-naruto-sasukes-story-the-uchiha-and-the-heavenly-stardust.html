11. where the story takes place is the story of the lion and mouse​


Jawaban:

The story of "The Lion and the Mouse" is one of Aesop's Fables. Like most fables, the exact location isn't specified, but it's generally understood to take place in a forest or jungle, as that's where both lions and mice can be found. The key locations in the story would be the lion's den, where the lion sleeps, and the surrounding area where the mouse accidentally disturbs the lion. Remember, the setting of fables is often less important than the moral or lesson they're trying to teach. In this case, the story teaches that no act of kindness, however small, is ever wasted.
