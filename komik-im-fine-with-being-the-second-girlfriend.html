Jawaban

1. Jane went on holiday with one of her friends.

⤷ Jane went on holiday with a friend of hers.

2. We played football with one of our neighbors.

⤷ We played football with a neighbor of ours.

3. My sister borrowed one of my necklaces.

⤷ My sister borrowed a necklace of mine.

POSSESSIVE PRONOUN

Possessive pronouns merupakan kata ganti yang menunjukkan kepemilikan atas sesuatu. Berbeda seperti possessive adjectives yang mengikuti kata benda (noun), possessive pronouns tidak perlu mengikuti kata benda (noun).

Possessive Pronoun List

                           Possessive Adjective               Possessive Pronoun

1st person                         My                                          Mine

2nd person                      Your                                       Yours

3rd person (m)                   His                                          His

3rd person (f)                     Her                                         Hers

3rd person thing                Its                                       (tidak ada)

1st person plural                Our                                         Ours

2nd person plural            Your                                         Yours

3rd person plural             Their                                        Theirs

Catatan:  

M: Male (laki-laki) F: Female (perempuan) Possessive pronoun untuk 3rd person thing (it) tidak ada

Contoh:  

Your bag is bigger than my bag. Yours is bigger mine. Her books are more colorful than his. Our classroom is cleaner than theirs. This is your pen. It is yours.It is our pool. The pool is ours.

39. rina :"good morning rino "rino ....how are you ? rina :Im fine how about you ?"rino :Im fine too​


Jawaban:

rina :"good morning rino "

rino : Good morning how are you ?

rina :Im fine how about you ?"

rino :Im fine too

Artinya:

Rina:"Selamat pagi, Rino"

Rino:"Selamat pagi, bagaimana kabarmu?"

Rina:"Aku baik, bagaimana denganmu?"

Rino:"Aku baik juga".

>>Pembahasan:

Karena awalnya si Rina mengucapkan Good morning, maka orang yang meresponnya (Si Rino), juga harus mengucapkan Good morning.

Good morning=>> Selamat pagi. Kata ini merupakan sapaan/salam ketika cuacanya cocok untuk menyambung salam tersebut.

Good night=>>Selamat malam (tidur)

Good morning=>>Selamat pagi

Good Afternoon=>> Selamat sore/siang

Good day=>>Selamat siang

Good evening=>>Selamat malam(ketika bertemu sebagai salam pembuka di waktu malam).
