SIMPLE PRESENT TENSEPositive

S + Verb 1 + Compliment or Object.

Negative

S + Do/Does + not + Verb 1 + Compliment or Object.

Interrogative

Do/Does + S + Verb 1 + Compliment or Object?

NB: Subjek tunggal pada verbnya ditambahkan imbuhan -s/-es.

39. Father :"so,you want to go to USA." Son :"That's right,Dad." Father :What's your plan there if I allow you? Son : I want to study there.I'd like to take Law field at Harvard,Dad? But i wonder what will i do for living?.................,Dad? Father : .................. you can have part time jobs such as,librarian,cashier and others. Son : ok, Dad ..................... will be alright when i'm there. Father : ................... my son.


Father : "So, you want to go to USA."

Son : "That's right, Dad."

Father : "What's your plan there if I allow you? "

Son : "I want to study there. I'd like to take Law field at Harvard, Dad? But i wonder what will i do for living? What do you think, Dad? "

Father : "I think you can have part time jobs such as, librarian, cashier and others. "

Son : "Ok, Dad don't worry I will be alright when i'm there."

Father : "Good luck my son."

Pada soal kita diminta untuk melengkapi kata dalam suatu dialog.

Dialog ini menceritakan tentang seorang anak yang memiliki keinginan untuk sekolah di luar negeri namun belum memiliki gambaran bagaimana untuk mencukupi hidup sendiri.

Sehingga ia meminta pendapat kepada ayahnya.

Meminta pendapat dalam bahasa inggris adalah asking opinion :

"What do you think, Dad? "

Memberikan pendapat dalam bahasa inggris adalah giving opinion :

"I think you can have part time jobs"
