7. Explain the difference between a material and a substanc Is glass a material or a substance? Say why. a How are sand and pure water similar? b How are they different? ​


Penjelasan:

A material refers to a substance that can be used to create objects, while a substance is a specific type of matter with consistent properties. Glass is both a material and a substance. It's a substance because it has a defined composition and properties. It's also a material because it can be used to create various objects.

a. Sand and pure water are similar in that they are both substances found in nature. Sand is made up of tiny rock and mineral particles, while pure water consists of H2O molecules.

b. They differ in their physical states and properties. Sand is a solid, while pure water is a liquid. Additionally, sand doesn't dissolve in water and has a gritty texture, whereas pure water is transparent and has a neutral taste.
