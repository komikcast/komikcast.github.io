14. At a pediatric clinic, there were 11 toddlers who were each b years old. Sally was the only child who was 5 years old. What was the total age of the children in the clinic? Express your answer in terms of b.


Jawaban:

Since there were 11 toddlers, and each toddler was b years old, the total age of the children in the clinic can be calculated by multiplying the number of toddlers by their age:

Total age = Number of toddlers * Age of each toddler

Total age = 11 * b

However, it is mentioned that Sally was the only child who was 5 years old. Therefore, we need to subtract her age from the total age obtained above:

Total age = 11 * b - 5

So, the total age of the children in the clinic, expressed in terms of b, is 11b - 5.
