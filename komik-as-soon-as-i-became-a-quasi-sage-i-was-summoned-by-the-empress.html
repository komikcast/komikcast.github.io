9. part of speech for As soon as the light comes back, I will finish my homework. is


Answer:

The part of speech is the function of words in the sentence. The part of speech known in  the English are noun, verb, adjective, adverb, pronoun, connectors, auxiliary verb so on.

Here's the part of speech for that sentence:

As soon as ----} connector

The light ----} article + noun

comes back -----} phrasal verb

I ------} pronoun

will ------} auxiliary verb

finish ------} verb

my ------} possessive pronoun

homework -----} noun.


Kelas: SMP

Mapel: English

Kategori: part of speech identification
