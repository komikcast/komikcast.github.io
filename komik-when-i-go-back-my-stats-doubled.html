6. My mother was cooking when i... Back home yesterday A. Come B. Came C. Comes D. Coming


came

maaf kalau salah

Jawab: B. Came

Pembahasan:

My mother was cooking when i... Back home yesterday

= ibuku sedang makan saat aku... rumah kemarin

A. Come = Verb 1 utk subject Plural

B. Came = Verb 2 utk Past Tense

C. Comes = Verb 1 utk subject singular

D. Coming = Verb+ing Continues Tense

* Alasannya memilih Jawaban B.

Karena terdapat keterangan waktu “yesterday” (kemarin) yg menunjukkan waktu lampau. Maka gunakan Rumus Past Tense :

(+) S + VERB 2 + O

= I + came + back home yesterday

* V1 come = V2 came

Jadi:

My mother was cooking when i CAME Back home yesterday

= ibuku sedang makan saat aku DATANG/pulang ke rumah kemarin

Kelas: SMP

Materi: Past Tense
