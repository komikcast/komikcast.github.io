10. Look at the sky! A flock of birds ... (Fly) westward


Present Continuous Tense

JAWABAN:

Look at the sky! A flock of birds are flying westward.

=> Lihat ke langit! Sekawanan burung sedang terbang ke arah barat.

PEMBAHASAN:

Kalimat pada soal di atas menggunakan pola kalimat present continuous tense, karena ada kalimat "Look at the sky!" yang menunjukkan bahwa kejadian tersebut sedang terjadi (berlangsung) sehingga jawaban yang tepat adalah are flying.

Berikut penjelasan lebih lanjut tentang present continuous tense:

Present Continuous Tense  

Present continuous tense digunakan untuk membicarakan aksi yang sedang berlangsung sekarang atau rencana dimasa depan.  

Present continuous tense dibentuk dengan auxiliary verb “be”, berupa is/am/are – karena present tense (waktu sekarang), dan present participle (-ing).  Is digunakan pada subject berupa singular noun (seperti: Tita, book) dan third person singular pronoun (seperti: she, he, it); are pada plural noun (seperti: boys, men, books), plural pronoun (seperti: we, they) dan you; sementara am pada I.  

Keterangan waktu:  

Now  Right now  At the moment  At present  

Rumus:  

(+) S + am/is/are + present participle (-ing)  (-) S + am/is/are + not + present participle (-ing)  (?) am/is/are + S + present participle (-ing)?  

Contoh:  

1. (+) I’m driving a car to Bandung now.

   (-) I’m not driving a car to Bandung now

   (?) Are you driving a car to Bandung now?

2. (+) The buses are arriving in an hour.

    (-) The buses are not arriving in an hour

    (?) Are the buses arriving in an hour?

3. (+) The teacher is explaining the lesson

   (-) The teacher is not explaining the lesson

   (?) Is the teacher explaining the lesson

4. (+) Ammar is drinking a cup of coffee

    (-) Ammar is not drinking a cup of coffee

    (?) Is Ammar drinking a cup of coffee?

5. (+) My brother and my mom are watching TV  

   (-) My brother and my mom are not watching TV  

   (?) Are my brother and my mom watching TV?

6. (+) The students are studying together in the class right now

   (-) The students are not studying together in the class right now

   (?) Are the students studying together in the class right now?

7. (+) They are reading a new novel

   (-) They are not reading a new novel

   (?) Are they reading a new novel?

8. (+) She is preparing foods and drinks for the animals

    (-) She is not preparing foods and drinks for the animals

    (?) Is she preparing foods and drinks for the animals?

9. (+) They are cleaning the animal's cages

    (-) They are not cleaning the animal's cages

    (?) Are they cleaning the animal's cages?

10. (+) They are washing the animals

    (-) They are not washing the animals

    (?) Are they washing the animals?

11. (+) She is taking care of sick animals

   (-) She is not taking care of sick animals

   (?) Is she taking care of sick animals?

12. What happened to Vasco da Gama sailors on the Journey back from India?​


indonesia

Paulo da Gama meninggal di Azores dalam perjalanan pulang, tetapi ketika Vasco da Gama kembali ke Portugal pada September 1499, ia mendapatkan hadiah yang sangat besar sebagai orang yang berhasil mewujudkan rencana yang telah disusun selama 80 tahun. Ia mendapatkan gelar "Admiral Samudera Hindia", dan hak-hak feodal atas Sines dikukuhkan. Ia juga dianugerahi gelar Dom (count) oleh Manuel I.

Pelayaran da Gama membuktikan bahwa pantai Afrika yang lebih jauh (pantai Timur), Contra Costa, adalah penting bagi kepentingan Portugis. Pelabuhan-pelabuhannya menyediakan air bersih dan perbekalan, kayu dan pelabuhan untuk reparasi, dan tempat untuk menunggu sementara musim tidak menguntungkan. Selain itu, komoditas rempah-rempah terbukti juga merupakan kontribusi penting bagi ekonomi Portugal.

inggris

Paulo da Gama died in the Azores on his way home, but when Vasco da Gama returned to Portugal in September 1499, he was rewarded with a huge prize for carrying out a plan that had been drawn up for 80 years. He received the title "Admiral of the Indian Ocean", and feudal rights to Sines were confirmed. He was also awarded the title Dom (count) by Manuel I.

Da Gama's voyage proved that the more distant African coast (East coast), Contra Costa, was of great importance to Portuguese interests. Its ports provided clean water and supplies, timber and a port for repairs, and a place to wait while the season was unfavorable. In addition, the spice commodity proved to be an important contribution to the Portuguese economy.

23. 27. Which statement uses the conjuction correctly...A:) My sister wears a thick jacket on winter to protect on the cold weather. B:) My sister wears a thick jacket on winter in order to protect on the cold weather C) My sister wears a thick jacket on winter for protect on the cold weather D) My sister wears a thick jacket on winter so protect on the cold weather.​


Jawaban:

The statement that uses the conjunction correctly is:

D) My sister wears a thick jacket on winter so protect on the cold weather.

The conjunction "so" is used correctly to show the cause-and-effect relationship between wearing a thick jacket and protecting oneself from the cold weather. Option A is incorrect because "to protect on the cold weather" is not grammatically correct. Option B is incorrect because "in order to protect" is too wordy and not necessary in this context. Option C is incorrect because "for protect" is not grammatically correct.
