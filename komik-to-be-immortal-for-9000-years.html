31. Mr.catt has delivered our mail for many years in to passive


Jawaban:

Our mail has been delivered by Mr. Catt for many years

Penjelasan:

Active menjadi Passive

has menjadi has been

Jawaban:

Our mail has been delivered by Mr.catt for many years

PEMBAHASAN

Mr.catt has delivered our mail for many years

Structure Kalimatnya:Subject => Mr.cattHas + Verb-3 => has delivered Object => pur mail Complement => for many years

in to passive?

Berdasarkan keterangan di atas:Kalimat menggunakan Present Perfect karena setelah Subjek ada has+V3Kalimat adalah Active Voice atau kalimat aktif yaitu Subjek melakukan aksi dalam kalimatnya Yg ditanyakan adalah diubah ke Passive Voice atau kalimat Pasif, maka Objek dikenai aksi oleh Subjek dalam kalimatnya.

Berikut Rumus yg digunakan:

Active Voice: S + has/have + V3 + O

=> Mr.catt has delivered our mail for many years

Passive Voice: O + has/have + been + V3 + By S

=> Our mail has been delivered by Mr.catt for many years

33. I............ (be) a primary school student for 6 years​


Untuk melengkapi kalimat di atas, jawabannya adalah..

I have been a primary school student for 6 years.

Penjelasan:

Karena ada tanda waktu "for", yang merupakan time signal dari present perfect, maka untuk melengkapi nya menggunakan rumus :

S + Have/has + V3 + O

Contoh :

* She has taught Biology since 4 years ago.

* They have lived here for 2 years.

Alasan:

Karena pada kalimat di atas subject nya "I" maka menggunakan auxiliary verb "Have".Bentuk V3 dari "be" adalah "been".

40. Santosh bought a refrigerator for Rs. 9000 and was forced to to sell it for Rs. 8100. Find the lost percentage​


Jawab:

Loss = Cost Price - Selling Price

Loss = Rs. 9000 - Rs. 8100 = Rs. 900

Loss Percentage = [(900 / 9000) * 100] = (10%)

So, Santosh incurred a loss of 10%.
