Panjang :

2x + 7cm > 15 cm

2x > 15 cm - 7 cm

2x > 8 cm

x > 8 cm/2

x > 4

Keliling : 2(p + l)

2[(2x + 7) + (3x - 5)] ≤ 74 cm

2(5x + 2) ≤ 74 cm

10x + 4 ≤ 74 cm

10x ≤ 74 cm - 4 cm

10x ≤ 70 cm

x ≤ 70 cm/10

x ≤ 7

Jadi batasan nilai x adalah 4 < x ≤ 7 (B.)
